Garlic Fried Bok Choy

Ingredients

  • Â½ tbsp coconut oil or light olive oil
  • 2 garlic cloves, sliced
  • 450 g bok choy, halved or savoy cabbage, quartered
  • 1 tbsp tamari soy sauce

Instructions

  1. Heat up a large frying pan with coconut oil and garlic on medium-high heat.
  2. Place the bok choy in the pan and fry until it gets some colour
  3. Turn the leaves and drizzle the soy sauce on top.
  4. Continue frying for a couple of minutes or until the bok choy has softened a bit.
  5. Remove them from the pan and keep warm.
