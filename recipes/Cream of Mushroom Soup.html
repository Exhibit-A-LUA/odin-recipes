Cream of Mushroom Soup

Description

Cream of Mushroom Soup

Ingredients

  • 200 gms Mushrooms
  • 2 tbsp Butter
  • 1 Bay Leaf
  • 1/3 cup finely chopped onions
  • 1/2 tsp minced garlic
  • 1 tbsp whole wheat flour or all-purpose flour
  • freshly crushed black pepper or ground pepper to taste
  • 1 cup water or vegetable stock or mushroom stock
  • 1 cup whole milk at room temp
  • 3+ tbsp heavy cream or 6 tbsp light cream
  • 1 tbsp chopped parsley or coriander leaves or any fresh herbs
  • 1 to 2 pinches ground nutmeg powder or grated nutmeg

Instructions

  1. Rinse or wipe dry 200 to 250 grams (about 1 cup) of white button mushrooms or cremini mushrooms.
  2. Slice of the lower part of the base stalk of each mushroom.
  3. Then chop them in halves or quarters, or slice them thin
  4. Heat a heavy saucepan or pan over low to medium-low heat.
  5. Melt 2 tablespoons of butter (salted or unsalted) in the pan.
  6. When the butter has almost melted add bay leaf or tej patta (Indian bay leaf).
  7. Keep the heat low and be careful to not burn the butter.
  8. Sauté until the bay leaf becomes fragrant, only for about 2 to 3 seconds.
  9. Add ⅓ cup finely chopped onions (about 1 small to medium-sized onion).
  10. Add ½ teaspoon minced or finely chopped garlic (2 to 3 small to medium garlic cloves).
  11. Mix and stir.
  12. Sauté until the onions soften and become translucent.
  13. Add the sliced or chopped mushrooms to the pot.
  14. Mix again.
  15. You will see the mushrooms releasing water after a few minutes.
  16. Continue to sauté as the mushrooms brown and all the water dries up in the pan
  17. Add 1 tbsp of whole wheat flour or all-purpose flour.
  18. Sauté for 3 to 4 minutes on a low heat as you continuously stir.
  19. You should not be able to smell the raw flour after a minute or two.
  20. Then add freshly crushed black pepper or ground pepper to taste. Mix well.
  21. Now add 1 cup of water or vegetable stock, or mushroom stock. Mix well.
  22. Add 1 cup whole (full fat) milk at room temperature. Mix very well.
  23. Simmer over medium-low heat.
  24. Stir often; it will start to bubble up. 5 mins
  25. Add cream. Mix well.
  26. Add parsley, simmer 1 to 2 minutes, stir often.
  27. Sprinkle 1 to 2 pinches of nutmeg, stir
