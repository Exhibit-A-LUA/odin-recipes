Everything But The Bagel Omelet

Description

Our keto Everything but the bagel omelet has everything but the bagel! Enjoy the crunch of sesame seeds, onion, and garlic with the rich flavours of turkey, bacon, and sun-dried tomatoes, and the creamy eggs that made even more dreamy with cream cheese in the filling. Is your mouth watering yet?

Ingredients

  • Â½ tsp unsalted butter
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 tsp bagel seasoning
  • 28 (g) cooked bacon, chopped
  • 55 (g) deli turkey, chopped
  • 2 tsp sun-dried tomatoes, chopped
  • 2 tbsp cream cheese

Instructions

  1. Prepare the sun-dried tomatoes, turkey, and bacon.
  2. Divide the cream cheese into small pieces.
  3. Set aside.
  4. Melt the butter in a small skillet or omelet pan over low heat.
  5. Whisk the eggs in a small bowl with the bagel seasoning until frothy.
  6. Pour the eggs into the skillet.
  7. When the egg begins to cook add the bacon, turkey, tomatoes, and cream cheese to one side of the omelet.
  8. Fold the other side of the omelet over the filling and continue cooking to warm the filling and melt the cream cheese.
  9. Carefully flip the omelet to warm the other side if necessary.
  10. Serve with a garnish of fresh herbs such as chives, basil, or thyme if desired.
