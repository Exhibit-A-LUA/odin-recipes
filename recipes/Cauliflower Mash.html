Description

Cauliflower Mash

Ingredients

  • 450 (g) cauliflower, cut into florets
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions

  1. Place cauliflower florets in a steamer or boil them in salted water for about 10 minutes.
  2. Remove from the heat, drain, and place in a blender or a food processor with the olive oil.
  3. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  4. Process until smooth, set aside, and keep warm.
