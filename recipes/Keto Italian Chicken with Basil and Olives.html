Keto Italian Chicken with Basil and Olives

Chicken breasts marinated in a flavourful dressing together with olives, sun-dried tomatoes, and basil.

description

Ingredients

Chicken and Olives

  • 800 (g) chicken breasts
  • 2 tbsp olive oil or butter, for frying
  • 1 tsp salt
  • ½ tsp ground black pepper
  • 85 (g) (180 ml) sun-dried tomatoes in oil, chopped
  • ite120 ml (65 g) olivesm
  • 120 ml (21 g) fresh basil, chopped

Basic Salad Dressing

  • see Basic Salad Dressing Recipe

Serving

  • 950 ml (120 g) baby spinach

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a large frying pan, melt butter or olive oil over medium heat.
  3. Season the chicken with salt and pepper.
  4. Fry the chicken on all sides until browned.
  5. Place the chicken in a baking dish.
  6. Bake in the oven for 15 to 20 minutes or until the chicken is cooked through. Let rest.
  7. Cut the chicken into 1” (2.5 cm) pieces.
  8. Top with sun-dried tomatoes, olives, and basil.
  9. Pour over the dressing.
  10. Cover with foil and let rest in the refrigerator for one hour or more.
  11. Serve the chicken cold with baby spinach.
