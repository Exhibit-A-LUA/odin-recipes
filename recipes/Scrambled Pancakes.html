Scrambled Pancakes

Description

Yummy Scrambled Pancakes

Ingredients

  • 3 large eggs
  • 80 ml unsweetened almond milk
  • 4 tbsp almond flour
  • 1 tsp ground psyllium husk powder
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • Â¼ tsp salt
  • 1 tbsp butter, for frying

Instructions

  1. Add the eggs, almond milk, almond flour, psyllium husk powder, baking powder, and salt to a bowl and whisk until smooth.
  2. Let the batter rest for a couple of minutes to set.
  3. Heat a large frying pan over medium-high heat.
  4. Add the butter when hot.
  5. Pour the batter into the pan.
  6. Using a rubber spatula, stir from the edges of the pan towards the middle, until the batter is scrambled and cooked.
