Description

Spinach Dip

Ingredients

  • 28 (g) frozen spinach
  • 60 ml (45 g) Greek yoghurt (0% fat)
  • 2 tbsp mayonnaise
  • 1 tbsp dried parsley
  • Â½ tsp onion powder or garlic powder
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Thaw the frozen spinach and remove excess liquid by squeezing it over the sink.
  2. Chop finely.
  3. Add yoghurt, mayonnaise, parsley, and onion powder to a bowl and mix.
  4. Add the spinach and mix until combined.
  5. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Set aside to let the flavours develop.
