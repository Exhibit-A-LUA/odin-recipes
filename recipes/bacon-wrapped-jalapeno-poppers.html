Bacon-Wrapped Jalapeño Poppers

Description

We're bringing apps to the table, and they won't last long! These keto jalapeño poppers are devilishly delicious. We stuffed spicy jalapeño peppers with a meaty, cheesy filling and then wrapped them snuggly in bacon before we baked them to crispy perfection. We're ready for game day — how about you?

Ingredients

  • 1 tsp olive oil
  • 230 (g) ground turkey
  • 1 tsp taco seasoning
  • 12 fresh jalapeños
  • 230 (g) (240 ml) cream cheese
  • 55 (g) (120 ml) cheddar cheese, finely shredded
  • 12 (325 g) bacon strips

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. Add olive oil to a small skillet over medium heat. Add the ground turkey and taco seasoning and fry until cooked through, breaking it up as you go. Set aside to cool.
  3. Cut a slit into the jalapeños along the entire length of the pepper, and remove the seeds. Set aside.
  4. Combine the cream cheese, cheddar cheese, and cooked ground turkey in a bowl. Fill the jalapeños with the mixture.
  5. Wrap the bacon strips diagonally around each stuffed jalapeño. Place on the prepared baking tray.
  6. Bake for 18-20 minutes until the bacon is crisp. There are two jalapeño poppers per serving.

Tips

  • To reduce the jalapenos' spiciness, remove the stem and the white ribs to which the seeds are attached.
  • Wear food-safe gloves when working with chili peppers, or ensure you thoroughly wash your hands after handling them. You don't want to touch your face with any chili residue on them!
  • If you're not a fan of spicy foods, you can use pimientos de Padrón instead of jalapeños.

Air Fryer Method

  1. Follow the steps 2 to 5 in Instructions above.
  2. Preheat the air fryer to 375°F (190°C) and air fry for 10-12 minutes until crispy
