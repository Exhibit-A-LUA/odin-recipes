Low Carb Chicken Sheet Pan with Chili Sauce

Description

This simple sheet pan meal is proof that simple, fresh flavours win the day! You can easily swap for whatever vegetables you have in the fridge, such as eggplant, tomatoes, or broccoli. For a slightly more adventurous version, you can try fennel bulbs, pumpkin, or radicchio.

Ingredients

Chicken and Vegetables

  • 2 tbsp olive oil, divided
  • 3 sprigs fresh rosemary
  • 900 (g) boneless chicken thighs
  • 2 (220 g) red onions, cut into wedges
  • 1 (120 g) red bell pepper, cut into strips
  • 55 (g) zucchini, chopped
  • 475 ml (70 g) watercress or arugula lettuce or lettuce
  • salt and pepper, to taste
  • 1 lemon, cut into wedges

Chilli Sauce

  • see Chilli Sauce Recipe

Instructions

part1

  1. Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Lightly oil a sheet pan or baking tray with half of the oil and scatter over the rosemary.
  3. Season the chicken thighs on both sides and place them on the rosemary, stretching them out flat so that they cook quickly.
  4. Scatter the onions, peppers, and zucchini around the chicken, season, and drizzle with the remaining oil.
  5. Cook for 20–25 minutes until the chicken thighs are cooked through.
  6. There should be no pink juices when you pierce the thickest part of the thigh, or the internal temperature should be higher than 165°F (74°C).
  7. Divide the cooked chicken and vegetables between the plates.
  8. Add watercress or arugula and a lemon wedge to each plate and serve with the sauce.

Chilli Sauce

  • see Chilli Sauce Recipe

Spice it up!

  • Taste one of the red chillies from the middle of the chilli where the pith joins the seeds to see how hot they are and decide whether you want more spice.
  • You can also add a pinch of chilli flakes.
