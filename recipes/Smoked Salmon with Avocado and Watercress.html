Smoked Salmon with Avocado and Watercress

Description

This is an ideal healthy keto breakfast. Few carbs, healthy fats, and lots of potassium and fibre Moreover, smoked salmon contains quite a bit of salt, which can be beneficial on a low carb or ketogenic diet. Make sure to drink plenty of fluids!

Ingredients

  • 475 ml (70 g) watercress, thick stems removed or baby spinach
  • 1 (200 g) avocado, sliced
  • 300 g smoked salmon or deli turkey
  • 2 tbsp chopped scallions
  • 1 tbsp finely chopped, fresh dill
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp finely chopped, fresh chives (optional)

Instructions

  1. Arrange the watercress, avocado slices, and salmon on plates.
  2. Garnish with scallions, dill, and chives.
  3. To serve, drizzle with olive oil, mostly over the watercress.

Tips

  • You can swap the smoked salmon for cooked salmon, canned salmon, or another fish of your choice.
  • If you don't like fish, try cooked chicken instead.
