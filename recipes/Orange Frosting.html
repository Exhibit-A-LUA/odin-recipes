Orange Frosting

Description

Orange infused buttercream frosting.

Ingredients

  • 280 (g) (290 ml) cream cheese room temperature
  • 55 (g) butter, room temperature
  • 60 ml (55 g) erythritol
  • 1 orange, zest
  • 2 tbsp fresh cranberries or loganberries (optional)

Instructions

  1. Use a hand mixer to blend the cream cheese and butter until smooth and creamy.
  2. Whip in the sweetener and orange zest.
  3. Let rest in the fridge for 1 hour.
  4. Use a piping bag to top the cupcakes with frosting.
  5. Garnish with cranberries, orange, or loganberries if desired.
