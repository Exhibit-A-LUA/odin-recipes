Cinnamon Roll Dough

Description

Cinnamon Roll Dough

Ingredients

  • 350 ml (170 g) mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • 2 tbsp cream cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 240 ml (110 g) almond flour
  • 60 ml (32 g) powdered erythritol
  • 2 tbsp coconut flour
  • 1 tsp white wine vinegar
  • ½ tsp baking soda

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 365°F (185°C).
  2. Heat mozzarella and cream cheese in a non-stick pan on medium heat or in a bowl in the microwave oven.
  3. Stir until they melt together.
  4. Add the other ingredients and mix well.
  5. Tip: use a hand mixer with dough hooks.
  6. Roll out the dough between two pieces of parchment paper with a rolling pin to a square shape about 12 x 10" (30 cm x 26 cm) that's about 0.1 " (3 mm) thick.
  7. Remove the parchment paper that's on top.
  8. Spread any fillings on top
  9. Roll up the dough tightly into a log using parchment paper (the same technique as when you make sushi rolls).
  10. Put the log in the fridge for about 20 minutes to firm up so the rolls will keep their shape when cutting.
  11. Cut the log into 12 pieces with a sharp knife.
  12. Put them in a baking dish (that is big enough just to fit all the buns) lined with parchment paper or into individual cupcake liners.
  13. Bake in the oven for about 10-12 minutes or until golden.
  14. Let them cool.
  15. Add any topping

Recommended special equipment

  • Hand mixer with dough hooks.
  • Rolling pin.
