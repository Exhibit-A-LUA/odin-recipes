Tomato and Garlic Sauce

Description

Tomato and Garlic Sauce

Ingredients

  • 2 garlic cloves, finely chopped
  • 120 ml tomato paste
  • 240 ml water
  • 2 tbsp finely chopped, sun-dried tomatoes in oil
  • salt and ground black pepper, to taste

Instructions

  1. Add the garlic to the pan, stirring frequently until lightly browned.
  2. Then add the tomato sauce ingredients and whisk them together.
  3. Bring to a boil, and then reduce the temperature to medium-low.
  4. Simmer uncovered for 5 minutes, stirring frequently.
  5. Add the meatballs, stir to coat, and simmer uncovered for 2-3 more minutes, or until the sauce has thickened.
