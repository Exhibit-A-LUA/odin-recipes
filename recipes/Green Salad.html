Green Salad

Description

A simple green salad with an easy vinaigrette is the perfect healthy week-night side dish to pair with your choice of protein.

Ingredients

  • 475 ml (110 g) leafy greens or chopped lettuce
  • 1 (110 g) tomato, diced
  • 55 g cucumber, diced
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Â½ tbsp balsamic vinegar or red wine vinegar
  • 1 tsp Italian seasoning (optional)
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Prepare the vegetables and add them to a bowl.
  2. Drizzle with olive oil, balsamic vinegar, and optional seasoning.
  3. Toss together and serve.
