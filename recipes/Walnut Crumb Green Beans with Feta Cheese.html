Walnut Crumb Green Beans with Feta Cheese

Description

evel up your average green bean dish with tangy feta cheese and walnut crumbs. A beautiful side dish for a special occasion or for your low carb holiday feast.

Ingredients

  • 200 g fresh green beans
  • 1 tbsp avocado oil or olive oil
  • 1 garlic clove, finely chopped
  • coarse salt, to taste
  • pepper, to taste
  • 2 tbsp crushed walnuts, or another nut of choice
  • 45 g (70 ml) feta cheese or goat cheese

Instructions

  1. Bring a saucepan of water to a boil.
  2. Add the beans to the boiling water and cook for 1-3 minutes or until preferred done-ness.
  3. Strain and plunge them into ice-cold water to stop them from overcooking and keep them al dente and bright green.
  4. Drain and pat dry with a paper towel.
  5. Add the oil, garlic, to the saucepan over medium-high heat.
  6. Add the beans to the oil; season to taste and toss to coat.
  7. Remove from the heat.
  8. Divide between serving plates or platter and serve topped with nuts and feta.

Tips

  • You can blanch the beans in boiling water, steam, or pan-fry.
  • Using an ice bath stalls the cooking process and keeps the beans bright in colour and al dente.
  • They are just as delicious cold as they are warm.
