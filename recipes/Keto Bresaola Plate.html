Keto Bresaola Plate

Description

Here's a classic Italian plate, featuring the salted, air-dried beef "bresaola", with the addition of protein-rich boiled eggs to make it the ultimate easy keto plate. Enjoy the broad range of flavours that work effortlessly together to give you this delectable, no-cook plate. Great for a fast lunch or light dinner.

Ingredients

  • 55 (g) bresaola, thinly sliced
  • 28 (g) Parmesan cheese, thinly sliced
  • 240 ml (20 g) arugula lettuce
  • 1 boiled large egg, cut in half
  • 1 tbsp fresh lemon juice
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Layout the bresaola on the plate.
  2. Top with arugula, parmesan cheese, and egg.
  3. Drizzle with olive oil, lemon juice, and add salt and pepper to taste.

Tips

  • Feel free to substitute any cold cut or cured meat for the bresaola.
