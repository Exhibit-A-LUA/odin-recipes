Description

Chocolate Cups

Ingredients

  • 160 (g) (290 ml) sugar-free dark chocolate
  • 45 (g) (50 ml) cocoa butter

Instructions

  1. Place chocolate and cocoa butter in a microwave-safe container.
  2. Heat in 30-second bursts, stirring after each burst of heat, until completely melted.
  3. Coat the inside edges of a 12-hole silicone mini muffin tray with melted chocolate.
  4. Refrigerate.
