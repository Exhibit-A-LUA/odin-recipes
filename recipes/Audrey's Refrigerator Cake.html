Audrey's Refrigerator Cake

Description

Audrey's Refrigerator Cake

Ingredients

For Cake

  • 1 cup (or more) Tia Maria (choice of Tia Maria or Kahlua or Both)
  • 24 Sponge Fingers
  • Cherries (for decoration)
  • 2 x 227 (g) (2 x 8oz) Blocks Dark Chocolate
  • 113 (g) (4 oz) Unsalted Butter
  • Desert spoon of Kahlua (optional)

Marzipan

  • see Marzipan Recipe

Butter Icing

  • see Butter Icing Recipe

Instructions

Marzipan

  • see Marzipan Recipe

Butter Icing

  • see Butter Icing Recipe

For Melted Chocolate

  1. Boil water in Pan
  2. Place ingredients in basin in water
  3. Gently mix while melting

For Cake

  1. Line Cake Tin with foil, longways and sideways.
  2. So you have enough to wrap over the cake before freezing.
  3. Roll out the marzipan.
  4. Line bread tin with marzipan on base and short side.
  5. Leave long sides without marzipan (they will be covered later)
  6. Soak Sponge Fingers in either Tia Maria or Kahlua (optionally dilute with water)
  7. Lay Sponge Fingers on Marzipan
  8. Pour melted chocolate over fingers - spread out flat with spatula
  9. Repeat layers till top of cake tin
  10. Add a layer of marzipan to finish
  11. Lift up extra foil sides to cover cake
  12. Wrap whole tin with foil
  13. Put in freezer for 24 hours (or until solid)
  14. Let cake come to room temp (to prevent butter icing from freezing)
  15. Cover cake with butter icing (make patterns with a fork)
  16. Decorate with cherries

Stuff Needed

  • Tin Foil
  • Oblong (Rectangular) Cake or Bread Tin (approx 8" x 4")
  • Roller (to roll marzipan flat)
  • Spatula (to smooth out chocolate layers)
