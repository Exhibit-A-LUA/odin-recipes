Chicken Liver Paté

Ingredients

  • 1 (110 g) red onion
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 230 (g) butter
  • 2 tbsp brandy or port wine or other type of liquor that you like (optional)
  • 1 tbsp tomato paste
  • it450 (g) chicken liversem

Instructions

  1. Chop onion and garlic finely and fry soft in a couple of tablespoons of butter on medium high heat.
  2. Remove from the pan.
  3. Increase the heat and add a couple of more tablespoons butter to the same pan.
  4. Fry the liver so that it gets fried on all sides.
  5. Add liquor, salt and pepper.
  6. Lower the heat and reduce the juices.
  7. Let cool for a couple of minutes.
  8. Place the fried onion, garlic and liver in a food processor or a blender and mix into a smooth batter together with the rest of the butter and tomato paste.
  9. Spread out in a small baking dish, about 7 x 8 inches (18 x 20 cm).

Tips

  • You can make the paté in advance and keep in the freezer.
  • It will keep for 3-4 days in the refrigerator.
