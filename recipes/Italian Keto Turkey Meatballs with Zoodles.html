Italian Keto Turkey Meatballs with Zoodles

Description

It's hard to beat these moist, home-made turkey meatballs married with a rustic tomato sauce of roasted garlic and sun-dried tomatoes. Served on top of "al dente" zucchini pasta, it's healthy comfort food at its best.
