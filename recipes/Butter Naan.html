Butter Naan

Description

Butter Naan

Ingredients

  • ½ cup lukewarm milk
  • 2 tsp active dry yeast
  • 2 ¼ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • ¼ cup plain yogurt
  • Salt, to taste
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 3 tbsp unsalted butter, melted

Instructions

  1. Activate the yeast by soaking it in lukewarm milk for 10-15 minutes.
  2. In a mixing bowl, add the flour, salt, sugar, activated yeast mixture, yogurt, and oil.
  3. Knead the dough until the surface becomes smooth.
  4. If needed, add more milk.
  5. Cover the dough with a cloth and bring it to rest in a warm place for about 1 hour, or until the dough doubles in size.
  6. Divide the dough into 6 equal parts.
  7. Roll each part into 8” circles, then lightly brush both sides with melted butter.
  8. Heat a large skillet over high heat.
  9. Place the rolled dough into the skillet.
  10. When small bubbles appear on the top, flip the naan over and cook the other side.
  11. When done, flip it again and brush with some more butter.
  12. Repeat steps with the remaining dough.
  13. Serve immediately while warm.
