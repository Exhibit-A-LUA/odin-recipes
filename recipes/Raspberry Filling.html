Description

Raspberry Filling

Ingredients

  • 180 ml heavy whipping cream
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 tbsp powdered erythritol
  • 120 ml (60 g) fresh raspberries, or frozen

Instructions

  1. Using a beater, whip the cream until firm and add in the vanilla and sweetener.
  2. Scatter the raspberries, breaking them up with your fingers if they are large.
