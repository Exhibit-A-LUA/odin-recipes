Steak with Keto Mushroom Sauce

Description

Elevate your steak with this decadent keto mushroom sauce. With just a few extra minutes, this sauce will take your juicy rib eye from great to excellent. Garlic-infused mushrooms serve as the stars, adding flavours that you never knew your steak was missing.

Ingredients

Steak

  • 900 (g) rib-eye steaks, 1 steak per serving
  • 1 tbsp ghee or butter
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste

Mushroom sauce

  • 1 tbsp butter
  • 2 garlic cloves, crushed
  • 120 ml (35 g) mushrooms, sliced
  • 80 ml (75 g) cream cheese
  • 120 ml heavy whipping cream
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste

Salad

  • 475 ml (110 g) leafy greens
  • 1 (110 g) tomato, diced

Instructions

Steak

  1. Take your steaks out of the fridge and bring them to room temperature.
  2. Pat the steaks dry with a paper towel and season generously with salt and pepper.
  3. Heat a large frying pan over medium-high heat depending on how you like your steak cooked.
  4. Add the butter (or preferably the ghee as it had a higher smoke point).
  5. Place the steaks in the frying pan and fry to your liking: rare, medium, or well done.
  6. Remove the meat from the pan, cover, and let set aside to rest while you prepare the sauce.

Mushroom sauce

  1. Lower the heat and add more butter (if necessary) to the same frying pan.
  2. Fry the garlic and mushroom slices until golden.
  3. Season with salt and pepper.
  4. Turn the heat down and add the cream cheese, stirring to avoid any lumps.
  5. Add the cream and stir until heated through.
  6. Taste and season with more salt and pepper if necessary.

Serving

  1. Serve the steaks with the creamy mushroom sauce and salad.

Tips

  • Instead of using butter or ghee you could also cut off some fat from the steaks and use that to cook your meat.
