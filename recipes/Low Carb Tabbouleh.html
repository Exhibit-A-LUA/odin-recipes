Low Carb Tabbouleh

Description

Middle Eastern Parsley Salad - An easy low carb side dish with a Mediterranean flair. Our version of tabbouleh with crispy cauliflower rice instead of crushed bulgur is super fresh and nutritious.

Ingredients

  • 110 g (1.6 litres) fresh parsley
  • 28 g (75 ml) fresh mint
  • 55 g (130 ml) scallions
  • 2 (230 g) tomatoes
  • 120 ml raw cauliflower rice
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Â½ lemon, juice
  • salt and ground black pepper

Instructions

  1. Finely chop the parsley, mint, and scallions.
  2. Dice the tomatoes.
  3. Shred the cauliflower if need.
  4. Add all the ingredients to a bowl with lemon juice and olive oil.
  5. Mix and serve.
