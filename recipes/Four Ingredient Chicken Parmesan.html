Four Ingredient Chicken Parmesan

Description

There are many chicken parmesan recipes, but none is simpler than this four-ingredient chicken parmesan. Golden brown chicken breasts are slathered in sugar-free marinara and topped with a gooey layer of cheese.

Ingredients

Chicken Parmesan

  • 450 (g) chicken breasts
  • salt and ground black pepper
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 120 ml unsweetened marinara sauce
  • 55 (g) (120 ml) mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • 45 (g) (140 ml) shredded Parmesan cheese

Serving

  • 475 ml (110 g) leafy greens
  • 8 (140 g) cherry tomatoes, quartered

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to broil, 430째F (220째C).
  2. Season chicken breasts with salt and pepper.
  3. Heat the oil in a frying pan on medium heat.
  4. Once the oil is hot, add the chicken and cook for about 3-5 minutes on each side until cooked through.
  5. You can use a meat thermometer to make sure it's 165째F (73째C).
  6. Remove the chicken and let it rest.
  7. In an oven-proof dish, add half the marinara sauce and place the chicken breasts on top of it.
  8. Pour in any remaining juices from the chicken.
  9. Top the chicken with the remaining marinara sauce, mozzarella, and finally the parmesan.
  10. Broil in the oven for about 5 minutes or until the cheese is golden.
  11. Serve the chicken parmesan with leafy greens and tomatoes.

Tips

  • If you follow a vegetarian diet, eggplant would be a great substitute for chicken.
