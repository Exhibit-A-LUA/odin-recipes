Chicken Skewers with Low Carb Tabbouleh and Hummus

Description

An easy low Carb meal with a Middle Eastern flair. Chicken skewers are marinated in a tangy lemon sauce and served with a delicious low Carb hummus made with edamame beans instead of chickpeas. A nutritious low Carb tabbouleh rounds out the meal with loads of fresh herbs and tomatoes.
