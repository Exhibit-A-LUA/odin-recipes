Lettuce Cups with Deli Turkey, Tomatoes and Mayo

Description

Everyone needs a go-to meal for moments when cooking feels like a chore. These satisfying cups require only slicing and assembly – the perfect option. We made sure to fill fresh romaine lettuce leaves with simple ingredients, but there's so much room for creativity!

Ingredients

The Turkey

  • 3 (85 g) Romaine lettuce leaves
  • 2 tbsp mayonnaise
  • 160 g deli turkey, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 6 (100 g) cherry tomatoes, halved
  • ½ (20 g) a stick of celery stalk, finely chopped
  • 1 tbsp fresh parsley, roughly chopped
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice (optional)
  • salt and pepper

Mayonnaise

  • see Mayonnaise Recipe

Instructions

  1. Put the lettuce leaves in a wide bowl.
  2. Spread the mayo evenly on lettuce leaves.
  3. Evenly divide the deli turkey, tomatoes, celery, and parsley between them.
  4. Finish with lemon juice, salt, and pepper.

Tips

  • Feel free to replace turkey with ham or bacon.
  • Parsley can be replaced by fresh basil or coriander.
