Healthy Tandoori Fish

Description

Healthy Tandoori Fish

Ingredients

  • 300 (g) Salmon, Herring or Mackerel
  • 8 cloves garlic
  • 1 piece ginger
  • 1 tbsp grated lime zest
  • ½ tsp cumin powder
  • Salt as required
  • 3 tbsp gram flour (besan)
  • 2 handful coriander leaves
  • 2 tbsp lime juice
  • 1 tbsp red chilli powder
  • ½ tbsp garam masala powder
  • 1 cup vegetable oil
  • 120 (g) curd

Instructions

  1. Wash and clean the fish.
  2. Next, make a fine paste with half of the vegetable oil, garlic, coriander, ginger, red chilli powder, cumin powder, garam masala, lime juice and zest and salt.
  3. Heat the oil in a pan and add the gram flour, stir to make a paste and cook for about a minute till fragrant and lightly coloured.
  4. Add the mixture to the spice paste and combine with the yoghurt.
  5. Mix well all the ingredients for the marinade.
  6. Gently rub the marinade over the fish pieces and marinate for an hour.
  7. Preheat the oven to 200° Celsius and spread out the marinated fish in a baking dish.
  8. Bake the tandoori fish for 10 – 15 minutes on the top rack, turning the fish pieces once.
  9. Baste the fish pieces once or twice during cooking with butter.
  10. Serve hot with chutney or sauce.
