Miso Dressing

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp miso paste
  • 1 tbsp water
  • 2 tbsp mayonnaise
  • 1 tbsp lime juice or lemon juice
  • 1 tsp sriracha sauce
  • Â½ tsp sesame oil

Mayonnaise

  • see Mayonnaise Recipe

Instructions

  1. Microwave the miso paste with the water for a few seconds and give it a good stir to loosen it up.
  2. Add the rest of the dressing ingredients and mix until well combined.
  3. Pour it into a piping bag or squeeze bottle if you have one.
  4. Set aside.
