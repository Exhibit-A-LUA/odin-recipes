Cream Cheese Cake Frosting

Description

Cream Cheese Cake Frosting

Ingredients

  • 55 (g) butter, room temperature
  • 300 (g) (300 ml) cream cheese, room temperature
  • 3 tbsp powdered erythritol
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Mix all the ingredients together until smooth.
  2. If it's still lumpy you can use an immersion blender.
