Chicken Stew With Vegetables

Description

The perfect balance between western and eastern flavours An exceptionally delicious stew with chicken legs, home-made broth, and fresh vegetables.

Ingredients

  • 55 (g) butter
  • 1.1 kg chicken legs
  • 21 (g) (55 ml) fresh ginger, finely chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, sliced
  • 1 tbsp salt
  • ½ tsp ground black pepper
  • 3 tbsp tamari soy sauce
  • 260 (g) (900 ml) mushrooms
  • 200 (g) Brussels sprouts, halved
  • 300 (g) (800 ml) broccoli, cut into small florets

Instructions

  1. Melt the butter in a large, non-stick pan, over medium-high heat.
  2. Add the chicken and fry for 15 minutes, or until browned on all sides.
  3. Transfer the chicken to a large pot, and pour water over the pieces, until just submerged.
  4. Bring to a boil over high heat, and then reduce temperature to medium-low.
  5. Cover, and simmer for 45 minutes.
  6. Stir in the ginger, garlic, salt, pepper, tamari soy sauce, and mushrooms.
  7. Cover, and cook for 15 minutes.
  8. Add the Brussels sprouts (see tip) and broccoli, and stir together.
  9. Cover, and cook for 5 more minutes.
  10. For serving, ladle the chicken, vegetables, and delicious broth into deep dishes.

Tips

  • I like my brussels sprouts al dente, in which case 5 minutes of cooking time should be enough.
  • If you prefer softer brussels sprouts, cook them a little longer.
