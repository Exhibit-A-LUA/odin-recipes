Steak Fajitas Sheet Pan

Description

No need for expensive cuts of beef with this tenderizing marinade. It takes just 30 minutes to infuse your sliced steak with tantalizing flavours

Ingredients

  • 650 (g) sirloin steaks, sliced into strips
  • 3 tbsp olive oil, divided
  • 2 tsp paprika powder
  • 2 tsp ground cumin
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • ½ tsp salt
  • ½ tsp ground black pepper
  • 1 (120 g) red bell pepper, sliced
  • ½ (55 g) yellow onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 red chilli pepper, sliced
  • 1 (200 g) avocado, sliced
  • 80 ml sour cream
  • 1 lime, sliced

Instructions

  1. In a medium mixing bowl, combine the sliced steak with half the olive oil and all of the paprika, cumin, garlic powder, salt, and black pepper until the steak is well coated.
  2. Cover and marinate in the fridge for at least 30 minutes.
  3. Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C).
  4. Line a baking sheet with a piece of parchment paper.
  5. Place the red bell pepper, sliced onion, and chilli on the baking sheet and drizzle with the remaining olive oil.
  6. Remove the steak from the fridge and place it on top of the vegetables, leaving a little room around each strip.
  7. Roast for 10 minutes or until beef has reached an internal temperature of 160°F (71°C).
  8. Serve with sliced avocado, sour cream, and lime.

Tips

  • This steak fajita mix will keep in the fridge for up to 5 days in airtight containers.
  • Make sure to serve with fresh avocado, as avocado doesn’t store well once it has been cut and been exposed to oxygen.
  • This meal goes well with a side of sour cream!
