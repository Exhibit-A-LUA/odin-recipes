Cod in Green Sauce with Asparagus

Description

A tasty and simple dish covered in a creamy green sauce made with fresh herbs. Try this healthy, low carb, high protein dish, to help you achieve your weight loss goals.

Ingredients

Cod and Asparagus

  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 260 g green asparagus
  • 350 g cod or hake fish, boneless fillets

Green Avocado Sauce

  • see Green Avocado Sauce Recipe

Instructions

Cod and Asparagus

  1. Wash the asparagus and cut away the tough part of the stalk.
  2. In a frying pan that can fit the fish and vegetables, heat a tablespoon of olive oil.
  3. Fry the asparagus over medium heat for 6-7 minutes.
  4. Season with salt and pepper.
  5. Keeping the asparagus in the pan, add the fish and fry it for 2-3 minutes on each side.
  6. Add one more tablespoon of olive oil if necessary for frying.
  7. Season to taste with salt and pepper.
  8. Stir the asparagus from time to time.
  9. Serve the fish and asparagus on top of the sauce.
