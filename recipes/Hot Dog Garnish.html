Description

Hot Dog Garnish

Ingredients

  • 230 g (1.2 litres) Romaine lettuce (1 large leaf for each hot-dog)
  • 55 g (120 ml) cheddar cheese, shredded
  • 110 g cooked bacon (bits)
  • 4 tbsp red onions, chopped
  • 2 pickled jalapeños, sliced

Instructions

  1. Wrap each dog in a romaine lettuce leaf.
  2. Top with guacamole, cheese, bacon, red onion, and pickled jalapeños.
