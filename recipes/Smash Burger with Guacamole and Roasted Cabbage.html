Smash Burger with Guacamole and Roasted Cabbage

Description

The best kind of burger is a smashed burger, and this one is no exception. By flattening the meatball directly in the pan you ensure maximum crust and maximum flavour â€” two qualities we love in a burger. Pair it with roasted cabbage and an herby guac and you're looking at a satisfying dinner!

Ingredients

Smash burger

  • 450 (g) ground beef, preferably chuck
  • salt or ground black pepper

Roasted cabbage

  • see Roasted Cabbage Recipe

Guacamole

  • see Guacamole Recipe

Instructions

Smash burger

  1. Divide the ground beef into two balls per serving, about 4 oz. (110 grams) each.
  2. Heat a large frying pan over high heat.
  3. When the pan is smoking hot, place the meatballs in the pan and press them down with a grill press, spatula, or the bottom of a glass, until less than 1/2-inch (1 cm) thick.
  4. Season generously with salt and pepper.
  5. Fry for about 2 minutes on each side or until the burgers have a nice brown crust.
