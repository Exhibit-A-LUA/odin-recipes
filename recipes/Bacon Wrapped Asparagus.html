Bacon Wrapped Asparagus

Description

Bacon Wrapped Asparagus

Ingredients

  • ½ tbsp olive oil
  • ½ tbsp balsamic vinegar
  • 170 (g) green asparagus, trimmed
  • 4 (110 g) bacon strips
  • ground black pepper

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. Whisk together olive oil and balsamic vinegar in a small bowl.
  3. Arrange the asparagus into bundles of 3 or 4 stems per bundle.
  4. Wrap one strip of bacon around each bundle.
  5. Overlap the ends of the bacon slightly to secure.
  6. When done, place them on the baking sheet, seam-side down.
  7. Brush the asparagus bundles with the balsamic mixture and season with black pepper.
  8. Bake for about 10-15 minutes or until the bacon is crispy and the asparagus is tender.
