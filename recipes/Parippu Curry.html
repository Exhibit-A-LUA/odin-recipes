Parippu Curry

Description

Parippu Curry - Serves 8

Ingredients

  • ½ cup Yellow Moong Dal
  • ½ cup coconut grated
  • ¼ - ½ tsp jeera
  • 1 green chilli
  • 2 cloves Garlic
  • ¼ tsp turmeric
  • 10 curry leaves
  • ½ tsp Ghee

Instructions

  1. Roast the dal till light brown.
  2. Let it cool, then wash
  3. Pressure cook 2 whistles with 1 cup water, turmeric, salt
  4. Mixi into paste, the coconut, green chilli, garlic, jeera, water
  5. Add coconut mix to dal
  6. Keep watery, add salt, curry leaves, boil on medium flame for 2 minutes
  7. Add ghee, turn off heat
