Chimichurri Sauce

Ingredients

  • 1 lemon, the juice and the zest
  • Â½ (70 g) yellow bell pepper or green bell pepper
  • 1 green chilli pepper
  • 180 ml olive oil
  • 120 ml (8 g) chopped fresh parsley
  • 2 pressed garlic cloves
  • salt and pepper

Instructions

  1. Wash the lemon and grate the zest.
  2. Be sure to only get the yellow part of the peel, not the white layer underneath.
  3. Squeeze the lemon juice in a bowl and add the zest.
  4. Split the bell pepper and chilli and remove the seeds.
  5. Finely chop both and add to the bowl.
  6. Add all other ingredients.
  7. Mix with an immersion blender until the sauce reaches the desired consistency.
