Roast Beef with Keto No-Tato Salad

Description

You will not believe how good this 2-minute no-tato salad is. Instead of potatoes, we use zucchini, which gives it the right consistency minus all the carbs. Serve with roast beef, smoked salmon, or rotisserie chicken.

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp sour cream
  • 1 tbsp mayonnaise
  • 1 tsp Dijon mustard
  • 2 tsp fresh chives or dried chives (save some for garnish)
  • 140 (g) zucchini, diced
  • 45 (g) (110 ml) celery stalks, diced
  • salt and ground black pepper
  • 170 (g) deli roast beef

Instructions

  1. Combine all the ingredients except for the roast beef in a bowl.
  2. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  3. Serve the no-tato salad with the roast beef and sprinkle with chives to garnish.

Tips

  • If you have extra ingredients, like zucchini or celery, chop them up and freeze them for future recipes, such as soups, stews, or omelets.
