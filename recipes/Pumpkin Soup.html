Pumpkin Soup

Description

Pumpkin Soup

Ingredients

  • 250 g Pumpkin
  • 100g Onions
  • 5 to 6 Pods Garlic
  • 1 Stock Cube
  • 0.5 Cup Cream
  • Salt and Pepper

Instructions

  1. Boil the Pumpkin, Onions, and Garlic with Maggi Stock cube
  2. Cool it and blend it in a mixi
  3. Put it back on stove, add water for preffered consistency
  4. Add fresh pepper and salt if needed (stock cube has some)
  5. Add half a cup of cream
  6. Boil slightly
