Description

Apple Pie Spice

Ingredients

  • 4 tbsp ground cinnamon
  • 1 ½ tsp ground nutmeg
  • ½ tsp ground allspice
  • 1 tsp ground ginger
  • 1 ½ tsp ground cardamom

Instructions

  1. Combine spices in a small bowl, mix well to combine.
  2. Store in a small jar or spice container.

Tips

  • These measurements don't need to be precise.
  • If you prefer more of one spice or less of another, feel free to adjust to your liking.
  • If using freshly grated nutmeg, you'll want to decrease the amount a bit as it tends to be be quite a bit stronger than jarred, ground nutmeg.
