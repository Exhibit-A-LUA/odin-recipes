Quick Salsa

Description

We may be cutting a few corners in preparation time, but we're not sacrificing any flavour in the process! This Quick salsa is easy to make and so much tastier than any store-bought version we've ever come across. Play with the spice level and take it from mild to hot as you dare! This salsa pairs beautifully with eggs, meat, fish, and veggies.

Ingredients

  • 2 (230 g) tomatoes, roughly chopped
  • ½ (55 g) red onion, roughly chopped
  • 120 ml (8 g) fresh cilantro
  • 1 fresh jalapeño, seeds and stem removed
  • 2 tsp lime juice
  • 1 tsp sea salt, or more to taste

Instructions

  1. Place the roughly chopped tomatoes, red onion, cilantro, and jalapeño in a food processor and pulse until evenly chopped.
  2. It does not need to be smooth; aim for a pea-sized chop.
  3. Transfer to a bowl, stir in the lime juice and sea salt.
  4. Taste, and adjust seasoning if necessary.

Tips

  • If you don't have a food processor you can finely chop all the ingredients by hand.
  • Serve alongside any Mexican dish.
  • This is particularly delicious with Keto huevos rancheros with cheese tortillas.
  • Not a fan of cilantro?, Not a fan of cilantro? Leave them out.
  • This salsa keeps well for up to three days in the fridge.
