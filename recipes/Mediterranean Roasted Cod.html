Mediterranean Roasted Cod

Description

If you like the sound of a keto dinner that's easy like Sunday morning, this is it! Pop some cherry tomatoes and garlic in the oven to roast, set the table, maybe dance around the kitchen, and then place the fish and all the seasonings on top for the final stretch in the oven. So simple to make, tons of flavour, and delightfully high in protein and satiety. It's the kind of meal that makes a week-night feel like a date night!

Ingredients

  • 450 g cod, boneless fillets
  • 110 g cherry tomatoes, preferably on the vine
  • 1 garlic clove, sliced
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • salt and ground black pepper
  • 1 tbsp small capers
  • 1 tsp fresh thyme leaves
  • ½ tsp chilli flakes
  • 1 tsp freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • ½ tsp lemon zest

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Place the cherry tomatoes in an oven-safe dish.
  3. Top with the sliced garlic and drizzle with half the amount of olive oil.
  4. Place the dish in the oven, then immediately reduce the temperature to 350°F (180°C).
  5. Roast for 15 minutes.
  6. After 15 minutes, remove the dish from the oven and place the fish on top of the tomatoes.
  7. Season everything with salt and ground black pepper.
  8. Drizzle the remaining olive oil over the fish.
  9. Scatter the capers, thyme leaves, and chilli flakes over the fish.
  10. Roast for a further 12-15 minutes until the fish is just cooked through.
  11. Remove the dish from the oven and divide the fish, tomatoes, capers, and herbs between the plates.
  12. Stir the lemon juice into the remaining juices in the oven dish and drizzle it over the fish.
  13. Finish by sprinkling freshly grated lemon zest over the plates.

Tips

  • Take the fish out of the fridge 15-20 minutes before cooking.
  • This will take the chill off and allow it to cook more evenly.
