Churro Bites

Description

ou won't believe that cheese is one of the main ingredients in these gluten-free treats — we had to try more than one to believe it! They taste like the real thing, but they're delightfully keto!

Ingredients

  • 170 g (350 ml) mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • 2 tbsp cream cheese
  • 240 ml (110 g) almond flour
  • 3 tbsp whey protein isolate (unflavoured)
  • 60 ml (55 g) erythritol
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 tsp white wine vinegar
  • ½ tsp baking soda
  • 475 ml oil for frying, or as much as you need to cover the bites in oil

Instructions

  1. Melt the mozzarella and cream cheese in a non-stick pan over medium heat or in a bowl in the microwave.
  2. Stir until thoroughly combined.
  3. Add the almond flour, whey protein isolate, erythritol, egg, white wine vinegar, and baking soda.
  4. Form a smooth dough by kneading it with your hands or using an electric mixer with dough hooks.
  5. Divide the dough into balls with your fingers or a small cookie scoop; they should be about 1-1/2 inches (4 cm) in diameter.
  6. Heat the oil in a deep pan to 325°F (160°C).
  7. Fry the churros in batches for 1-1/2 to 2 minutes until golden, rolling them around gently as they fry.
  8. Remove them with a slotted spoon and place them on a plate lined with paper towels to absorb the excess oil.

Tips

  • There is one churro bite per serving.
  • These are best served on the same day they're made.
  • You can bake them instead. Grease a mini muffin tin and fill each muffin hole with a churro bite. Bake in the oven at 400°F (200°C) until golden, for about 20 minutes. Roll the bites in the cinnamon-erythritol mixture while warm.
