Ingredients

  • 300 ml sour cream
  • 2 tbsp powdered erythritol
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 120 ml heavy whipping cream (optional)
  • 5 fresh strawberries, halved (optional)

Instructions

  1. In a medium-sized bowl, whisk together the sour cream, sweetener, and vanilla.
