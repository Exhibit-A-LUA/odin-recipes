Strawberry Frozen Yoghurt

Description

This healthy, instant frozen yogurt is the perfect sugar-free dessert. This ice cream takes you less than 5 minutes to prepare since it doesn't require freezing. You could also use other frozen low Carb berries such as blueberries or raspberries. Or why not a mix?

Ingredients

  • 260 (g) frozen strawberries
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 4 tbsp erythritol (optional)
  • 240 ml (180 g) Greek yogurt (4% fat)

Instructions

  1. Add the frozen strawberries, vanilla, and sweetener (if using) to a blender or food processor.
  2. Blend for a couple of seconds.
  3. Add the yoghurt and with a spatula, mix with the berries until combined.
  4. If you use the blender to mix in the yoghurt, the consistency will be similar to a smoothie.

Tips

  • If you would like to increase the protein even more, use fat free yogurt and add a scoop or two of whey protein powder.
