Loaded Keto Turkey Dogs with Guacamole

Description

These high protein turkey dogs are loaded with fresh, California-style flavours like avocado, spicy jalapeños, and bacon bits. All wrapped in nature's easiest hot-dog bun – a romaine lettuce leaf. This is the way we love our dogs, but don't be afraid to make it your own.

Ingredients

Hot dogs

  • 8 (800 g) turkey hot dogs, 2 per person
  • 1 tsp butter or olive oil (optional)

Easy Guacamole

  • see Easy Guacamole Recipe

Hot Dog Garnish

  • see Hot Dog Garnish Recipe

Instructions

  1. Grill the hot-dogs or cook them on the stovetop in a frying pan.
  2. Until the skin has grill marks or a nice brown colour.
  3. If you choose the stovetop method, it's best to use a little butter or oil.
  4. Meanwhile, combine all guacamole ingredients in a bowl. Set aside.
  5. Wrap each dog in a romaine lettuce leaf.
  6. Top with guacamole, cheese, bacon, red onion, and pickled jalapeños.
