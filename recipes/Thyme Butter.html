Description

Thyme Butter

Ingredients

  • 110 (g) butter
  • 1 tbsp dried thyme
  • 1 tsp ground black pepper

Instructions

  1. Add butter to a small pan and melt carefully on medium heat.
  2. Let the white milk protein sink to the bottom and carefully pour the cleared butter into a small bowl.
  3. Stir in thyme and pepper.
