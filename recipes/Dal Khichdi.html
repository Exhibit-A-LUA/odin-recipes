Dal Khichdi

Description

Dal Khichdi

Ingredients

  • ½ cup Mung Dal
  • ½ cup rice
  • ¼ cup finely chopped onions
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1 pinch Hing (Asafoetida)
  • 1 tsp jeera (cumin seeds)
  • 1 Diced green chilli
  • 1 small onion – chopped
  • ½ cup or 1 medium tomato
  • 1 tsp crushed/grated ginger
  • 1½ cup vegetables finely diced (carrot, peas, beans, etc).
  • 1 tsp Ghee/butter/cooking oil (as per availability/preference)

Instructions

  1. Put the rice and mung dal in separate pots and soak.
  2. Cut vegetables as they soak and heat the pressure cooker.
  3. Add ghee, butter or oil once it has absorbed some heat.
  4. Keep a low flame, add the jeera and bay leaf.
  5. Sauté till you get the aroma and add a pinch of hing.
  6. Add the ginger and saute it so that it doesn’t burn, after a minute add the onions and the green chilli.
  7. Once the onions get a golden brown colour, add the tomato, turmeric and salt.
  8. If you like, you can also add a pinch or two of chilli powder.
  9. Sauté till everything becomes mushy and add the vegetables you have chosen.
  10. You can go ahead without any vegetables too.
  11. At this point, the dal should be ready, drain it out and add it into the pressure cooker.
  12. Sauté for a few minutes till the aroma comes out.
  13. Next, drain the water from the rice and add it into the cooker, followed by 4 cups of water.
  14. If you want a soft mushy texture instead of a porridge-like texture, add 3 cups.
  15. Taste the water as you cook, it should be a little salty.
  16. Add more salt if needed.
  17. Cover the pressure cooker and wait until 4 whistles.
  18. If you would like a more whole consistency, then cook for 2 whistles.
  19. After that, turn the flame off and let it sit until the pressure releases on its own.
  20. Your khichdi is ready to eat.
  21. Garnish with ghee if you like and serve.
