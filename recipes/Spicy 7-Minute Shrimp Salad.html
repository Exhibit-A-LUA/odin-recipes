Spicy 7-Minute Shrimp Salad

Description

A restaurant-quality salad in minutes. Cooked shrimp mixed with a blend of creamy yogurt and mayo, spiced up with chili, onions, and celery for crunch. This high protein, low carb salad is perfect for a packed lunch, but also fancy enough for guests.

Ingredients

  • 400 g cooked shrimp chopped
  • 120 ml (50 g) chopped celery stalks
  • 2 tbsp chopped white onions
  • 60 ml mayonnaise or vegan mayonnaise
  • 80 ml (60 g) plain Greek yogurt (4% fat)
  • 1 tsp lemon juice
  • 2 tsp sambal oelek or sugar-free chilli garlic sauce
  • 700 ml (170 g) leafy greens
  • salt to taste

Mayonnaise

  • see Mayonnaise Recipe

Instructions

  1. Place the chopped shrimp in a medium-sized bowl.
  2. Add the chopped celery and onions.
  3. Measure in the mayonnaise, Greek yogurt, lemon juice, and sambal oelek.
  4. Stir to combine.
  5. Just before serving, place the leafy greens on plates.
  6. Divide the shrimp salad and place it on top of the greens.

Tips

  • The shrimp salad can be made the day before serving.
  • Refrigerate any leftovers for up to two days.
