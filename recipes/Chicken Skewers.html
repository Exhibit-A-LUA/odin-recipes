Chicken Skewers

Description

Chicken skewers marinated in a tangy lemon sauce.

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 lemon, zest and juice
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tsp paprika powder
  • 2 lbs chicken breasts, cubed
  • salt and ground black pepper

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 400 °F (200 °C) or turn on the grill.
  2. In a bowl, combine the olive oil, lemon juice, lemon zest, and paprika.
  3. Then, add the cubed chicken and give it a good mix.

Tips

  • Split tomatoes in half and grill them at the same time as the chicken. The juices from the tomatoes pair perfectly with the rest of the dish.
