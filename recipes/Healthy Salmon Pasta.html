Description

Healthy Salmon Pasta

Ingredients

  • 350g penne
  • 2 salmon steaks, about 175g/6oz each
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 tbsp pine nut
  • 1 red pepper, deseeded and chopped
  • 300g mushroom, sliced
  • handful basil leaves

Instructions

  1. Cook the pasta following pack instructions.
  2. Add the salmon to the water 6 mins before the end of the cooking time.
  3. Heat the oil in a pan, then cook the pine nuts until toasted.
  4. Add the pepper and fry until softened.
  5. Tip in the mushrooms and stir until they start to soften.
  6. Add a ladleful of the pasta water.
  7. When the pasta is cooked, remove the salmon to a plate, then drain the pasta.
  8. Fork the salmon into flakes and add to the vegetables along with the pasta and the basil leaves.
  9. Season with pepper and lightly toss together before serving.
