Roasted Brussels Sprouts

Description

Roasted Brussels Sprouts

Ingredients

  • 450 (g) Brussels sprouts, trimmed and halved
  • ½ (55 g) red onion, wedges
  • 60 ml pecans
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • it1 tsp dried rosemaryem
  • ½ tsp garlic powder
  • salt and ground black pepper

Instructions

  1. Pre-heat the oven to 375°F (185°C).
  2. Spread the Brussels sprouts, red onion, and pecan nuts on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  3. Sprinkle with seasoning and drizzle with olive oil and toss until evenly coated.
  4. Bake in the oven for about 20 minutes or until the Brussels sprouts are tender and a bit golden.
