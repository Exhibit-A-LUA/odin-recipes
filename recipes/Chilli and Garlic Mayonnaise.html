Description

Chilli and Garlic Mayonnaise

Ingredients

  • 120 ml mayonnaise
  • 1 tbsp chilli paste
  • 1 garlic clove, minced

Instructions

  1. Mix the mayonnaise, chilli paste, and garlic together in a small bowl.
