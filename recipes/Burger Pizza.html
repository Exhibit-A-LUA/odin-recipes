Burger Pizza

Description

Is it a burger? Is it a pizza? It's both! A flat burger patty acts as the crust, and the delicious toppings are a mix of pizza fixings and a classic cheeseburger. With over 50 grams of protein per serving, this healthy and hearty low Carb meal will keep you satisfied with no hankering for fries.

Ingredients

Burger Crust

  • 400 (g) ground beef or any ground meat of choice
  • 1 egg
  • ¾ tsp salt
  • ¼ tsp ground black pepper

Topping

  • 3 tbsp ketchup (no sugar added) or tomato sauce
  • 85 (g) (180 ml) cheddar cheese, sliced
  • 1 (110 g) tomato, sliced
  • 28 (g) (180 ml) lettuce, shredded
  • ¼ (28 g) yellow onion, sliced
  • 2 tsp roasted sesame seeds (optional)

Dressing

  • 3 tbsp sour cream
  • ½ tbsp ketchup (no sugar added) or tomato sauce
  • ½ tbsp yellow mustard
  • 1 (140 g) pickle, finely diced

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. In a bowl, mix the ground meat with egg, salt, and pepper until thoroughly combined.
  3. The meat will release some meat drippings in the oven so if you have a wire rack baking sheet, use that.
  4. If not line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  5. Divide the meat mixture into one piece per serving.
  6. Roll into balls and flatten out the meat crust mixture into thin flat circles (about 7" or 18 cm in diameter) using wet hands or with a spatula.
  7. Place on the baking sheet.
  8. Bake for 10 minutes.
  9. While the crust is in the oven, cut the vegetables and mix the ingredients for the dressing in a small bowl.
  10. Remove the meat crust from the oven and spread the ketchup or tomato sauce evenly over the crust.
  11. Cut or tear the cheese into smaller pieces and distribute it on top of the crust.
  12. Bake for another 2 minutes or until the cheese has melted.
  13. Remove from the oven.
  14. Place the burger crust on plates.
  15. Top with tomatoes, onion, and lettuce.
  16. Drizzle the dressing on top and serve.
