Tuna Pie with Potato Topping

Description

Tuna Pie with Potato Topping

Ingredients

  • 6 medium size potatoes, peeled and roughly chopped
  • 1/4 cup (60ml) milk
  • 70 g unsalted butter, chopped
  • salt and pepper
  • 1 leek, thinly sliced
  • 1 large carrot, chopped
  • 2 sticks celery, chopped
  • 300 ml thickened cream
  • 1 tbsp plain flour
  • 2 x 185g cans tuna in oil, drained and roughly flaked
  • 310 g can corn kernels, drained
  • 1/2 cup (60g) frozen peas
  • 4 tbsp grated Tasty cheese
  • 2 tbsp finely chopped flat-leaf parsley

Instructions

  1. Steam or boil the potatoes until tender.
  2. Mash the potatoes along with the milk, 50g butter, salt and pepper, then set aside, cover and keep warm.
  3. Meanwhile, melt the remaining 20g butter in a large non-stick frying pan over medium heat.
  4. Add the leek, carrot and celery and cook for 3-4 minutes or until softened.
  5. Add the flour and cook, stirring constantly, for another 1 minute.
  6. Stir in the cream.
  7. Bring to a simmer, reduce the heat and cook for 5 minutes or until thickened.
  8. Add the tuna, corn, peas, 2 tbsp cheese, parsley, salt and pepper and cook for another 5 minutes.
  9. Preheat grill on high.
  10. Transfer tuna mixture to a 6-cup capacity ovenproof dish.
  11. Top the tuna mixture with the mashed potato and sprinkle with the remaining 2 tbsp cheese.
  12. Place pie under preheated grill for 2 minutes or until golden brown on top.
  13. Serve with a simple salad.
