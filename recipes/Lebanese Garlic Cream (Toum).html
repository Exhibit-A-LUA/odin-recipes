Lebanese Garlic Cream (Toum)

Description

If you love garlic as much as we do, this may quite possibly be your new favourite keto side dish. This irresistible Lebanese version of aioli is armoured with a heavenly fluffy and smooth texture with a fresh touch of lemon.

Ingredients

  • 2 garlic cloves, crushed
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 2 egg whites
  • 240 ml light olive oil
  • ½ tsp sumac (optional)

Instructions

  1. Add crushed garlic, lemon juice, salt and egg whites to a tall and narrow mixing bowl or beaker.
  2. Use an immersion blender to mix thoroughly until white and fluffy — it takes about 1 minute.
  3. Pour in the oil in a thin stream while you keep blending.
  4. The garlic cream is ready when it's thick, white and fluffy.
  5. Top with some sumac or chilli powder.
