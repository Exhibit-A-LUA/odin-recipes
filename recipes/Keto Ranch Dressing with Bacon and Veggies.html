Keto Ranch Dressing with Bacon and Veggies

Description

Our keto ranch dressing starts with a large batch of dry seasoning that can be saved and used for other recipes. Next, we add the wet ingredients to make a keto dressing that is perfect for dipping. Best served with crunchy vegetables and of course – crispy bacon!

Ingredients

Keto Ranch Dressing

  • see Keto Ranch Dressing Recipe

Snacks

  • 450 g bacon
  • 140 g (325 ml) celery stalks, about 4 stalks, cut into 2" (3 cm) sticks
  • 140 g (220 ml) sliced, red bell peppers
  • 140 g (220 ml) sliced, yellow bell peppers
  • 200 g cherry tomatoes

Instructions

Keto Ranch Dressing

  • see Keto Ranch Dressing Recipe

Snacks

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Set aside a large baking dish, lined with parchment paper, for easy clean-up
  3. Arrange the bacon strips in a single layer without overlapping, in the baking dish.
  4. Bake for 18 - 25 minutes, or until done to your preference.
  5. Using tongs, transfer the bacon to a paper-towel-lined plate, to absorb excess grease.
  6. For serving, place the bacon and vegetables on a large serving platter or cutting board, and start dippin'!

Tips

  • For a thicker dressing, use less heavy cream.
  • For a thinner texture, stir in some water.
