Sea Bass Butterflied

Description

Sea Bass Butterflied

Ingredients

  • 1 (500 g) whole sea bass or white fish fillet
  • salt and ground black pepper
  • 2 garlic cloves, sliced
  • 1 tbsp olive oil

Instructions

  1. Butterfly the sea bass (or ask your fishmonger to do it for you).
  2. Remove the insides, the scales, and the fins.
  3. Leave the tail and the head-on.
  4. Cut it along the backbone and all the way through the head.
  5. Do not cut it all the way through the belly; this is where it should remain connected, at least partially.
  6. Butterflying the sea bass will help it cook faster and will crisp up the meat inside.
  7. Season the fish with salt and pepper on the inside and outside.
  8. Open the fish and place it in a baking dish skin side down.
  9. Distribute the garlic on top and drizzle with olive oil.
  10. Place in the oven and bake for about 20 minutes depending on the size of the fish.
