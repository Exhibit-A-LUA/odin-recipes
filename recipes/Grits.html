Description

Grits

Ingredients

  • 400 g cauliflower
  • 350 ml chicken stock
  • 28 g butter
  • ½ tsp salt
  • ¼ tsp ground black pepper
  • 55 g (120 ml) cheddar cheese, shredded
  • 3 tbsp heavy whipping cream

Instructions

  1. Using a food processor or a hand-held grater, rice the cauliflower.
  2. Aim for a fine, couscous-like consistency.
  3. In a large saucepan, bring the chicken stock to a boil.
  4. Add the cauliflower, cover with a lid, and cook over medium heat for about 15 minutes.
  5. Remove the lid, add the butter, salt, and pepper.
  6. Cook for 10 more minutes, until the broth has reduced and the cauliflower is tender.
  7. Add the cheese and cream and stir to combine, keep stirring until the cauliflower grits are at your desired consistency.
  8. Keep warm until serving.
