Pie Pastry

Ingredients

  • 240 ml (110 g) almond flour, ground
  • 120 ml (50 g) coconut flour
  • 70 (g) butter, softened and cut into small cubes
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 tbsp lemon zest
  • 2 tsp erythritol
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 425°F (200°C).
  2. Line an 8.5" (22 cm) tart pan with parchment paper that's about 1.5" (4 cm) larger than the pan.
  3. Mix the pastry ingredients together in a medium-sized bowl with a spoon, or add to a food processor, and pulse until just combined.
  4. Don’t over-mix as the pastry will be too soft.
  5. Using your hands, press the pastry into the tart pan, covering the bottom and sides, about 0.5" (1.5 cm) thick.
  6. Use a fork to prick a few holes in the pastry base, to prevent it from rising.
  7. Bake on the middle rack for 8–10 minutes, or until firm to the touch and golden brown.
  8. Remove the pastry from the oven and use the parchment to lift it carefully onto a cooling rack.
  9. After a couple of minutes, carefully slide the parchment out from the bottom so the pastry can cool more quickly.
