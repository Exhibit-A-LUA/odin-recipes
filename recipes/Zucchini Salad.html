Description

Zucchini Salad

Ingredients

  • 280 g zucchini
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 3 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 red chilli pepper, or a pinch of chilli flakes
  • 1 garlic clove, grated
  • salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions

  1. Slice the zucchini into ribbons, using a vegetable peeler.
  2. Place into a medium-sized bowl with the remaining zucchini salad ingredients.
  3. Stir to combine, and set aside.
