Ragi Dosa

Description

Ragi Dosa

Ingredients

  • 1 cup ragi flour or sprouted ragi flour – 150 grams
  • ¼ cup besan (gram flour) or rice flour
  • ⅓ to ½ cup finely chopped onions
  • ½ tsp finely chopped ginger or ½ inch ginger
  • ½ tsp chopped green chillies or 1 green chilli or serrano pepper, chopped
  • 8 to 10 curry leaves chopped
  • 3 tbsp chopped coriander leaves (cilantro)
  • ½ tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 pinch asafoetida optional
  • 3 tbsp fresh grated coconut or desiccated coconut
  • ½ cup buttermilk or ¼ cup curd (yoghurt) mixed in ¼ cup water
  • 1.5 cups water or add as required
  • Salt as required
  • 2 tsp oil or ghee

Instructions

  1. Take ragi flour and besan (gram flour) in a mixing bowl or pan.
  2. You can even use rice flour or chickpea flour instead of besan.
  3. Add finely chopped onions, finely chopped ginger, chopped curry leaves, chopped green chillies, chopped coriander leaves, asafoetida, cumin seeds, grated coconut and salt as per taste.
  4. Add buttermilk and 1.5 cups of water.
  5. To make the buttermilk, stir briskly ¼ cup curd with ¼ cup water.
  6. Mix very well to a thin batter.
  7. Cover and let the batter rest for 15 to 20 minutes.
  8. Heat a hot tawa and sprinkle it with some water.
  9. Give the batter a good mix and pour it on the pan with a ladle.
  10. Cook it till crisp.
