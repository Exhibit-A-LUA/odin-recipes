Cabbage Slaw

Description

Cabbage Slaw

Ingredients

  • 240 ml shredded green cabbage
  • 1 tsp white vinegar 5%
  • lime juice, from half of a lime
  • salt, to taste

Instructions

  1. Combine the cabbage, vinegar, and lime juice.
  2. Season with salt to taste.
