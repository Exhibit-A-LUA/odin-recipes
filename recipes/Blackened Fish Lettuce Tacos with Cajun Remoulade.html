Blackened Fish Lettuce Tacos with Cajun Remoulade

Description

The perfect warm weather crowd pleaser. Blackened keto fish tacos wrapped in cool lettuce leaves and served with a spicy pico de gallo and zippy Cajun remoulade.

Ingredients

Cabbage Slaw (112g)

  • see Cabbage Slaw Recipe

Pico de Gallo Salad (201.50g)

  • see Pico de Gallo Salad Recipe

Cajun Remoulade (134.17g)

  • see Cajun Remoulade Recipe

The Fish

  • 900 g white fish fillets cut into thin strips
  • 1 tbsp cajun seasoning
  • salt and pepper, to taste

For Serving

  • 110 g iceberg lettuce, 2 large leaves per person

Instructions

Cabbage Slaw (112g)

  • see Cabbage Slaw Recipe

Pico de Gallo Salad (201.50g)

  • see Pico de Gallo Salad Recipe

Cajun Remoulade (134.17g)

  • see Cajun Remoulade Recipe

The Fish

  1. Season the fish with cajun seasoning, salt, and pepper on all sides.
  2. Sear fish in a hot pan in a little oil, until cooked - about four minutes.
  3. Keep turning so each side gets seared.

For Serving

  1. Add two strips of fish to each lettuce shell.
  2. Top with the slaw, pico de gallo and remoulade.
  3. Garnish with the leftover cilantro and jalapeño.

Tips

  • For a spicier version, try using habanero peppers instead of jalapeños.
  • Our favourite fish for this recipe is Mahi Mahi, but if you can't find that, tilapia, cod, halibut or any other white fish will work well.
