Description

Spanish Tuna Pie Filling

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 (110 g) yellow onion, diced
  • 1 (140 g) green bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 (120 g) red bell pepper, chopped
  • 300 g tomato sauce
  • 260 g tuna in olive oil, drained
  • salt or ground black pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Boil water in a medium-sized pot.
  2. Lower the eggs carefully in the water with a slotted spoon.
  3. Let them cook for 10 minutes.
  4. Once they are cooked, remove them from the water and place them in a bowl of cold water to peel them more easily.
  5. Peel and chop them.
  6. In a frying pan, heat olive oil.
  7. Add the bell peppers and onions and stir-fry with a pinch of salt.
  8. Stir-Fry for 15 minutes until the onion is translucent and the peppers are very soft.
  9. Add the tuna and the tomato sauce and stir well.
  10. Add salt and pepper to taste.
  11. Cook on low for 5 more minutes.
  12. Remove from the heat.
  13. Add the chopped hard-boiled eggs and stir again to integrate them into the mixture.
