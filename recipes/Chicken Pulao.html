Chicken Pulao

Ingredients

For the Chicken

  • 227 (g) chicken thighs
  • 227 (g) chicken breasts
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1⁄2-inch piece ginger, minced
  • 1 tbsp red chilli powder
  • 2 tsp garam masala
  • 1⁄4 cup yoghurt
  • Salt, to taste (I use 1/4 teaspoon)

For the Pulao

  • 4 tbsp ghee
  • 1 1⁄2 large onion, diced or sliced
  • 3 green chilli, sliced (optional)
  • 3 cups basmati rice, rinsed well
  • 5 cups vegetable broth, or water
  • fresh cilantro, chopped
  • fresh mint, chopped
  • 5 cardamom pods
  • 1 1⁄2 tsp cumin
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 7 cloves
  • 2 star anise
  • Salt, to taste (I use 1 1⁄2 teaspoon)

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, add garlic, ginger, red chilli powder, garam masala, salt, and yogurt.
  2. Mix until well-combined.
  3. Add chicken and mix until it is coated in the marinade.
  4. Allow the mixture to marinate for at least 30 minutes for best results.
  5. Heat a large pot on medium-high heat.
  6. Once hot, add ghee.
  7. Then add onion and green chillies Sauté until the onions are soft and translucent.
  8. Add the spices – cardamom, cumin, bay leaves, cinnamon, cloves, and star anise.
  9. Sauté until fragrant.
  10. Add in the marinated chicken and fry until cooked through, about 4-8 minutes depending on the size of your chicken.
  11. Mix in lemon juice, cilantro, and mint, then stir to combine.
  12. Pour in the vegetable broth and bring to a boil.
  13. Then add salt and the rinsed basmati rice.
  14. Reduce the heat to a simmer, then cover and cook until rice has absorbed all the broth, about 16 to 18 minutes.
  15. Fluff up the rice, then top with some more ghee, yogurt, and/or chopped cilantro (optional).

Tips

  • Make sure your rice is thoroughly rinsed (I like to rinse mine 3-4 times).
  • If the rice is not rinsed well, you may end up with mushy rice.
  • Do not omit the yoghurt in the marinade.
  • The yoghurt keeps the chicken tender and juicy.
  • If you’re dairy-free, you can substitute with nut milk yogurt (like this one) or coconut yogurt.
