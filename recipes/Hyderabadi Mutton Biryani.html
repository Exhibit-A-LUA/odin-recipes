Hyderabadi Mutton Biryani

Ingredients

Marination

  • 500 (g) Mutton
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tsp Shahi jeera
  • i4-5 no Cardamomtem
  • 1 small piece Cinnamon
  • 1 1/2 tsp Turmeric
  • 1 tbsp Chilli powder
  • Â½ tsp Mace (javitri) powder
  • 1 tsp Cardamom powder
  • 1 tbsp Garlic paste
  • 1 tbsp Ginger paste
  • 2 Green chillis
  • 3/4 cup Browned onion
  • 1 cup Mint leaves
  • 1 1/2 cup Curd
  • 1/4 cup Oil

Rice

  • 3 lts Water
  • 3 tbsp Salt
  • 3-4 nos Cardamom
  • 1 no Green chilli
  • 2 cups Basmati rice (soaked)
  • 1 cup Water from Blanched Rice
  • a very small pinch Saffron
  • 2 tbsp Desi ghee
  • Dough for sealing

Instructions

Meat

  1. Mix all the ingredients under marination and place them in a vessel.
  2. Cook the meat covered till it gets tender.
  3. iteTry and cook the meat on medium heat covered so that no water is added, however, if the meat starts to dry up then add some water or stock as required to keep the curry of the meat runny.m

Rice

  1. In a separate deep vessel bring water to boil and add salt, cardamom, chilli and let it boil for 5 mins more.
  2. Skim the chilli and spices.
  3. Add soaked rice and cook till rice is half cooked.
  4. Strain the rice at this stage and add them over the meat.
  5. Spread the rice and add a ladle of the hot water strained from the rice.
  6. Drizzle dissolved saffron on top.
  7. At this stage, you can add 2 tbsp of ghee or can add 3-4 tbsp leftover oil after browning onions.

Placing On Dum

  1. Seal the vessel with dough and a tight-fitting lid.
  2. Cook on high heat for a minute, now lower the heat and cook on dum for 12-15min.
  3. Remove from heat and let it sit for 10 mins before opening the lid.
  4. Serve hot with raita.
