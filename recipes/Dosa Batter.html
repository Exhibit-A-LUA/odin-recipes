Description

Dosa Batter

Ingredients

  • 1 Cup Urad Dal
  • 3 Cup Idli Rice
  • 1 tsp Methi Seeds
  • 1 tbsp Poha
  • 1 tbsp Chana Daal
  • 1 tbsp Puffed Rice
  • 1 tbsp Sabudana
  • Salt optional

Instructions

  1. Soak all ingredients for 4 hours
  2. Grind and sit overnight
