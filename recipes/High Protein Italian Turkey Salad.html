High Protein Italian Turkey Salad

Description

Quick, easy, and healthy. This tasty and nutrient-dense salad comes together in 6 minutes, tops! The pesto and sun-dried tomatoes go perfectly together with the turkey and crisp greens.

Ingredients

  • 120 ml (110 g) cottage cheese
  • 1 tbsp green pesto
  • 1 tbsp finely chopped sun-dried tomatoes in oil
  • salt or ground black pepper to taste
  • 240 ml (55 g) leafy greens
  • 45 (g) cucumber, diced
  • 110 (g) deli turkey or smoked deli ham

Instructions

  1. Mix the cottage cheese, pesto, and sun-dried tomatoes in a bowl.
  2. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  3. Add the leafy greens, cucumber, deli turkey, and the cottage cheese mix to a plate or bowl and serve.
