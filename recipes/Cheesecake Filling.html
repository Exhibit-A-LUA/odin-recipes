Description

Cheesecake Filling

Ingredients

  • 650 (g) (650 ml) cream cheese, room temperature
  • 120 ml (110 g) erythritol
  • 80 ml sour cream
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 tsp lemon juice
  • 3 large eggs, room temperature

Instructions

  1. Whip the cream cheese in the bowl of a stand mixer, or in a mixing bowl with a hand mixer until fluffy.
  2. Add the sweetener and beat until dissolved.
  3. Beat in the sour cream, vanilla extract, and lemon juice until smooth.
  4. Add the eggs, one at a time, and mix just until thoroughly incorporated, using a rubber spatula to scrape down the sides as needed.
  5. Do not over-mix.
