Lentil and Tuna Salad

Description

Lentil and Tuna Salad

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp sherry vinegar
  • 1 tsp Dijon mustard
  • 2 garlic cloves, finely grated
  • 50ml olive oil
  • 2 x 250g pouches ready-cooked puy lentils
  • 2 x 160g cans tuna steaks in spring water, drained and flaked
  • 160g cherry tomatoes, halved (about 10)
  • 2 ready-roasted peppers, chopped
  • handful of parsley, finely chopped
  • Â½ small bunch of chives, finely chopped, plus extra to garnish

Instructions

  1. Whisk the vinegar, mustard and garlic together in a small bowl.
  2. Slowly drizzle in the oil, whisking as you go, until emulsified, then season to taste.
  3. Add the lentils, tuna, tomatoes, peppers and herbs to a large bowl and toss together.
  4. Pour over the dressing and toss again.
  5. Divide between four bowls and garnish with the remaining chives.
