Portuguese Octopus Salad

Description

This salad is a typical dish from the Iberian peninsula where it can be found in any tavern or bar. We made it Portuguese-style by adding cilantro. This dish is the perfect simple and refreshing low-carb appetizer that is ideal to share with friends on a warm summer night.

Ingredients

  • 200 g cooked octopus
  • ½ (7.5 g) scallion, chopped
  • ½ (70 g) green bell pepper, chopped
  • ½ (60 g) red bell pepper, chopped
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp white wine vinegar
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste
  • 120 ml (8 g) fresh cilantro, roughly chopped

Instructions

  1. Cut the octopus in about 0.5 inch (1 cm) slices and prep the vegetables.
  2. Place all the ingredients in a salad bowl or a tray and add the oil, vinegar, salt, and pepper.
  3. Mix the ingredients.
  4. Chop the cilantro and sprinkle it over the salad.
  5. Cool in the fridge for at least 15 minutes before serving.

Tips

  • This salad can be kept for a couple of days in the fridge in an airtight container as long as it has not been seasoned with the dressing.
