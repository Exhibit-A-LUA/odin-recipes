Quick Keto Fish Stew with Tapenade

Description

Crunchy green beans and fresh fish come together to make this perfect all-season stew. The tapenade adds a nice depth to the sauce, perfectly complementing the white fish. Packed with healthy fats, this 10-minute meal is sure to keep you satiated.

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp butter
  • 1 garlic clove, minced
  • 650 g white fish fillets
  • 280 g fresh green beans, trimmed
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste
  • 240 ml heavy whipping cream
  • 2 tbsp olive tapenade or green pesto
  • 2 tsp white vinegar 5% (optional)

Instructions

  1. Heat a frying pan with butter over medium-high heat.
  2. Add the garlic when hot.
  3. Cut the fish fillets into 1 inch-sized (2.5 cm) cubes and chop the green beans into small pieces.
  4. Add the fish and green beans to the frying pan and season with salt and pepper.
  5. Cook for about 2 minutes.
  6. Add the cream, tapenade, and vinegar.
  7. Let it simmer until the sauce has thickened a bit and the fish is cooked.
  8. Serve warm.
