Sesame Crusted Tuna Steaks

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp sesame seeds (preferably a mix of white and black)
  • 1 tbsp almond flour
  • salt and pepper
  • 450 g fresh tuna fillets steaks (1 per serving)
  • Â½ tbsp coconut oil or light olive oil

Instructions

  1. Add the sesame seeds, almond flour, salt, and pepper to a plate and mix until combined.
  2. Press the tuna steaks down into the mixture on both sides until covered.
  3. Add coconut oil to a hot pan and fry the tuna steaks until preferred done-ness.
  4. We recommend only frying it for a minute on each side so it will still be juicy and rare inside.
  5. Serve the tuna steaks with the bok choy and the wasabi mayo.
  6. Garnish with optional scallions, chilli, and lime wedges for a vibrant presentation and a boost of flavour
