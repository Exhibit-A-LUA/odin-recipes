Caesar Salad Dressing

Ingredients

  • 120 ml mayonnaise
  • 2 tbsp water
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 garlic clove, minced
  • 2 anchovies (optional)
  • 120 ml (40 g) shredded Parmesan cheese, save some for topping
  • Â½ tbsp Dijon mustard
  • salt and ground black pepper

Instructions

  1. Add all the ingredients for the dressing to a blender or a mixing bowl if using an immersion blender.
  2. Mix until smooth.
  3. Taste and adjust flavours to your liking.
  4. Set aside.
