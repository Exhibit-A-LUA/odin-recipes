Keto Beef and Broccoli Rice Bowl with Miso Dressing

Description

This simple keto Asian beef bowl has cabbage, bell pepper, spring onions, and a delicious miso dressing. The best part is that you can make it your own with whatever you have leftover in the fridge.

Ingredients

Miso dressing

  • see Miso Dressing Recipe

Fried beef and vegetables

  • 450 (g) beef, tenderloin or flank steak
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • 2 tbsp tamari soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp rice vinegar
  • 70 (g) (180 ml) broccoli, riced
  • 70 (g) (240 ml) red cabbage, chopped
  • salt and pepper, to taste

Fresh vegetables

  • 80 (g) (130 ml) yellow bell peppers, diced
  • 2 tbsp scallions, finely sliced
  • 1 tsp sesame seeds (optional)

Instructions

Miso dressing

  • see Miso Dressing Recipe

Fried beef and vegetables

  1. In a large bowl mix the sesame oil, soy sauce, and vinegar.
  2. Combine with the beef cubes or steaks.
  3. Set aside.
  4. While the meat is marinating, grate the broccoli using a food processor, or finely chop with a knife.
  5. Put the riced broccoli in a bowl and season with salt.
  6. Microwave for two minutes.
  7. Alternatively, you can cook it in a pan with some butter/ghee till tender.
  8. If you want the broccoli rice to be hot when serving, cook it right before you assemble the bowl.
  9. In a large hot pan, sear the beef (save the marinade for later), season with salt and pepper, and cook it to your liking.
  10. Remove the meat from the pan and let it rest.
  11. Add the cabbage to the same pan and add the remaining marinade from the beef.
  12. Cook until the cabbage is tender.

Assembling the bowl

  1. Slice the beef into strips or bite-sized pieces.
  2. Distribute the broccoli rice, beef, cabbage, and yellow peppers in serving bowls.
  3. Finish the bowls with scallions, a drizzle of the miso dressing, and sprinkle some sesame seeds on top.
