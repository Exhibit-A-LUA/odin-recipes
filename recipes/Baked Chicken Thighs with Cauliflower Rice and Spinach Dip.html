Baked Chicken Thighs with Cauliflower Rice and Spinach Dip

Description

Chicken thighs provide a great source of protein and have a higher fat content than chicken breasts, making them an excellent choice for a keto meal. Pair them with this tasty cauliflower rice and creamy spinach dip, and we think you'll be a happy camper, indeed!
