Tempering

Ingredients

  • 3 tsp oil
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1 dried red chilli
  • few curry leaves
  • pinch hing

Instructions

  1. Heat 3 tsp oil - use ghee for better flavour.
  2. Splutter the cumin, red chilli, curry leaves and hing.
