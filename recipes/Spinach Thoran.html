Description

Spinach Thoran

Ingredients

  • 1 Bunch (100 gm) Spinach, chopped finely (green or red variety)
  • 1 tbsp Olive oil
  • 1 Pinch Mustard seeds
  • 1 tbsp Curry leaves
  • 1 Large Dried red chilli torn into half
  • 1 Small Onion, chopped finely
  • 5 Clove (25 gm) Garlic, crushed
  • 2 Small Green chilli, chopped finely
  • 1/2 tsp Turmeric powder
  • 3 tbsp Grated coconut
  • Salt To Taste

Instructions

  1. Heat up oil in a pan and splutter mustard seeds, followed by curry leaves and dried chillies
  2. Add chopped onions and saute until translucent.
  3. Add garlic and green chillies and saute until onions turn slightly brown.
  4. Add turmeric powder and the chopped spinach; cook until the spinach is wilted.
  5. Add salt at this stage.
  6. Now add the grated coconut and cook for another couple of minutes for the flavours to infuse.
