Cottage Cheese Sauce with Chives

Description

Meet your new favourite sauce. And dip. And dressing. This garlicky, creamy cottage cheese sauce could easily become your new favourite side to everything. Perfect for grilled food, vegetables and salads.

Ingredients

  • 120 ml mayonnaise
  • 110 (g) (120 ml) cottage cheese
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tbsp fresh chives or dried chives
  • ¼ lemon, the zest (optional)
  • salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions

  1. Using a mixer or an immersion blender, mix all ingredients until smooth.
  2. Then let the dip rest for a couple of minutes before serving so that the flavours can develop.

Tips

  • Vary the sauce with your favourite seasoning – pink pepper, curry, paprika or cumin?
  • Or maybe by using flavoured cottage cheese.
  • The sauce also works excellently as a dip.
  • You can swap the mayo for sour cream or full-fat Greek yoghurt.
  • It lasts for 4-5 days in the refrigerator and can be served as a condiment to grilled food, vegetables and salads.
