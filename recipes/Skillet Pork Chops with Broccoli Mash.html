Skillet Pork Chops with Broccoli Mash

Description

Broccoli mash is the answer for mashed potato lovers looking for a low Carb alternative. It makes a tasty green side dish, and it's packed with vitamins and nutrients too! Served with succulent pork chops cooked in a skillet, this high protein dinner will be something to write home about.

Ingredients

Skillet Pork chops

  • 2 (550 g) pork chops, 260 g each
  • 1 tbsp butter or olive oil
  • 2 tsp dried thyme or barbecue seasoning or Italian seasoning (optional)
  • salt and ground black pepper

Broccoli mash

  • see Broccoli mash Recipe

Instructions

Skillet pork chops

  1. Remove pork chops from the fridge at least 10 minutes before cooking to take the chill off the meat.
  2. Room temperature meat cooks more evenly.
  3. Pat the chops dry with a paper towel.
  4. Season generously on both sides with salt, pepper, and any other spices(Barbecue or Italian seasoning, for example).
  5. Heat a large frying pan over medium-high heat.
  6. When hot, add the butter.
  7. Fry the pork chops for about 4 minutes per side or until a nice crust forms and the inside temperature reaches 135°F (57°C).
  8. Rest the pork chops for a couple of minutes before serving.

Broccoli mash

  • see Broccoli mash Recipe

Tips

  • Feel free to substitute olive oil for some of the butter in any proportion you choose.
