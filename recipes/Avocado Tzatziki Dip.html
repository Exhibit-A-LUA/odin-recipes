Avocado Tzatziki Dip

Description

Avocado Tzatziki Dip

Ingredients

  • 2 (400 g) avocados, diced
  • 240 ml (180 g) Greek yoghurt (4% fat)
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • salt and ground black pepper

Instructions

  1. Mix the avocado with yoghurt and garlic in a bowl.
  2. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
