Description

Cauliflower Rice

Ingredients

  • 350 (g) cauliflower
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • salt to taste
  • Â¼ tsp turmeric (optional)

Instructions

  1. Shred the cauliflower using a grater or grater attachment on a food processor.
  2. You can also chop it finely with a knife.
  3. Melt butter in a skillet.
  4. Add the cauliflower and cook over medium heat for 5-10 minutes or until the riced cauliflower has softened a bit.
  5. Add salt and the optional turmeric, or any other spices of preference, while frying.
