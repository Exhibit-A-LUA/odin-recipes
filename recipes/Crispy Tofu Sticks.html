Crispy Tofu Sticks

Description

Crispy tofu is pan-fried to perfection.

Ingredients

  • 400 g firm tofu
  • 1 tbsp coconut oil
  • salt to taste (adjust depending on which other flavourings you are adding)
  • 1 tbsp tamari soy sauce
  • 1 tsp desired seasoning such as chilli powder or taco seasoning (optional)

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Slice the tofu into sticks, about 0.5" (1 cm).
  3. Distribute the tofu on a clean, absorbent kitchen towel or paper towels.
  4. Pat dry to remove excess moisture; this step is key for crispiness.
  5. Add the coconut oil to an oven-safe skillet over medium-high heat.
  6. Alternatively, use a regular frying pan and prepare a baking tray with parchment paper.
  7. When the oil is hot, add the tofu and salt.
  8. Fry for a couple of minutes or until the tofu is lightly golden.
  9. Add the tamari soy sauce and seasoning of your choice.
  10. Stir the tofu, mixing well.
  11. Transfer the skillet with the tofu to the oven, or transfer the tofu to the parchment-lined baking tray and then put it in the oven.
  12. Bake for 15 minutes or until the tofu is crispy.
