Spanish Pizza Crust

Description

Spanish Tuna Pizza Crust or Coca

Ingredients

  • 180 g tuna in water drained
  • 100 g (300 ml) shredded Parmesan cheese
  • 1 tbsp ground psyllium husk powder
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 1 tsp dried oregano (optional)
  • 1 tsp garlic powder (optional)

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, mix the ingredients for the crust with a fork until you get a homogeneous dough.
  2. Place the dough between two sheets of parchment paper and roll it out into a thin rectangle (less than 1/2 inch or 2 mm).
  3. Put it on a baking sheet keeping only the bottom sheet of the parchment paper.
  4. Bake for 10 minutes at 360°F (180°C) on the upper rack.
  5. Then, carefully flip the crust over on the parchment paper and bake for another 10 minutes.
  6. Remove from the oven and let cool for 10 minutes on a cooling rack until it hardens and becomes crispier.
