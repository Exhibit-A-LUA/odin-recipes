Sour Cream and Onion Omelet

Description

This 4-ingredient keto omelet is all that and a bag of chips. Seriously though, the inspiration behind this recipe was a can of pringles! Enjoy these classic munchy flavours without the extra carbs in this quick keto breakfast recipe.

Ingredients

  • 3 large eggs
  • 60 ml (30 g) shredded cheese
  • 60 ml (40 g) red onions, finely diced
  • 1½ tbsp sour cream, room temperature
  • salt and ground black pepper, to taste
  • ½ tbsp butter

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, beat together the eggs, cheese, onion, sour cream, salt, and pepper.
  2. Heat butter or oil in a large frying pan over medium-high heat.
  3. When hot, add the egg mixture.
  4. Cook for 3 to 4 minutes, or until your preferred done-ness is reached.
  5. Fold it into a half-moon shape and serve right away.

Tips

  • If you prefer, you can sauté the red onions for a couple of minutes first to caramelize and soften them.
  • Garnish with extra sour cream and red onion to dress it up.
