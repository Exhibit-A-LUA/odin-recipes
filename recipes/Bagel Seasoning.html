Bagel Seasoning

Ingredients

  • 3 tbsp white sesame seeds
  • 2 tbsp black sesame seeds
  • 2 tbsp dried onion flakes
  • 2 tbsp dried minced garlic granules
  • 2 tbsp poppy seeds
  • 2 tbsp flaky sea salt (I've used flaky pink Himalayan sea salt )

Instructions

  1. Toast sesame seeds in a fry pan for about 5 minutes or until white seeds have slightly changed to be golden, on a medium heat.
  2. Let seeds cool in a bowl
  3. Once cooled, add all other ingredients and mix together
  4. Pour into a glass jar and its ready to enjoy on your meals, snacks, and next cook ups!
