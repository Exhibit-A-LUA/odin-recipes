Chicken Breast Tex-Mex

Description

Chicken Breast Tex-Mex

Ingredients

  • 2 (450 g) chicken breasts 8 oz (230 g) each
  • salt and ground black pepper
  • 2 tsp taco seasoning (optional)
  • 1 tbsp olive oil or butter

Instructions

  1. To speed up the cooking process, slice the chicken breasts horizontally and lengthwise into thin cutlets.
  2. Season both sides of the chicken with salt, ground black pepper, and seasoning of choice.
  3. Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat.
  4. Add the oil or butter to the skillet when hot.
  5. Carefully place the chicken breasts in the skillet and cook for about 4 minutes on each side.
  6. The chicken is ready to serve when the inside is no longer pink.
