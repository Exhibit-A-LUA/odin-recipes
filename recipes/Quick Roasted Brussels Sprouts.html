Description

Quick Roasted Brussels Sprouts

Ingredients

  • 450 (g) Brussels sprouts, trimmed and halved
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • ½ tsp salt
  • ¼ tsp ground black pepper

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Trim and halve the sprouts.
  3. Add them to a baking dish, and add olive oil, salt, and pepper..
  4. Toss to combine..
  5. Bake for 30 minutes, turning them after 15 minutes..
  6. Meanwhile, prepare the salmon and horseradish sauce..
