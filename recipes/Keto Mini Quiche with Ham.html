Keto Mini Quiche with Ham

Description

Delicious for breakfast, brunch, or lunch! Crispy prosciutto is the perfect protein-packed crust, filled with a creamy egg, mascarpone cheese, and chives on top. Simplicity at its best.

Ingredients

  • 400 (g) prosciutto, sliced or ham, cured
  • 8 large eggs
  • 200 (g) (200 ml) mascarpone cheese or cream cheese, room temperature
  • 3 tbsp finely chopped, fresh chives

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350째F (180째C).
  2. Set aside small, oven-proof ramekins (see tips).
  3. Place three slices of prosciutto in each ramekin, covering the sides and bottom.
  4. In a medium-sized bowl, whisk together the eggs, mascarpone cheese, chives, salt, and pepper.
  5. The mixture doesn't have to be completely smooth.
  6. Pour the egg mixture into the ramekins, and bake on the middle rack, for 15 minutes or until the egg mixture has set in the middle.
  7. Serve slightly warm.

Serving suggestion

  • If you're having this for lunch or dinner, a fresh salad will be great with this dish.

Air fryer method

  1. Preheat the air fryer to 325째F (160째C).
  2. Set aside oven-safe ramekins or silicone baking cups.
  3. Proceed with steps 2 and 3 above.
  4. In step 6 above, place the ramekins in the air fryer for 10-12 minutes until the egg mixture has set in the middle.
