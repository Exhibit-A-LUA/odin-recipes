Description

Chicken Fritters

Ingredients

  • 800 (g) finely diced chicken breasts or ground chicken
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 tbsp mayonnaise
  • 28 (g) (65 ml) scallions, finely sliced
  • ½ tbsp lemon zest (optional)
  • 1½ tsp salt
  • ¼ tsp ground black pepper
  • 2 tbsp olive oil, for frying

Mayonnaise

  • see Mayonnaise Recipe

Instructions

  1. In a big bowl, mix all the ingredients for the chicken fritters.
  2. Heat a large frying pan (or two depending on how many servings you make) with olive oil over medium-high heat.
  3. With a big spoon or ice cream scoop, scoop the chicken mixture into the pan (about 3 fritters per serving).
  4. Press them down with a spatula to make a fritter.
  5. Fry on both sides for about 6 minutes or until cooked all the way through and golden.
