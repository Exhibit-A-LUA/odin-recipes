Anjali's Chocolate Cake

Ingredients

  • 200 grams salted butter at room temperature
  • 4 eggs
  • 200 grams castor sugar
  • 200 grams all purpose flour
  • 50 grams cocoa powder
  • 1 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 125 ml curd
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla essence

Instructions

  1. Sift the flour, cocoa, baking powder and soda.
  2. Pile up all the ingredients in the food processor starting with the butter, eggs, sugar.
  3. Then add the sifted flour and lastly the curd and essence.
  4. Blend till well mixed.
  5. Pour into an 8 inch round tin.
  6. Place in a preheated oven at 180Â° for fifty minutes.
  7. Let the cake come to room temperature before turning it over into a plate.
