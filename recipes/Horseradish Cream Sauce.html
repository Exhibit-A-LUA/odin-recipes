Description

Horseradish Cream Sauce

Ingredients

  • 240 ml heavy whipping cream
  • 1 tbsp grated fresh horseradish, or prepared from a jar
  • 1 tbsp Dijon mustard
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • ½ tsp sea salt
  • ¼ tsp ground black pepper
  • 1 tbsp fresh chives, finely chopped, plus extra for garnish

Instructions

  1. Add the heavy cream to a saucepan over medium heat and gently bring to a soft boil.
  2. Once it starts to bubble, turn the heat down to simmer until the cream thickens a bit; this takes 8-10 minutes.
  3. Remove the pan from the heat.
  4. Whisk in the horseradish, Dijon mustard, lemon juice, salt, and freshly ground black pepper.
  5. Return to very low heat to keep warm, stirring occasionally.
  6. It should not boil.
