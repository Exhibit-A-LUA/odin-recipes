Description

Bang Bang Cauliflower

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 tbsp sriracha sauce
  • 1 tsp lime juice
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • ¼ tsp salt
  • ¼ tsp ground black pepper
  • 220 g cauliflower, cut into florets

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C) and line a baking tray with parchment paper.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together olive oil, sriracha sauce, lime juice, garlic powder, salt, and black pepper.
  3. Place the cauliflower on the prepared baking tray, pour over the chili sauce, and toss until fully coated.
  4. Spread it out to a single layer and roast for 30 minutes until it starts to brown at the edges.

Air fryer method

  • Prepare the cauliflower through Step 1 in the recipe.
  • Place one third of the cauliflower in a single layer in the air fryer basket.
  • Fry at 360°F (180°C) for 12 minutes, tossing halfway though.
  • Repeat with the rest of the cauliflower.
