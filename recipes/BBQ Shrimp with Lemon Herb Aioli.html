BBQ Shrimp with Lemon Herb Aioli

Description

Perfect for a family share platter, this keto shrimp dish is fast and easy to prepare with loads of fresh, healthy flavours

Ingredients

BBQ Shrimp (934.71g)

  • see BBQ Shrimp Recipe

Easy Lemon Herb Aioli (196.87g)

  • see Easy Lemon Herb Aioli Recipe

Serving

  • 950 ml (120 g) baby spinach
  • 280 g cucumber, sliced or cut into ribbons
  • 140 g cherry tomatoes
  • 1 tsp chilli flakes (optional)
  • 2 tbsp fresh parsley chopped (optional)
  • 1 lemon, cut into wedges (optional)

Instructions

BBQ Shrimp (934.71g)

  • see BBQ Shrimp Recipe

Easy Lemon Herb Aioli (196.87g)

  • see Easy Lemon Herb Aioli Recipe

Serving

  1. Lay shrimp on a bed of spinach, cucumber ribbons, and cherry tomatoes on a serving platter and top with your cooked shrimp.
  2. Garnish with lemon wedges, chilli flakes, and some fresh parsley.
  3. Serve with a generous dollop of Easy lemon herb aioli

Tips

  • The aioli can be made in advance and will store well in the fridge for up to one week.
