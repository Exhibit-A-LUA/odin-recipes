Spinach Paratha

Description

Spinach Paratha

Ingredients

  • 2 cups Spinach (around 200 gms)
  • 2.5 cups Whole Wheat Flour
  • ½ tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 Green Chilli
  • 4 – 5 cloves of garlic (pieces from the garlic bulb)
  • Salt to Taste
  • Oil for brushing

Instructions

  1. Wash the spinach leaves thoroughly, make sure there is no grit in the leaves.
  2. Add into a mixer along with the cumin seeds, garlic and chilli.
  3. Turn it into a smooth, fine paste, adding as little water as possible.
  4. Add flour into a bowl followed by spinach paste and some salt to taste.
  5. Mix it all together until you get a smooth doughy texture.
  6. After that, add a teaspoon of oil and mix thoroughly into the dough.
  7. Let it sit for 20 minutes.
  8. Pinch the dough out into evenly sized balls.
  9. Take one, flatten it and apply some dry flour to both sides.
  10. Using a rolling pin, roll it flat into a roti shape.
  11. Heat up the pan and put one of the parathas on for a minute or two before flipping it over.
  12. You can drop some oil on the semi-cooked side and turn it over again.
  13. Apply oil to the other side and turn it over again.
  14. Your paratha is now ready to eat.
