Mug Bread

Description

A 5-minute high protein mug bread. It's keto, gluten-free, quick, and versatile, come up with all sorts of sandwich toppings.

Ingredients

  • 1 tsp soft butter
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 tbsp cream cheese, at room temperature
  • 1 tbsp almond flour
  • 1 tbsp whey protein isolate (unflavored) or pea protein powder (unflavored)
  • ½ tsp baking powder
  • 1 pinch salt

Instructions

  1. Grease a flat-bottomed mug with butter.
  2. Crack in the egg and cream cheese.
  3. Combine with a fork or tiny whisk until as smooth as possible.
  4. Add the almond flour, whey protein isolate, baking powder, and salt.
  5. Mix until combined.
  6. Microwave on medium-high (approximately 700 watts) for 2 minutes.
  7. Check if the bread is done in the middle — if not, microwave for another 15-30 seconds.
  8. Let cool for a minute and remove from the mug.
  9. For a crunchier texture, slice the bread in half and toast it.
