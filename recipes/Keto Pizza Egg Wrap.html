Keto Pizza Egg Wrap

Description

f you eat breakfast, make it satisfying and quick. This hearty keto breakfast wrap has 25 grams of protein to keep you full for hours and is much faster and healthier than any drive-through choice.

Ingredients

  • 2 large eggs
  • ½ tbsp butter
  • ½ tbsp tomato sauce
  • 14 (g) (30 ml) mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • 45 (g) salami, sliced

Instructions

  1. Heat a large, non-stick frying pan over medium heat.
  2. Add the butter.
  3. Crack the eggs into a bowl, and whisk until combined.
  4. Slowly pour the eggs into the pan, allowing the mixture to go right to the edges.
  5. Cook until the edges begin to lift off the side of the frying pan.
  6. Using a spatula all around the edge, lift the egg from the pan.
  7. Flip and cook on the other side for 30 seconds.
  8. Remove from the pan.
  9. Layer tomato sauce, mozzarella cheese, and salami in the middle.
  10. Roll it together into a wrap.

Tips

  • If you prefer the cheese to be melted, put the wrap back into the pan until the cheese is melted.
