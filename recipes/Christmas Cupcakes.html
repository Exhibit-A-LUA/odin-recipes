Christmas Cupcakes

Description

descriCinnamon, cardamom, ginger, and clove come together in this lightly sweet cupcake.ption

Ingredients

  • 110 (g) butter, melted
  • 1 tbsp ground cinnamon
  • 1 tbsp ground cardamom (green)
  • 1 tsp ground cloves
  • 1 tsp ground ginger
  • 120 ml heavy whipping cream
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 600 ml (280 g) almond flour
  • 3 tbsp ground psyllium husk powder
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 6 large eggs
  • 120 ml (110 g) erythritol

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (180°C).
  2. Prepare a 12-cup muffin tin by greasing or lining with paper liners.
  3. Use a small bowl to combine the melted butter, cinnamon, cardamom, cloves, ginger, and heavy cream. Set aside.
  4. In a medium-sized bowl, mix the almond flour with psyllium fibre powder, baking powder, and salt. Set aside.
  5. In another bowl, beat the eggs, vanilla extract, and sweetener with an electric mixer.
  6. Beat until the sweetener has dissolved and the eggs are frothy.
  7. Beat in the butter mixture and the flour mixture a little at a time, continuously mixing at low speed.
  8. Let the batter rest for a few minutes.
  9. Use two spoons to scoop the batter into the prepared muffin tins.
  10. Divide the batter evenly among the wells.
  11. Bake for 20-25 minutes until the tops are just firm to the touch.
  12. Let cool completely before frosting.

Recommended special equipment

  • Electric mixer
  • Cupcake moulds / 12-cup muffin tin
