Veg Paneer Paratha

Description

Veg Paneer Paratha

Ingredients

Dough

  • ¾ cups of whole wheat flour
  • ½ tsp olive oil
  • Salt to taste

Filling

  • ¼ cup boiled mashed green peas
  • ¼ cup grated carrots
  • ¼ cup grated paneer
  • 2 tsp grated beetroot
  • 1 tsp chopped coriander
  • 1 tsp finely chopped green chillies
  • salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Combine all the ingredients and knead into a soft dough.
  2. Divide the dough and filling into 4 equal portions.
  3. Place filling inside each portion and seal it tightly.
  4. Roll out the dough and sprinkle whole wheat flour for rolling.
  5. Take a hot non-stick pan and apply a small brush of ghee/butter.
  6. Cook the paratha until you see golden brown specks.
  7. Serve hot with curd.
