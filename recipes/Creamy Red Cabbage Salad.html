Creamy Red Cabbage Salad

Description

Creamy Red Cabbage Salad

Ingredients

  • 600 (g) red cabbage, sliced
  • 55 (g) butter
  • 170 (g) leeks, thinly sliced
  • 60 ml cider vinegar
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp ground nutmeg
  • 1 tsp ground black pepper
  • 240 ml sour cream
  • ½ meat bouillon cube (optional)
  • 120 ml drippings from the meat

Instructions

  1. Use a large skillet to fry the sliced cabbage in half of the butter.
  2. When the cabbage is tender, add the leek and the remaining butter.
  3. Fry until the leek is tender, turning occasionally.
  4. Pour in the vinegar, and season the vegetables with salt, pepper, and nutmeg.
  5. Let simmer for 5 minutes.
  6. Stir in the sour cream, the gravy from the ribs, and bouillon.
  7. Let simmer for an additional 10–20 minutes to thicken.
  8. Serve the creamy red cabbage together with the ribs and red onions.
