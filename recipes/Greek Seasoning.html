Greek Seasoning

Ingredients

  • 3 tbsp oregano
  • 2 tbsp basil
  • 1 tbsp dill
  • 2 tbsp onion powder
  • 2 tbsp garlic powder
  • ½ tbsp salt
  • 1 tbsp black pepper

Instructions

  1. Mix in a bowl, stir together all ingredients including the dried oregano, basil, dill, onion powder, garlic powder, salt and pepper.

Tips

  • Use- Aim for 2 tablespoons of seasoning per pound of chicken or vegetables.
  • Recipe makes ½ cup
  • Store in an air tight container at room temperature for up to 1 year.
  • Keep out of direct sunlight and store in a cool, dark pantry for best results.
