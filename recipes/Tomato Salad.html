Tomato Salad

Description

Tomato Salad

Ingredients

  • 450 (g) (600 ml) tomatoes, diced
  • 28 (g) white onions, thinly sliced
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp red wine vinegar
  • salt and ground black pepper

Instructions

  1. Mix the tomatoes with the white onion, olive oil, vinegar, salt, and pepper in a bowl.
