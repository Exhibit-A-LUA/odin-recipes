Low Carb Christmas Yule Log

Description

Yule logs are a European Christmas tradition. But can this rich, festive cake actually be made low Carbs? You bet! This delicious version takes a bit of time to create and assemble, but the result is worth it in appearance and decadent chocolate taste. Indulge in this treat while your Yuletide logs burn bright.

Instructions

Log Construction

  1. Spread the filling evenly over the surface with a palette knife, leaving a 3/4" (2 cm) border all around the edges.
  2. Scatter over the raspberries, breaking them up with your fingers if they are large.
  3. Use the parchment to roll up the sponge lengthwise around the cream filling.
  4. It should overlap slightly to seal in the cream.
  5. Arrange the roll on a serving platter with the seam underneath.
  6. Spoon the chocolate coating over the log and use a palette knife to create the texture of bark.
  7. Scatter the chocolate shavings over top.
  8. Put in the fridge to chill for a minimum of 1 hour and a maximum of 1 day before serving.
