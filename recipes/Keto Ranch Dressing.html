Keto Ranch Dressing

Description

This keto ranch dressing starts with a large batch of dry seasoning that can be saved and used for other recipes. Next, we add the wet ingredients to make a keto dressing that is perfect for dipping. Best served with crunchy vegetables and of course – crispy bacon!

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp ranch seasoning
  • 180 ml mayonnaise
  • 180 ml sour cream
  • 180 ml heavy whipping cream
  • ½ tsp lemon juice
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. In a medium-sized bowl, mix together the ranch seasoning, mayonnaise, sour cream, heavy cream, and lemon juice.
  2. Season with salt, to taste.
  3. Refrigerate for at least an hour, before serving.

Tips

  • For a thicker dressing, use less heavy cream.
  • For a thinner texture, stir in some water.
