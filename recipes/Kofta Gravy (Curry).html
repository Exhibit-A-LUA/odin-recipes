Kofta Gravy (Curry)

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup onion paste
  • 1 cup tomato paste
  • 1/4 cup cashew paste
  • 3/4 tsp red chilli powder
  • 1 inch ginger
  • 4-5 garlic cloves â€“ made in paste
  • 1/4 tsp garam masala
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 tsp kasuri methi/dry fenugreek leaves
  • salt as required
  • sugar as required (optional)
  • oil for shallow or deep frying the koftas

Instructions

  1. Heat about 1 or 1.5 tbsp oil.
  2. Add all the garam masala and fry till the oil becomes fragrant.
  3. Add the onion paste and brown it.
  4. Add the ginger-garlic paste and fry for some seconds till the raw aroma of the paste goes away.
  5. Add the tomato puree and saute for 2-3 minutes.
  6. Add the turmeric powder, red chili powder, garam masala powder and cashew paste.
  7. Stir and saute till the oil starts to leave the sides of the masala paste.
  8. Add water and stir.
  9. Simmer till the gravy begins to thicken approx 11-12 minutes on a low flame.
  10. Towards the end add crushed kasuri methi leaves and salt.
  11. You can also add sugar if you want a light sweet taste in the curry.
  12. Simmer for 1-2 minutes more.
