Description

Grilled Chicken Breast

Ingredients

  • 500 (g) boneless chicken breast without the skin
  • 3 tbsp olive oil (extra virgin)
  • 4 large cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp thyme (dried)
  • ½ tsp oregano (dried)
  • Salt to taste. Recommended 1¼ teaspoons
  • ½ tsp ground black pepper
  • 1 tsp lemon zest

Instructions

  1. Find a ziplock bag and lay the chicken flat inside it.
  2. Use something heavy and wide to pound it so that it spreads wider.
  3. Do this until it is around ½ inch thick.
  4. Take care not to hit it too hard, it is better to take your time and use consistent blows.
  5. Mix all the other ingredients in a bowl and add it to the ziplock bag if it is still in good shape.
  6. If you do not have a usable ziplock you can do this on a plate.
  7. Massage the chicken so that the mix is evenly spread across it.
  8. After this, put the bag or plate in the fridge and let it stay there for at least 4 hours so it can absorb the marinade.
  9. Once ready, preheat the oven for a while before you add the chicken.
  10. It should cook quickly, so keep it on each side for 3 minutes to get the grill marks.
  11. Once done, serve and eat.
