Protein Poha

Description

Protein Poha - Serves 2

Ingredients

  • 60 (g) Poha rice
  • 30 (g) sprouts or paneer
  • 2 diced green chilli
  • 2 spoons peanuts
  • ½ cup chopped tomatoes
  • 1 tsp mustard seeds
  • ¼ tsp turmeric powder
  • ½ tsp red chilli powder
  • 2 tbsp cabbage, capsicum and peas (other vegetables of your choice)
  • Curry leaves
  • 1 tsp cooking oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Garnish with coriander

Instructions

  1. If you’re using sprouts, boil sprouts until they become soft.
  2. In a bowl, add half a cup of water and the poha and set aside.
  3. Use cold water to rinse if you want it out of the water immediately.
  4. Put a pan on the stove, add the oil and roast some peanuts in it.
  5. Once they have cooked, take them out to add later on.
  6. After removing the peanuts, add mustard seeds to the oil and cook till a crackling or popping noise begins.
  7. At that point, add chillies and curry leaves.
  8. Add all the vegetables except the tomatoes and cook till they are soft, saute for more even cooking.
  9. Add salt along with chilli powder and turmeric.
  10. Add tomatoes and cook for 2 – 3 minutes after mixing well with the rest of the ingredients.
  11. Add a little water and wait for the tomatoes to become squishy.
  12. Add sprouts or paneer as per your choice and mix until well coated with the mixture.
  13. Now add the poha.
  14. Keep low heat and mix while you cook for around 3 – 5 minutes.
  15. After which you can add the peanuts and coriander.
  16. Turn off the flame and let it rest.
