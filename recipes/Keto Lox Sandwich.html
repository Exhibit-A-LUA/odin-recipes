Keto Lox Sandwich

Description

We can't get enough of one of our latest hits, the 5-minute high protein mug bread. It's keto, gluten-free, quick, and versatile, and we've had a (tasty) blast coming up with all sorts of sandwich toppings. This classic features the refreshing combination of cream cheese, smoked salmon, cucumber, red onion, and capers. Elegant, easy, and ever so scrumptious — won't you join us for a bite to eat?

Ingredients

Mug Bread

  • see Mug Bread Recipe

Toppings

  • 1 tbsp cream cheese
  • 28 g cucumber, sliced
  • 85 g smoked salmon
  • ½ tbsp red onions, thinly sliced
  • 1 tsp capers (optional)
  • ground black pepper to taste (optional)

Instructions

Mug Bread

  • see Mug Bread Recipe

Toppings

  1. Spread cream cheese on both halves and top with cucumber, smoked salmon, thinly sliced red onion, and capers.
  2. Finish it off with freshly ground black pepper.
  3. Serve it open-faced or sandwich style.

Instructions for baking in the oven

  1. If you're making more than one batch of bread, you might find it easier to use the oven method.
  2. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C), and grease a 4.5-inch (10 cm) wide, oven-proof mug.
  3. You can also use large cupcake liners, in which case there's no need to grease them.
  4. Instead, add the butter (melted) to the batter.
  5. In a medium-sized bowl, whisk together all dry ingredients.
  6. Add the melted butter, egg and cream cheese, whisking until smooth.
  7. Make sure there are no lumps.
  8. Pour the batter into the prepared mug(s) or cupcake liner(s).
  9. Bake for 10-15 minutes.
  10. Insert a toothpick or sharp knife into the middle of the bread.
  11. If the toothpick comes out clean, it is done!
  12. Let cool and remove the bread from the mug(s) or cupcake liner(s).
  13. Slice in half and toast for the best texture and taste.
