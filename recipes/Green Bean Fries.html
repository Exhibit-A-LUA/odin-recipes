Green Bean Fries

Description

Green Bean Fries

Ingredients

  • 170 g fresh green beans or wax beans
  • 2 tbsp light olive oil or coconut oil (flavourless)
  • salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Wash and trim the green beans.
  2. Pat them dry with a paper towel (to avoid oil splatters).
  3. Heat a large skillet with oil over medium-high heat.
  4. Add the green beans to the pan and fry for about 5 minutes.
  5. They should retain their fresh, natural crunch!
  6. Place the green beans on a plate with a paper towel to absorb any excess oil.
  7. Season with salt before serving.
