Egg Bhurji 2

Ingredients

  • 4 Eggs
  • 2 onions, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 3 green chillies or as per taste
  • 1 capsicum, chopped
  • 2-inch ginger, finely chopped
  • ½ cup coriander leaves
  • 3 – 4 curry leaves
  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil or ghee
  • 1 tsp jeera
  • 1 tsp red chilli powder
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • As per taste pav bhaji masala
  • A pinch of asafoetida (hing)
  • Salt (as per taste)

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, whisk the eggs using a spoon and keep them aside.
  2. Take a pan, add oil or ghee and heat.
  3. Now add jeera, onions, hing, green chillies, curry leaves, capsicum and ginger.
  4. Sauté for 3 – 4 minutes.
  5. Add tomatoes and few coriander leaves.
  6. Sauté for 2 minutes.
  7. Add 1 tbsp butter, pav bhaji masala, red chilli powder, turmeric powder and salt as per taste.
  8. Mix well.
  9. Lastly, pour in the slightly beaten eggs and keep whisking until cooked.
  10. Top it up with butter.
  11. Garnish with the remaining coriander leaves.
