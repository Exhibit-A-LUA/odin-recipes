Keto Holiday Turkey Meatballs With Roasted Brussels Sprouts

Description

These juicy, and simple to make, keto meatballs with a hint of allspice and cinnamon pairs perfectly with the roasted Brussels sprouts and crunchy pecan nuts. Serve them with a generous dollop of the luscious mustard mayo, and we're sure you'll be hitting that favourite button.
