Salmon Marinade

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp light olive oil
  • 2 tbsp tamari soy sauce
  • 2 tsp fresh ginger, grated
  • 1 garlic clove, minced
  • 1 lime, the juice
  • Â½ red chilli pepper, finely chopped
  • 2 tsp sesame seeds
  • salt and ground black pepper

Instructions

  1. In a medium-sized bowl, mix the ingredients to the marinade.
  2. Place the salmon in the bowl and toss together.
  3. Cover or seal, and refrigerate for at least 10 minutes.
