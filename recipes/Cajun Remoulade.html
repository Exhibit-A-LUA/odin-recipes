Cajun Remoulade

Description

Cajun Remoulade

Ingredients

  • 120 ml mayonnaise
  • 2 tsp cajun seasoning
  • 1 tsp paprika powder
  • 1 tsp capers
  • salt, to taste

Instructions

  1. Stir together the mayonnaise, cajun seasoning, paprika, and capers until completely combined.
  2. Add salt to taste.
