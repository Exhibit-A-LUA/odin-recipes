Tuna Stuffed Avocado

Description

Looking for a healthy breakfast with no eggs? Grab a spoon and dig into this quick and easy budget-friendly keto breakfast that will keep you satisfied for hours! Prepare the creamy filling in advance and keep it in the fridge until serving for the ultimate fuss-free morning.

Ingredients

  • 2 (400 g) avocados, halved lengthwise, pit removed (see tip)
  • 140 g tuna in water, drained
  • 60 ml mayonnaise or vegan mayonnaise
  • 1 (40 g) celery stalk, finely chopped
  • 28 g (40 ml) red onions, finely chopped
  • salt and ground black pepper, to taste
  • 1 tbsp fresh chives, finely chopped (optional)

Mayonnaise

  • see Mayonnaise Recipe

Instructions

  1. In a small bowl, mix together the tuna, mayonnaise, celery, and onion, until well combined.
  2. Season with salt and pepper, to taste.
  3. For serving, top each avocado half with equal amounts of tuna filling, and garnish with chives.

Tips

  • Make a slice in the bottom of the base of each avocado half to make it more stable on your plate.
  • The filling stays fresh in the refrigerator in an air-tight container for at least four days.
  • Avocado halves may brown slightly around the edges.
  • Instead of tuna, you can also use chopped cooked chicken, ham, or salmon to make this delicious on-the-go keto breakfast.
