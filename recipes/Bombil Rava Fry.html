Bombil Rava Fry

Description

Bombil Rava Fry

Ingredients

  • 5 Large sized 700gms Fresh Bombay Duck-Bombil
  • 1-tsp Turmeric Powder
  • 1 Lemon Juice
  • 3 Green Chilies
  • 7-8 Cloves of Garlic
  • 1-inch Ginger root
  • Cooking Oil
  • ¾ Cup 150 gms Semolina (Bombay Rava)
  • ½ Cup 75 gms Rice flour (can be replaced with Wheat Flour or Sorghum Flour)
  • 4-tbsp Malvani Masala (or 3 tbsp Kashmiri red Chili Powder + 1-tbsp Garam Masala Powder)
  • Salt

Instructions

Clean Bombay Duck fish

  1. Spread a paper on the board, so that it doesn't get dirty.
  2. Take a chopping board and a sharp knife.
  3. Take a fish on chopping board.
  4. Chop off Tail, Head and Fins using a sharp knife
  5. Ensure knife that is used for cleaning is sharp.
  6. Cut below the head in a diagonal manner and pull out the offal.
  7. Chop off the fins now.

Remove its scales and organs

  1. With the blunt edge of knife scrape off the shells by running it along its body from tail to head.
  2. Cut along its stomach and open the fish.
  3. We will run the knife along this faint line running through its stomach.
  4. Remove the offal from inside and discard.
  5. Keep going deeper gradually so that we can open the fish completely.
  6. You can use the center bone as a reference for cutting along.
  7. Flip to other side.
  8. If it still does not look completely flat, you can go a little deeper.
  9. Do not cut too deep, we don't want to cut it into two different parts.
  10. Wash and clean the fish with water.
  11. After cleaning with water, pat dry with a kitchen napkin.

Prepare the fish

  1. Marinate with Turmeric Powder, Salt and Lemon Juice.
  2. Turmeric Powder has antiseptic and anti-bacterial properties.
  3. Lemon Juice and Turmeric Powder helps get rid of fish smell.
  4. Apply this mixture on fish on both sides.
  5. Keep aside for 10 mins.
  6. Bombay Duck has lot of moisture content which must be removed from the fish.
  7. We will take a chopping board.
  8. You can take any large flat object and cover with a piece of cloth.
  9. Arrange marinated fish on this cloth.
  10. Now, cover fish with another piece of cloth.
  11. Place another chopping board/flat surface object.
  12. Place a heavy object/load on this board.
  13. Leave the fish for 30 mins.
  14. For second marination, grind chilies, ginger and garlic together.
  15. Grind into a thick and coarse paste with 1-2 tsp water.
  16. The coarse paste is ready. Keep it aside.
  17. After removing the excess moisture and the fish has become firm.
  18. Take a large plate, and put green masala paste.
  19. Apply this masala on all the fish. Fish is ready now.
  20. We will refrigerate for 10 mins before frying.
  21. This will help in making fish firm and it becomes easy to handle while frying.

Cook the fish

  1. Now, we will make a dry batter for frying the fish.
  2. In a large bowl, put Semolina, Rice flour and Malvani Masala.
  3. If you don't have this Masala, you can use 3 tbsp Kashmiri red Chili Powder+ 1 tbsp Garam Masala Powder instead.
  4. Add Salt to taste and mix well.
  5. We have used more Semolina/Rava in the batter as this is Rava fry.
  6. If you chose, you can skip Rava completely and make a batter of rice flour, Malvani masala and salt.
  7. You can store this dry batter in your refrigerator and use it when required for fish fry.
  8. Take some quantity of batter in a plate and reserve remaining.
  9. Remove fish from refrigerator and coat it with this batter.
  10. Apply the coating nicely.
  11. Put a pan on heat. Let's start frying the fish now.
  12. We have heated 2-3 tbsp Cooking Oil in a Pan.
  13. As the Oil becomes hot, reduce heat and put a batter-coated fish on the pan and fry on low-medium heat.
  14. For a good fish fry, it is important that you heat the Oil and tawa perfectly and then fry fish on low-medium heat.
  15. If Oil temperature is too low, fish will stick to the pan and while changing its side its skin may come off.
  16. Such fried fish does not look appetizing.
  17. Cook the underside for 3 mins, then change sides.
  18. The underside is well cooked and looks crisp fried.
  19. Spread the Oil all around the tawa.
  20. Cook for 3 more mins from other side.
  21. Remove from pan after 3 mins.
  22. Add some more Oil and fry remaining fish.
