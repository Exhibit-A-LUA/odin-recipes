Burrata With Tomato, Cream And Pesto

Description

A marriage of beautiful flavours, all in one rustic low carb salad. Fresh burrata cheese, tomato, and pesto topped with roasted pine nuts for crunch.

Ingredients

  • 21 g (37 ml) pine nuts
  • 2 (230 g) tomatoes
  • 60 ml (60 g) green pesto
  • 280 g burrata
  • 60 ml heavy whipping cream
  • 1 tbsp olive oil, for serving
  • salt and ground black pepper

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 °F (180 °C).
  2. Spread the pine nuts in a single layer, on a rimmed baking sheet.
  3. Bake on the middle rack, stirring occasionally for 5-10 minutes, or until lightly browned.
  4. Remove from the pan, and set aside.
  5. Break the tomatoes into pieces with your fingers, and arrange on a serving dish or individual dishes.
  6. Place dollops of the pesto and burrata, in between the tomatoes.
  7. Pull the burrata apart into chunks, allowing the interior cream to spill out onto the dish(es).
  8. To serve, drizzle the heavy cream and olive oil on top, and garnish with the pine nuts.
  9. Season with salt and pepper, to taste.
