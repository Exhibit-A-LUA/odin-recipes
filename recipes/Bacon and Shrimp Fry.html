Bacon and Shrimp Fry

Ingredients

  • 450 g peeled and deveined shrimp
  • 2 tsp cajun seasoning
  • Â½ tsp ground black pepper
  • salt (optional)
  • ite100 g baconm
  • 1 big minced garlic clove
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • 1 tsp fresh chives for garnish
  • 1 tsp lemon juice

Instructions

  1. Rinse, drain and pat dry the shrimp, add them to a large bowl.
  2. Season with the cajun seasoning, salt, and black pepper and set aside.
  3. If the seasoning contains salt already, you can reduce or omit it.
  4. Chop the bacon and fry it in a pan over medium-high heat until crispy, then remove with a slotted spoon and set aside.
  5. Add the shrimp to the same pan that you cooked the bacon in.
  6. Make sure you do NOT remove any of the bacon fat from the pan.
  7. About 2 minutes after adding the shrimp, add the minced garlic and cook for an additional 2 minutes.
  8. Next, add the butter to the frying pan and cook for another minute more until the shrimp is cooked all the way through.
  9. Turn off the heat and add in the lemon juice and give it a final stir.
  10. Serve the shrimp and bacon bits over keto grits and garnish with chives or the green part of a spring onion.
