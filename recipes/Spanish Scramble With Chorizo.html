Spanish Scramble With Chorizo

Description

Scrambled eggs make a simple and satisfying keto breakfast all on their own, but adding spicy, oily chorizo really makes them sing. Here, theyâ€™re paired with juicy tomatoes and crunchy chard, and a handful of Manchego cheese for a well-balanced meal that features plenty of healthy fats.

Ingredients

Chorizo and chard

  • 1 tbsp olive oil or butter
  • 28 (g) (40 ml) sliced, red onions
  • 200 (g) (1.3 litres) chard, stalks removed and chopped, leaves roughly chopped
  • 85 (g) chorizo (see tip)

Scrambled eggs

  • 4 large eggs
  • 2 egg yolks
  • salt and pepper
  • 1 tbsp olive oil or butter
  • 85 (g) cherry tomatoes or tomatoes, diced
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 55 (g) manchego cheese, shredded
  • 1 tbsp finely chopped, fresh basil, or fresh parsley (optional)

Instructions

Chorizo and chard

  1. Heat the olive oil in a large, non-stick skillet over medium heat.
  2. Add the sliced onion and cook for 2 to 3 minutes, until fragrant.
  3. Add the chard stalks and chorizo and cook for 5 to 8 minutes, or until the chard stalks are crisp-tender.
  4. Add the chard leaves and cook for 1 minute, until just wilted.
  5. Remove from heat, and transfer to a plate.

Scrambled eggs

  1. In a small bowl, whisk together the eggs and egg yolks, with a pinch of salt and pepper.
  2. Heat the olive oil in the same skillet, over medium-low heat.
  3. Pour in the eggs, and swirl the pan to coat the bottom.
  4. After about 1 minute, add the tomatoes.
  5. Using a spatula, stir occasionally to scramble the eggs, and cook for 3 to 5 more minutes.
  6. Remove from heat.
  7. Drizzle the eggs with olive oil, and top with the Manchego cheese and chopped herbs.
  8. Serve warm, with the chorizo and chard.

Tips

  • If using "Spanish" chorizo (hard and cured), dice, or cut into small cubes.
  • If using fully cooked chorizo sausage links, simply crumble the sausage after removing it from the casing.
