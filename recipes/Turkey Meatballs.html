Turkey Meatballs

Description

Turkey Meatballs

Ingredients

  • 650 (g) ground turkey or ground beef
  • 60 ml mayonnaise
  • ¾ tsp salt
  • ½ tsp ground black pepper
  • 1 tsp onion powder
  • 3 tbsp olive oil, for frying

Mayonnaise

  • see Mayonnaise Recipe

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, add the ground turkey, mayo, salt, pepper, and onion powder.
  2. Gently mix together with your hands, or a wooden spoon.
  3. Lightly moisten hands with water, and form the meat mixture into 1" (3 cm) meatballs.
  4. Heat the olive oil in a large frying pan over medium heat.
  5. Place the meatballs in the pan, evenly spaced out, and fry for about 2-3 minutes per side until browned on all sides (this can be done in batches if needed).
  6. Remove the meatballs from the pan with a slotted spoon, set aside, and keep warm.
  7. Leave the fat and drippings in the pan, and heat over medium temperature.
