Chocolate Dip

Description

Chocolate Dip

Ingredients

  • 110 (g) (200 ml) sugar-free baking chocolate

Instructions

  1. Use a double boiler or put a heat-proof glass bowl over a small pot of boiling water.
  2. Ensure the glass bowl does not touch the bottom of the pan.
  3. Add the dark chocolate a little at a time and stir until completely melted and glossy.
  4. Remove the bowl from the heat.
