Description

Mayonnaise Tzatziki Sauce

Ingredients

  • 55 (g) cucumber, peeled, shredded, and drained
  • 120 ml (90 g) Greek yoghurt (4% fat)
  • 60 ml mayonnaise
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • salt and pepper

Mayonnaise

  • see Mayonnaise Recipe

Instructions

  1. In a medium-sized bowl, mix together the shredded cucumber, yoghurt, mayonnaise, and garlic.
  2. Season with salt and pepper, to taste.
