Cauliflower Parmesan Mash

Description

Cauliflower Parmesan Mash

Ingredients

  • 220 g cauliflower
  • 80 ml (27 g) shredded Parmesan cheese
  • 28 g butter
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Cut the cauliflower into florets.
  2. Bring a pot of lightly salted water to a boil over high heat.
  3. Add the cauliflower and boil for a couple of minutes â€“ just enough so the florets are tender but retain a slightly firm texture.
  4. Drain the cauliflower in a colander, and discard the water.
  5. Add the cauliflower to a food processor (or use an immersion blender) along with the Parmesan cheese and butter.
  6. Pulse until it reaches a smooth and creamy consistency.
  7. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
