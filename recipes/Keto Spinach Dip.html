Keto Spinach Dip

Description

Spinach dip is pure, creamy deliciousness. This super-easy keto recipe takes no time to make and is a major crowd pleaser! Scrumptious for dipping, or use it as a sauce. Party on, keto-style!

Ingredients

  • 55 (g) frozen spinach
  • 2 tbsp dried parsley
  • 1 tbsp dried dill
  • 1 tsp onion powder
  • ½ tsp salt
  • ¼ tsp ground black pepper
  • 120 ml mayonnaise
  • 120 ml sour cream
  • 2 tsp lemon juice

Mayonnaise

  • see Mayonnaise Recipe

Instructions

  1. Thaw the frozen spinach and remove excessive liquid.
  2. Place in a bowl and mix with the other ingredients.
  3. Let sit for 10 minutes to let the flavours develop.

Tips

  • Get creative with your presentation... serve in a colourful crock, a hollowed-out veggie, or add a dollop on top of cucumber rounds.
