Pumpkin Spice

Description

Pumpkin is magical. And pumpkin pie... it truly means that autumn is upon us. Here, 5 amazing flavours unite to make a wonderful pumpkin pie spice. So warm and inviting, just like home.

Ingredients

  • 3 tbsp ground cinnamon
  • 2 tsp ground nutmeg
  • 2 tsp ground ginger
  • 1 tsp ground cloves
  • 1 tsp ground allspice

Instructions

  1. Mix the spices well and put in a jar with a tight-fitting lid.
  2. If you have whole spices on hand you can grind them using a spice or coffee grinder.
  3. Remember that the spices should be kept in a dark, dry and cool place.
  4. You can use small tin containers for storage.
