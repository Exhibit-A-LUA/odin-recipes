Ginger Chicken Udon Noodles

Ingredients

  • 1 tsp sunflower oil
  • 3 boneless, skinless chicken thighs, diced
  • Â¼ white cabbage, finely sliced
  • 25 (g) ginger, peeled and finely grated
  • 1 red chilli (deseeded if you like), finely chopped
  • 1 tbsp low-salt soy sauce
  • 2 tsp rice vinegar
  • 2 tsp mirin
  • 100 (g) ready-to-eat bean-sprouts
  • 3 spring onions, finely sliced
  • 300 (g) straight-to-wok udon noodles
  • small handful of coriander, finely chopped
  • 1 tbsp pickled ginger (optional)

Instructions

  1. Heat the oil in a large, deep frying pan over a medium-high heat.
  2. Once hot, stir-fry the chicken and cabbage for 5-7 mins until browned and almost cooked through
  3. Add the ginger and chilli, and cook for a few minutes more until fragrant.
  4. Add the remaining ingredients, except the coriander and pickled ginger, and fry until the chicken is cooked and the noodles are tender, about 1-2 mins more.
  5. Season, adding more soy, vinegar or mirin, if you like.
  6. Top with the coriander and pickled ginger, if using.
