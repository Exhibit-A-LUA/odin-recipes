Chilli Sauce

Ingredients

  • 1 red chilli pepper
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 6 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 fresh rosemary sprig, stem discarded
  • salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions

  1. Put all the sauce ingredients in a food processor and whizz to combine, or chop finely by hand and mix together.
  2. Transfer to a container and set aside.
