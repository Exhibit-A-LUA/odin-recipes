Rajma Masala

Description

Rajma Masala

Ingredients

Rajma

  • 1 cup rajma / red kidney bean
  • 1 tsp kasoori methi
  • 1 tbsp coriander leaves chopped
  • ¼ cup onion finely chopped
  • 1.5 cups tomato puree
  • 1 tsp ginger garlic paste
  • ¼ tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 heaped tsp red chilli powder
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala powder
  • salt to taste

Tempering

  • see Tempering Recipe

Instructions

  1. Soak rajma in water for 8hrs minimum or overnight.
  2. Soak in water till rajma is immersed fully and water should be at least 1/2 inch above.
  3. Next day rinse well at least twice.
  4. Add it to a pressure cooker along with water till immersing level.
  5. Pressure cook for 5 whistles.
  6. Let pressure release by itself
  7. Open to check, it should be soft when pressed easily mashable this is the right consistency.
  8. Heat 2 tbsp oil in a kadai.
  9. Add 1 tsp cumin seeds let it crackle completely.
  10. Add 1/4 cup onion finely chopped.
  11. Sauté until transparent then add 1 tsp ginger garlic paste.
  12. Sauté for a minute.
  13. Reduce flame to lowest.
  14. Add 1/4 tsp turmeric powder, 1 heaped tsp kashmiri red chilli powder and 1 tsp coriander powder.
  15. If you want you can add 1/2 tsp cumin powder.
  16. Give a quick mix.
  17. Do not burn the spice powders.
  18. Immediately add 1.5 cups blanched tomato puree. or blanched 4 tomatoes, removed the skin then pureed.
  19. Add 1/2 cup water, then give a quick mix.
  20. Let the gravy boil for 5-7 mins or until raw smell of the tomatoes and masalas leave.
  21. It does not take time as tomato is already blanched.
  22. Keep stirring in between to avoid sticking at the bottom.
  23. Add cooked rajma along with little of cooked water.
  24. If there is more of cooked water add only half of it.
  25. Give a quick mix.
  26. Cook covered for 7-10 mins.
  27. Give a quick mix.
  28. Mash rajma here and there if you prefer.
  29. Add 1/2 tsp sugar, 1 tsp kasoori methi(crush and add), 1 tbsp coriander leaves and 1 tsp garam masala powder.
  30. Give a quick mix.
  31. Finally add 1 tsp butter.
  32. Give a quick mix and Switch off.
  33. Rajma Masala is ready! Serve hot!
