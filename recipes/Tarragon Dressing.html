Description

Tarragon Dressing

Ingredients

  • 80 ml mayonnaise or vegan mayonnaise
  • 2 tbsp water
  • ½ tbsp dried tarragon
  • ½ tbsp Dijon mustard
  • 1 garlic clove, pressed

Instructions

  1. Whisk together the dressing ingredients in a bowl and refrigerate.
