Pancake Mix

Ingredients

  • 2 large eggs
  • Â½ tsp lemon juice
  • 2 tbsp powdered erythritol
  • 80 ml (38 g) almond flour
  • 2 tbsp coconut flour
  • 1 tbsp ghee or butter

Instructions

  1. Separate the eggs and add the egg whites to the bowl with the egg white left over from the filling.
  2. Set aside the egg yolks using the shell as a little bowl.
  3. Add the lemon juice to the egg whites and start whisking using an electric mixer or a hand whisk, and add in the erythritol.
  4. Keep beating until stiff peaks form.
  5. Fold the egg yolks into the mixture using a silicone spatula.
  6. Sift in the almond flour and coconut flour and gently combine with the egg white mixture without deflating it.
