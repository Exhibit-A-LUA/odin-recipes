Miso Glazed Salmon

Description

Miso Glazed Salmon

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp miso paste
  • 1 tbsp reduced sodium tamari soy sauce
  • 1 tsp lime juice
  • 350 g salmon, boneless fillets

Instructions

  1. Combine the miso paste, tamari soy sauce, and lime juice in a small bowl.
  2. Line a baking tray with parchment paper and place the salmon on it.
  3. Brush the miso mixture over the salmon.
  4. Bake for 8-12 minutes with the cauliflower.
  5. For an opaque, slightly underdone finish, bake a 1-inch (2.5 cm) thick fillet for 8-9 minutes.
  6. Leave it in for 11-12 minutes if you prefer your salmon well done.
