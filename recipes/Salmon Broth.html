Salmon Broth

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp coconut oil
  • 1 (110 g) small yellow onion, finely sliced
  • 1 (120 g) red bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 425 ml coconut milk
  • 550 ml warm chicken stock
  • 2 fresh or dried lime leaves (optional)
  • 550 g salmon, boneless fillets

Instructions

  1. Heat a large saucepan or wok over medium-high heat and add coconut oil.
  2. Saut√© onions until soft and translucent.
  3. Add red pepper and spice paste, and stir-fry until sizzling and fragrant.
  4. Pour in coconut milk, stock, and add lime leaves.
  5. Bring to a boil, then reduce the heat and simmer gently, uncovered for 5 minutes.
  6. Add the salmon and simmer for about 5 minutes or until the fish is cooked.

Tips

  • Feel free to substitute shrimp, shredded chicken, or tofu for the salmon.
