Loaded Keto Breakfast Wrap

Description

Treat yourself to a hearty and healthy keto breakfast that will keep you full for hours. The classic breakfast flavours of bacon, cheese, and avocado form a hearty wrap to get your day started right. Check out the tips section below for a variety of filling options that will suit your inventory!

Ingredients

Filling

  • 28 (g) cooked bacon, chopped
  • ¼ (50 g) avocado, diced
  • 3 tbsp mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • 1 tbsp sour cream

Wrap

  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tbsp heavy whipping cream
  • salt and ground black pepper
  • ½ tsp chilli powder
  • ½ tbsp fresh chives
  • 1 tbsp butter

Instructions

Filling

  1. Fry the bacon in a large frying pan over medium heat, for 10 minutes or until crispy.
  2. Place bacon on paper towels to absorb excess fat.
  3. Once cooled, chop or cut into small pieces and set aside.

Wrap

  1. Whisk the eggs, cream, chilli powder, chives, salt, and pepper in a small bowl.
  2. Melt the butter in the frying pan, then pour in the egg mixture.
  3. Swirl the frying pan until the egg mixture is evenly spread and thin.
  4. This will act as the wrap for your filling.
  5. Place a lid over the egg 'wrap' and leave to cook for 2 minutes.
  6. Gently lift the egg 'wrap' from the frying pan with a clean spatula onto a plate.
  7. Fold mozzarella cheese, bacon, avocado, and sour cream in the egg wrap.
  8. Enjoy

Tips

  • Try a filling of Cheese, ham, and chipotle sauce.
  • Try a filling of Wilted spinach and cream cheese.
  • Try a filling of Smoked salmon, dill, and cream cheese.
  • Try a filling of Roast beef and horseradish sauce.
