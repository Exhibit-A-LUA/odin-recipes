High Protein Keto Breakfast Bowl

Description

This keto breakfast is super easy and fast to make, but still yummy and packed with protein, healthy fats, and vitamins - almost too good to be true!

Ingredients

  • 2 boiled large eggs, cut in wedges
  • 85 (g) smoked deli ham or deli turkey
  • 110 (g) (120 ml) cottage cheese
  • 120 ml (15 g) baby spinach
  • 2 tsp Dijon mustard
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Place everything in a bowl.
  2. Season with salt and pepper to taste and serve.
  3. Boil the eggs for 5-6 minutes for soft-boiled eggs, 6-8 minutes for medium-boiled, and 8-10 minutes for hard-boiled eggs.
  4. Boil many eggs at the same time and keep them in the fridge to save some time in the mornings.
