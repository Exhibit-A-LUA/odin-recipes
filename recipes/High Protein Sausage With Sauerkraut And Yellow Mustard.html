High Protein Sausage with Sauerkraut and Yellow Mustard

Description

Healthy fast food at its best. Buy high-quality smoked sausage and pick any meat of preference: chicken, pork, beef, or perhaps a really good lamb sausage.

Ingredients

  • 1 tsp butter
  • 230 (g) smoked sausage (>90% meat)
  • 120 ml sauerkraut
  • 2 tsp yellow mustard
  • 2 tsp fresh parsley, chopped (optional)

Instructions

  1. Cut the sausages in half lengthwise so they will cook faster.
  2. Heat up a frying pan with butter and place sausages in pan once the butter has melted.
  3. Alternatively, you can grill them for a couple of minutes on both sides.
  4. Fry or grill until browned.
  5. Serve together with sauerkraut and mustard.
  6. Garnish with parsley for a splash of colour

Serving suggestions

  • If you would like to add some more fat to this dish it goes perfectly with mayonnaise.
  • Why not mix it with some tomato paste for a nice ketchup vibe.
