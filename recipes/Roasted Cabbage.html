Description

Roasted Cabbage

Ingredients

  • 450 (g) green cabbage
  • ½ tbsp olive oil
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Split the green cabbage into wedges and remove the thick stem in the middle.
  3. Cut slices — less than 1-inch (2.5 cm) wide — and place them on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper, or in a large baking dish.
  4. Season with salt and pepper and drizzle with olive oil.
  5. Bake for 20 minutes or until the cabbage is roasted.
