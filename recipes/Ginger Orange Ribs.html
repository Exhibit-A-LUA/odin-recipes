Description

Ginger Orange Ribs

Ingredients

  • 1.5 kg spare ribs
  • 1 orange, juice and zest
  • 60 ml tamari soy sauce
  • 3 tbsp ground ginger
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp ground black pepper

Instructions

  1. Mix all of the ingredients for the marinade in a small bowl.
  2. Set aside.
  3. Place the ribs in a sealable plastic bag, and pour the marinade over, coating the ribs completely.
  4. Let the meat marinate in the fridge for 24 hours.
  5. Pre-heat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  6. Remove the ribs from the marinade and season with salt on both sides.
  7. Pour the marinade into the baking dish.
  8. Bake for 1.5-2 hours depending on the size of the meat.
  9. Let the ribs cool on a cutting board.
  10. Slice along with the rib bones.
