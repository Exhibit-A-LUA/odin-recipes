Keto Skillet Lasagna

Description

All the flavours you love from a traditional lasagna, but without the Carb-heavy pasta and the time-consuming assembly. Our quick keto lasagna will become your new week-night favourite

Ingredients

Meat and cabbage mix

  • 280 (g) green cabbage, shredded
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 (110 g) yellow onion, finely chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 900 (g) ground beef or ground turkey
  • 1½ tsp salt
  • ½ tsp ground black pepper
  • 1 tbsp dried basil
  • 1 tbsp dried oregano
  • 240 ml (250 g) crushed tomatoes

Cheese sauce

  • 180 ml heavy whipping cream
  • 85 (g) (90 ml) cream cheese
  • 1 garlic clove, minced
  • salt and ground black pepper
  • 140 (g) (290 ml) shredded cheese
  • 2 tbsp fresh basil as garnish (optional)

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 400ºF (200ºC).
  2. Shred the cabbage by cutting the cabbage head in half and slicing it with a sharp knife into thin cabbage noodles.
  3. Heat a large frying pan or ovenproof skillet on medium-high heat.
  4. Add the cabbage and a splash of water.
  5. Toss it around with a tong and cook until the cabbage is soft.
  6. Remove from the pan and drain.
  7. Return the same pan to the stove and add the olive oil.
  8. When hot, add garlic and onion.
  9. Fry until it starts to get soft and translucent.
  10. Add the ground meat to the pan.
  11. Fry while breaking the meat up using a spatula.
  12. Season with salt, ground black pepper, basil, and oregano.
  13. Add the crushed tomatoes and the cooked cabbage when the meat starts to get brown.
  14. Give it a good mix and let it simmer for a couple of minutes while you prepare the cheese sauce.
  15. Mix the heavy whipping cream, cream cheese, garlic, salt, and pepper in a bowl until smooth.
  16. Pour it on top of the meat and cabbage mixture.
  17. Sprinkle the cheese on top.
  18. Bake in the oven for 20 minutes or until the cheese has a golden colour
  19. Let it cool a bit before serving.
  20. Garnish with fresh basil.

Tips

  • If you don't have an ovenproof skillet or frying pan, you can transfer the meat and cabbage mixture to a baking dish and top with the sauce and cheese before baking it in the oven.
  • If you want a more traditional lasagna, you can boil whole cabbage leaves and use them instead of pasta—layering the cabbage, meat mixture, and cream sauce.
  • Serve with a crisp green salad.
