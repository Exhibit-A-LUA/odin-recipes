Eggless No-Bake Mango Cheesecake

Description

Eggless No-Bake Mango Cheesecake

Ingredients

For the Crust

  • 1.5 cups Graham cracker crumbs or digestive biscuits - 2 cups for a thicker crust,
  • 75 gm Unsalted butter, melted (about 1/3 of a cup)

For the Cheesecake

  • 250 gm Philadelphia Cream Cheese
  • 170 gm Cream (I used Nestle)
  • 1/2 cup Sugar
  • 3/4 cup Mango puree
  • 1 Tbsp Gelatine
  • 1/4 cup Hot Water

Instructions

  1. Whizz the Graham crackers in a food processor.
  2. Add melted butter and whizz again until the mixture comes together.
  3. Press into the bottom of an 8" spring-form pan that's lined with aluminium foil or use individual glasses.
  4. Refrigerate while preparing the rest of the cheesecake.
  5. Mix the cream cheese, cream, and sugar in a bowl until well combined.
  6. You can do it by hand or use low speed on your Kitchen-aid.
  7. An electric beater is not needed.
  8. Add the gelatine to the hot water and stir till it completely dissolves.
  9. Add the mango puree and stir well.
  10. Keep half of this mixture aside and add the rest to the cream cheese mixture.
  11. Stir gently until well combined.
  12. Pour on top of the graham cracker base.
  13. Return to fridge for another 10-30 mins.
  14. Pour the mango mixture as the third layer.
  15. Do this gently so that the layers don't combine.
  16. If you use a spring-form pan, the top layer will be much thinner, as it should be.
  17. Refrigerate overnight and get ready to serve a yummy dessert at your party that can be prepared well in advance!
