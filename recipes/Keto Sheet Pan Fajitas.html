Keto Sheet Pan Fajitas

Description

Keto sheet pan meals are amazingly easy and require minimal clean-up. Delicious chicken fajitas are no exception. Just slice, mix, spread, and into the oven it goes.

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp chilli powder
  • 1 tbsp paprika powder
  • 2 tsp dried oregano
  • 2 tsp ground cumin
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 650 g chicken breasts, sliced into strips
  • 1 (120 g) sliced red bell pepper
  • 1 (110 g) yellow onion, sliced
  • 2 garlic cloves minced
  • 60 ml olive oil
  • 2 tbsp lime juice
  • 28 g (425 ml) fresh cilantro, for garnish

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Mix the chilli powder, paprika, oregano, cumin, and salt in a small bowl and set aside.
  3. Put the chicken strips, bell peppers, onion, garlic, oil, and lime juice in a large bowl and mix to distribute the oil and lime juice thoroughly.
  4. Sprinkle in the spice mixture and toss to coat the chicken and vegetables.
  5. Spread the seasoned chicken and veggies on the sheet pan in a single layer.
  6. Bake for 20-25 minutes, stirring halfway through the baking time until the chicken is cooked through and tender.
  7. If you enjoy a little char on your fajitas, you can place the pan under the broiler for 3 to 5 minutes at the end before serving.
  8. Garnish with fresh cilantro and serve.

Tips

  • Refrigerate leftovers for up to 5 days.
