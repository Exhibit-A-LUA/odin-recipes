Description

Pie Crust

Ingredients

  • 55 g unsalted butter, melted
  • 350 ml (170 g) almond flour
  • 120 ml (60 g) hazelnut flour or almond flour
  • 60 ml (55 g) erythritol
  • ¼ tsp ground cinnamon
  • ¼ tsp vanilla extract
  • ¼ tsp salt

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Add the butter, almond flour, hazelnut flour, sweetener, cinnamon, vanilla, and salt to a medium-sized bowl.
  3. Mix together until crumbly, and well combined.
  4. Evenly press the crust mixture over the bottom and sides of a 9" (23 cm), un-greased pie plate.
  5. Set aside.
