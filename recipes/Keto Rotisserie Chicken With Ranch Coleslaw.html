Keto Rotisserie Chicken With Ranch Coleslaw

Description

This is the perfect meal for busy week-nights that the whole family will love. In less than 10 minutes you'll have a healthy, low Carb, high protein meal on the table. No cooking required!

Ingredients

  • 170 (g) rotisserie chicken (meat)
  • 85 (g) green cabbage, shredded
  • 2 tbsp shredded carrots
  • 2 tbsp mayonnaise
  • 2 tbsp Greek yogurt (4% fat)
  • 1 tsp ranch seasoning
  • salt to taste

Mayonnaise

  • see Mayonnaise Recipe

Instructions

  1. Mix the ingredients for the coleslaw in a bowl.
  2. Place the chicken and coleslaw on a plate and serve.
