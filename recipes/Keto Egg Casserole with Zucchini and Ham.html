Keto Egg Casserole with Zucchini and Ham

Description

Keto Egg Casserole with Zucchini and Ham

Ingredients

  • 450 (g) zucchini, diced (about 0.25 inches or 0.5 centimetre)
  • 6 large eggs
  • 60 ml whole milk
  • 55 (g) smoked deli ham, diced
  • 110 (g) (110 ml) cream cheese, softened
  • 1 (15 g) scallion, sliced
  • 240 ml (110 g) cheddar cheese, shredded
  • salt and pepper, to taste
  • 1 tbsp butter, for greasing (optional)

Instructions

  1. Pre-heat oven to 350° F (180° C).
  2. Set aside a lightly greased, 9"x 9" (23 x 23 cm) baking dish.
  3. Whisk the eggs and milk together.
  4. Add the softened cream cheese.
  5. Small lumps of cream cheese in the mix are ok!
  6. Add the ham, spring onion, zucchini, and half the amount of shredded cheese.
  7. Add salt and pepper to taste.
  8. Stir and pour into a greased baking dish.
  9. Cover with the remaining shredded cheese.
  10. Bake for 20-30 minutes, or until the middle is set.
  11. Set aside to cool for 10 minutes prior to serving.

Tips

  • To quickly bring the cream cheese to room temperature, cut into 1" (2.5 cm) squares, separate, and rest on the counter-top for 10 minutes.
  • Alternatively, place the whole cream cheese block on the counter-top, and rest for 60 minutes.

Zucchini

  • The zucchini will be more al dente in this recipe.
  • If you prefer your zucchini softer, you can saute your diced zucchini in a pan for a few minutes first.
  • This will also release some water from the vegetable, making your casserole firmer, and less moist.
