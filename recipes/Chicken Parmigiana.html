Chicken Parmigiana

Ingredients

  • 2 large, skinless chicken breasts, halved through the middle
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 75g breadcrumb
  • 75g parmesan, grated
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 garlic cloves, crushed
  • half a 690ml jar passata
  • 1 tsp caster sugar
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • half a 125g ball light mozzarella, torn

Instructions

  1. Halve 2 large skinless chicken breasts through the middle.
  2. Place the 4 pieces between cling film sheets and bash out with a rolling pin until they are the thickness of a coin.
  3. Dip in 2 beaten eggs, then 75g breadcrumbs, mixed with half of the 75g grated parmesan.
  4. Set aside on a plate in the fridge while you make the sauce.
  5. Heat 1 tbsp olive oil and cook 2 crushed garlic cloves for 1 min.
  6. Tip in half a 690ml jar passata, 1 tsp caster sugar and 1 tsp dried oregano.
  7. Season and simmer for 5-10 mins.
  8. Heat grill to High and cook the chicken for 5 mins each side, then remove.
  9. Pour the tomato sauce into a shallow ovenproof dish and top with the chicken.
  10. Scatter over torn pieces of half a 125g ball light mozzarella, and the remaining grated parmesan.
  11. Grill for 3-4 mins until the cheese has melted and the sauce is bubbling.
  12. Serve with vegetables or salad and some pasta or potatoes, if you like.
