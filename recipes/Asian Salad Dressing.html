Description

Asian Salad Dressing

Ingredients

  • 2 (30 g) scallions, thinly sliced
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tbsp fresh ginger, grated
  • 80 ml olive oil
  • 120 ml tamari soy sauce
  • 60 ml rice vinegar
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil

Instructions

  1. In a jar with a tight-fitting lid, combine the scallions, garlic, ginger, olive oil, tamari soy sauce, rice vinegar, and sesame oil.
  2. Close the lid tightly and shake vigorously to make the dressing.
  3. You can also use a small bowl and a whisk.
