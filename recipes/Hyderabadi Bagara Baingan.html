Hyderabadi Bagara Baingan

Description

Hyderabadi Bagara Baingan

Ingredients

Roast Ground Paste

  • 1/8 tsp Fenugreek seeds
  • 1 tsp Cumin
  • 1 tsp Coriander Seeds
  • 2 tbsp Groundnuts
  • 1 tbsp Sesame seeds
  • 1 tsp Poppy Seeds
  • 2 tbsp Grated coconut

Fry

  • 250 (g) Brinjal

Paste

  • 1 small onion
  • 1 medium Tomatoes
  • 1 tsp Garlic Ginger paste
  • 2 tbsp Tamarind pulp

Cook

  • 2 Bay leaves
  • ½ tsp Mustard Seeds
  • 3 Green Chillies
  • 5 Curry Leaves
  • ½ tsp Coriander powder
  • ¼ tsp Cumin powder
  • ¼ tsp Turmeric
  • ¾ tsp Red chilli powder
  • 1 cup Water
  • 1 tsp Salt

Garnish

  • Coriander Leaves

Instructions

  1. Add a tsp oil and Roast spices.
  2. Sauté 2 minutes or till they slightly change colour Remove to a blender and make Smooth paste.
  3. Add water as required
  4. Wash the brinjals thoroughly.
  5. Dry them and slit down middle 80% turn and slit down middle 80%
  6. Fry in the oil till 70% done, and keep aside
  7. Sauté bay leaves, mustard seeds fry till pop
  8. Add slit green chillies, Curry Leaves, fry till chillies are wilted.
  9. Add ginger garlic paste and sauté till raw smell goes off
  10. Add onion paste, sauté till oil separates
  11. Add tomato paste, sauté till the gravy thickens
  12. Add the ground paste, sauté till oil separates
  13. Add the coriander powder, cumin powder, turmeric, red chilli powder, tamarind pulp, water and salt.
  14. Add the fried brinjals and cook for 10 more minutes with lid
  15. Garnish with coriander leaves
