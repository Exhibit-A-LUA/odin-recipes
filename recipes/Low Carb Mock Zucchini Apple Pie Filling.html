Low Carb Mock Zucchini Apple Pie Filling

Description

The flavours of apple in zucchini? Yes, it really tastes delicious! The secret is simmering the zucchini in spices and a little apple-sauce so that it takes on the flavour of apples. Serve this as a simple dessert or use it to make our cream-filled crepes.

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 200 g zucchini, peeled and chopped
  • 120 ml water
  • 55 g unsweetened apple sauce
  • 2 tbsp erythritol
  • 1½ tsp apple pie spice
  • ¼ tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1⁄8 tsp salt
  • 50 g pecans or walnuts
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Use a 1-quart saucepan to melt the butter on medium heat.
  2. Add the zucchini and stir until coated with butter.
  3. Add the water, apple-sauce, sweetener, and spices and simmer for 15-20 minutes on low heat until the zucchini is tender and the mixture creates a thick sauce.
  4. Remove from heat and stir in the nuts and vanilla.

Tips

  • Serve with a dollop of freshly whipped cream or a scoop of vanilla ice cream.
  • Roast the pecans before adding them to the zucchini mixture for an even warmer flavour
  • Apple pie spice is sold as a pre-made mix, but you can also create your own blend by experimenting with different amounts of your favourite fall spices.
  • Traditional apple pie spice is made with warm ground spices, including cinnamon, cloves, ginger, nutmeg, allspice, and cardamom.
