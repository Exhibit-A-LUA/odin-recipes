Smashed Beef Burger

Description

When it comes to a fast, tasty, and healthy you can't beat a classic burger. Effortless preparation, plenty of protein, and so easy to mix 'n' match. Wrap it in lettuce instead of a bun, or just eat it with a knife and fork. Create your version of the perfect keto burger by adding cheese, pickles, and sugar-free sauce.

Ingredients

  • 450 (g) ground beef, preferably chuck
  • salt or ground black pepper

Instructions

  1. Divide the ground meat into two balls per serving, about 4 oz (110 (g) each).
  2. Heat up a large frying pan on high heat.
  3. When the pan is smoking hot, place the meatballs in the pan and press them down with a grill press, spatula, or using the bottom of a glass, until less than 0.5" (1 cm thick).
  4. Season generously with salt and pepper.
  5. Fry for about 2 minutes on each side or until the burgers have a nice brown crust.

Tips

  • Love cheese? After flipping the burger, melt a piece of cheddar cheese over top of the browned side.
  • Why not season your burgers with smokey chipotle chilli and garlic powder?
  • Or mix a dollop of Dijon mustard and herbs to the ground beef before rolling them into balls.
