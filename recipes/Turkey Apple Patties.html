Turkey Apple Patties

Description

These protein-packed turkey apple patties will keep you satisfied for hours.

Ingredients

  • 325 g ground turkey
  • ½ (75 g) apple, peeled and diced
  • ½ tsp garlic powder
  • ½ tsp dried sage
  • ½ tsp paprika powder
  • salt and ground black pepper
  • 1 tbsp olive oil

Instructions

On a pan method

  1. Mix the ground turkey, apple, garlic, sage, paprika powder, salt, and pepper in a bowl.
  2. Form into patties, two per serving.
  3. Fry the patties in a skillet with the olive oil for 4-5 minutes on each side, over medium-high heat until cooked through.

Air fryer method

  1. Mix the ground turkey, apple, garlic, sage, paprika powder, salt, and pepper in a bowl.
  2. Form into patties, two per serving.
  3. Preheat the air fryer to 400°F (200°C).
  4. Spray, or wipe, the air fryer basket with coconut oil spray.
  5. Add the turkey patties and air fry for 8-10 minutes, flipping them halfway, until cooked.

Tips

  • Feel free to add other seasonings to the ground meat, such as onion powder, and/or ground fennel seed.
  • Speed up the cooking process by making the turkey patties thinner.
