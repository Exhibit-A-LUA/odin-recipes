Keto Thousand Island Dressing

Description

Rethink this old standby without the sugar and you are in for an even tastier dressing. Still offering a kick of heat—still sporting little chunks of dill pickles. Just like mom used to make, only better!

Ingredients

  • 240 ml mayonnaise
  • 240 ml crème fraîche or sour cream
  • 60 ml mild chili sauce
  • 1 (30 g) shallot, finely chopped
  • ½ (60 g) red bell pepper, finely chopped
  • 60 ml dill pickles, finely chopped
  • 1 tsp Tabasco
  • salt and pepper to taste

Mayonnaise

  • see Mayonnaise Recipe

Instructions

  1. Mix all ingredients thoroughly in a bowl.
  2. Let sit in the refrigerator for at least 15 minutes to let the flavours develop.
  3. Skip (or reduce) the Tabasco if you're using a hot chilli sauce.
  4. You can also use sugar-free ketchup or tomato paste if you want a milder sauce.

Tips

  • A perfect touch to complete that Reuben sandwich on Oopsie bread!
  • This dressing will keep for 4 to 5 days in the refrigerator; make some extra for dressing your salad later in the week.
