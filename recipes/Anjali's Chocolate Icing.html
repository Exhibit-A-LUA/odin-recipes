Description

Anjali's Chocolate Icing

Ingredients

  • 50 grams unsalted butter
  • 50 grams icing sugar
  • 2 tablespoons warm milk
  • 3 teaspoons cocoa powder

Instructions

  1. Put the butter into a thick bottomed pan.
  2. Place it on top of a double boiler.
  3. Or place the pan over a pan of hot water and turn the gas on low so that the water keep simmering.
  4. Now you have got to be fast.
  5. - mix the cocoa well in the hot milk
  6. - add the cocoa-milk mixture and the icing sugar to the butter and stir till just about mixed.
  7. - Take care not to let the butter melt too much.
  8. Spread the icing on the cake and decorate with whatever suits your style
