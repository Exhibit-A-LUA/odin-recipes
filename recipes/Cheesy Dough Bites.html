Cheesy Dough Bites

Description

Cheesy Dough Bites

Ingredients

  • 170 g (350 ml) mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • 2 tbsp cream cheese
  • 240 ml (110 g) almond flour
  • 28 g finely grated Parmesan cheese, divided
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 tsp white wine vinegar
  • ½ tsp baking powder

Instructions

  1. Melt the mozzarella and cream cheese in a non-stick pan over medium heat or in a bowl in the microwave. Stir until thoroughly combined.
  2. Add the almond flour, half the amount of Parmesan cheese, egg, white wine vinegar, and baking powder. Form a smooth dough by kneading it with your hands or using an electric mixer with dough hooks.
  3. Divide the dough into balls with your fingers or a small cookie scoop; they should be about 1-1/2 inches (4 cm) in diameter. Roll them in the remaining Parmesan cheese.
  4. Preheat the air fryer to 400°F (200°C). When it's hot, place the dough bites in the basket and cook for 8 minutes.
