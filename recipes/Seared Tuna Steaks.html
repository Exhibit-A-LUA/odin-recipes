Seared Tuna Steaks

Description

These meaty tuna steaks cook quickly on the stovetop. Their subtle flavour can be enhanced by a variety of seasonings, such as Asian or Mediterranean inspired.

Ingredients

  • 2 (400 g) fresh tuna fillets
  • salt and ground black pepper
  • 1 tbsp olive oil or coconut oil
  • 1 garlic clove, finely chopped
  • 1 tsp chilli flakes (optional)

Instructions

  1. Season the tuna fillets with salt and pepper (and any other spices you prefer) on both sides.
  2. Heat up a large frying pan on medium to high heat with oil.
  3. When hot, add the garlic.
  4. Cook garlic for 1 minute and then add the tuna steaks.
  5. Fry for about 2 minutes on each side for medium-rare.
  6. Tuna is low in fat so it will get dry if you overcook.
  7. Slice and serve right away.

Spice it up

  • Like Asian flavours? Add thinly sliced ginger, a splash of tamari soy, and sesame oil to the pan together with the tuna. Garnish with cilantro and lime wedges.
  • Add lemon juice, chopped fresh herbs like basil, thyme, or oregano for a Mediterranean vibe.
