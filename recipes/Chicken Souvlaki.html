Chicken Souvlaki with Yogurt Dip

Description

Chicken Souvlaki

Ingredients

Chicken Souvlaki Marinade

  • 1 tsp coriander, ground
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp paprika powder
  • 1 large garlic clove, minced
  • 1 tsp ground black pepper
  • 2 tsp lemon juice
  • 2 tsp salt
  • 3 tbsp extra virgin olive oil

Chicken Souvlaki

  • 800 (g) chicken breasts, cut into cubes
  • 1 (110 g) red onion, cut into wedges
  • 1 (120 g) red bell pepper, cut into squares

Yogurt Dip

  • see Yoghurt Dip Recipe

Instructions

  1. Thoroughly mix all the marinade ingredients in a bowl.
  2. Add the chicken, onion, and red peppers to the bowl and mix to coat well.
  3. Cover and refrigerate for a minimum of 30 minutes or overnight.
  4. Meanwhile, prepare the yogurt dip by mixing all the dip ingredients. Season to taste and set aside in the fridge.
  5. Preheat the grill/barbecue (or oven) with the lid down until the temperature reaches 350째F (180째C).
  6. Thread the marinated chicken and vegetables alternately onto your skewers.
  7. Grill for five to seven minutes until they are just browned.
  8. Turn over and grill for another 5-7 minutes.
  9. If you are using the oven bake for 20 minutes or until the internal temperature of the chicken reaches 175째F (80째C).
  10. Serve the skewers as soon as they are cooked with the yogurt dip and lemon wedges.

Tips

  • The yogurt dip will keep covered in the fridge for up to two days.
  • Feel free to substitute the dip with aioli or tzatziki.
  • This dish is delicious with a traditional greek salad.
