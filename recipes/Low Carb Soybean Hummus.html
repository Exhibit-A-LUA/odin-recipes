Low Carb Soybean Hummus

Description

This awesome hummus is made with edamame beans (Soybeans) instead of chickpeas. Not only is it lower in carbs, but it's also way higher in protein. The perfect dairy-free condiment that tastes great with any grilled meat, fish, or vegetarian option.

Ingredients

  • 230 (g) (350 ml) soybean, cooked without the pods
  • 3 tbsp water
  • 1 garlic clove, minced
  • 2 tbsp tahini (sesame paste)
  • Â½ lemon, juice
  • salt and ground black pepper

Instructions

  1. Put all ingredients for the hummus in a food processor and mix until smooth.
  2. Alternatively, put the ingredients in a bowl and use a hand mixer.
  3. Taste and add more salt or water if needed.
  4. Enjoy!
