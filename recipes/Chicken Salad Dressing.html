Chicken Salad Dressing

Description

Chicken Salad Dressing

Ingredients

  • 120 ml (90 g) Greek yoghurt (4% fat)
  • 60 ml mayonnaise
  • 1 tsp lemon zest
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tsp Dijon mustard
  • ½ tsp salt
  • ¼ tsp ground black pepper

Instructions

  1. Combine the Greek yoghurt, mayonnaise, lemon zest and juice, Dijon mustard, salt and black pepper in small bowl.
