Peanut Butter Cookies

Description

Peanut Butter Cookies - Satisfy your sweet tooth and your low Carb diet with our vegan low Carb peanut butter cookies. We've jazzed up our popular classic peanut butter cookie by making it egg-free.

Ingredients

  • 160 ml (75 g) almond flour
  • ¼ tsp baking soda
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 3 tbsp powdered erythritol
  • 120 ml peanut butter
  • 1 tbsp unsweetened almond milk

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 325°F (175°C)
  2. In a bowl, combine the almond flour, baking soda, salt, and powdered erythritol.
  3. Stir until combined.
  4. Stir in peanut butter and unsweetened almond milk to form a dough.
  5. Roll into 1-inch (2.5 cm) balls and place them on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  6. Lightly press a fork into the cookie balls to make an “x”.
  7. Make sure not to flatten the cookies completely, leave them about 1/2-inch (1cm) thick.
  8. Bake on the centre rack for 10 minutes.
  9. They will still be very soft, so let them cool for at least 30 minutes.

Tips

  • You could also use granulated erythritol. The dough gets a little more crumbly and falls apart more easily when making the cookies, but the cookies also get crispier.

Storing and freezing

  • These cookies keep for up to a week in the fridge in an airtight container.
  • You can also store them in an airtight ziplock bag and freeze for up to 3 months.
