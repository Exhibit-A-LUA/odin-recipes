Description

Almond Paste

Ingredients

  • 180 ml (85 g) almond flour
  • 2 tbsp powdered erythritol
  • 4 drops bitter almond extract
  • 4 tbsp heavy whipping cream

Instructions

  1. In a small food processor, blend the ingredients and some water into a smooth paste.
  2. Set aside.
