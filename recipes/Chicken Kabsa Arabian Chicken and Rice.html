Chicken Kabsa Arabian Chicken and Rice

Description

Chicken kabsa – Turn your next dinner into a feast with this Arabian style chicken and rice. Spiced grilled chicken and flavourful rice makes this the ultimate choice for any special occasion. Channel your inner chef, and cook up a storm for friends and family.

Ingredients

Chicken Kabsa

  • 2 tbsp oil or butter
  • 3 onions, sliced
  • 1 tbsp minced ginger (ginger paste)
  • 1 tbsp minced garlic (garlic paste)
  • 1 green chilli
  • 2 dried bay leaves
  • 6 cloves
  • 4 cardamom pods
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 2 tbsp tomato puree (tomato paste)
  • 1 pinch ground nutmeg (nutmeg powder)
  • 1/2 tsp ground black pepper (black pepper powder)
  • 1/4 tsp ground cumin (cumin powder)
  • 1/2 tsp ground coriander (coriander powder)
  • 3 medium carrots, thinly sliced
  • 200 (g) canned diced tomatoes (or 3 tomatoes chopped)
  • 2 chicken stock cubes
  • 1 1/2 kg whole chicken, cut into 6 pieces
  • 3 cups basmati rice, rinsed
  • 1/4 cup raisins
  • Water
  • Salt to taste
  • Raisins, to garnish (optional)
  • Slivered almonds, to garnish (optional)

Kabsa Spice Mix

  • see Kabsa Spice Mix Recipe

Instructions

Chicken Kabsa

  1. Heat oil in a large heavy bottomed pan over medium-high heat.
  2. Add onions, ginger, garlic and green chillies Sauté for 2-3 minutes or until the onions turn golden brown.
  3. Add bay leaves, cloves, cardamom pods and cinnamon stick.
  4. Fry for a minute.
  5. Stir in tomato purée.
  6. Add ground nutmeg, ground black pepper, ground cumin, ground coriander and kabsa spice.
  7. Season with salt.
  8. Fry the spices for a minute.
  9. Add carrots and diced tomatoes.
  10. Stir, and cook for 2 minutes.
  11. Add chicken cubes and chicken pieces.
  12. Brown chicken, turning occasionally, for about 30 minutes.
  13. Remove the chicken pieces from the pan. Set aside.
  14. Add rice and raisins.
  15. Pour in 4 cups water.
  16. Season with salt.
  17. Bring it to the boil.
  18. Reduce the heat, cover with lid, and simmer for 10-15 minutes.
  19. Preheat grill.
  20. Grill chicken for 10-15 minutes or until cooked through.
  21. Serve rice with grilled chicken.
