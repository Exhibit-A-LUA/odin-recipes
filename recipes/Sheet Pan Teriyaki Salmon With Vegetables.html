Sheet Pan Teriyaki Salmon With Vegetables

Description

Our healthy take on teriyaki – a popular Japanese dish with meat, marinated in a sweet, soy-based sauce served with rice. We have swapped the meat for salmon and decreased the carbs by replacing the sugar with semi-sweet coconut aminos and replaced rice with tender and vibrant veggies. This quick and easy one-pan meal is a keeper for sure!

Ingredients

Teriyaki Sauce (181.51g)

  • see Teriyaki Sauce Recipe

Salmon and vegetables

  • 800 g salmon, boneless fillets, cut into portions
  • 280 g (750 ml) broccoli florets, halved
  • 280 g Napa cabbage, cut into smaller pieces
  • 1 red chilli pepper, sliced

Instructions

Teriyaki Sauce (181.51g)

  • see Teriyaki Sauce Recipe

Salmon and vegetables

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (175°C).
  2. Set aside a large sheet pan.
  3. Prep the salmon and vegetables.
  4. Place the salmon on the sheet pan, surrounded by the broccoli and cabbage.
  5. Drizzle the teriyaki sauce over everything.
  6. Bake on the middle rack for 15-20 minutes, depending upon the thickness of the salmon.
  7. To serve, top with the red chilli slices.
  8. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

Tips

  • To add some more fat and flavour, combine mayonnaise with sriracha sauce to make a spicy dip.
  • You could also garnish the dish with some scallions, lime, and fresh cilantro for an even more impressive presentation.
  • If you can't find coconut aminos you could just add more tamari and/or add a little bit of honey for sweetness.
  • Balsamic vinegar would work nicely as well.
