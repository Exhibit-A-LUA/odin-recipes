Description

Cheesecake Crust

Ingredients

  • 350 ml (170 g) almond flour
  • 2 tbsp powdered erythritol
  • 3 tbsp salted butter, melted

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 325°F (160°C).
  2. Line the bottom of an 8" (23 cm) spring-form pan with parchment paper.
  3. In a medium-sized bowl, mix together the almond flour, sweetener, and melted butter.
  4. Press the mixture into the bottom of the prepared spring-form pan.
  5. Par-bake the crust for 6 to 8 minutes, until barely browned.
  6. Remove from the oven and set aside to cool.
