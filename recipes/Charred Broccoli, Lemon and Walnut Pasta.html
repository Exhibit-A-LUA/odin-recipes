Charred Broccoli, Lemon and Walnut Pasta

Ingredients

  • 1 head broccoli, cut into small florets and stalk cut into small pieces
  • 3 tsp olive oil
  • 150g penne or fusilli
  • 2 garlic cloves, crushed
  • 1 tbsp roughly chopped walnuts
  • pinch of chilli flakes
  • Â½ lemon, zested and juiced

Instructions

  1. Heat the grill to high.
  2. Put the broccoli on a baking tray and drizzle over 1 tsp of the oil.
  3. Season, and toss together.
  4. Grill for 8-10 mins, tossing around halfway through, until crispy and charred.
  5. Cook the pasta in salted water following pack instructions.
  6. Drain, reserving a cup of the cooking water.
  7. In a frying pan, heat the remaining 2 tsp oil over a medium heat, and fry the garlic, walnuts and chilli for 3-4 mins until golden.
  8. Tip in the pasta, broccoli, lemon zest and juice, reserving a little of the zest.
  9. Add a splash of the reserved cooking water and toss everything together to coat the pasta.
  10. Serve in warmed bowls with the remaining lemon zest scattered over.
