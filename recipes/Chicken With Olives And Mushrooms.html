Chicken With Olives And Mushrooms

Description

You'll enjoy delightfully full flavours from this simple low Carb dish. Chicken, olives, and mushrooms combine to make a rustic dinner that will please even the most discerning taste buds.

Ingredients

  • 450 (g) chicken breasts, bone-in with skin
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 28 (g) butter
  • 400 (g) (1.4 litres) mushrooms, sliced
  • 150 (g) (270 ml) pitted olives, chopped
  • 1 sprig fresh rosemary, finely chopped
  • 120 ml water

Instructions

  1. Season the chicken with salt and pepper on both sides.
  2. Fry the chicken breasts in a large frying pan with olive oil until browned on both sides over medium-high heat.
  3. Continue to cook the chicken breasts on a wire rack in the oven at 350 °F (180 °C) for 20 minutes or until the meat is no longer pink, or the inner temperature of meat is 165°F (74°C).
  4. Let it rest for a couple of minutes before slicing the chicken to keep it juicy.
  5. Add the butter to the pan.
  6. First, sauté the mushrooms for a few minutes until soft and then add the olives.
  7. Season with salt and pepper and the finely chopped rosemary.
  8. Let the vegetables cook for a few more minutes, then add the water.
  9. Let the mixture simmer briefly so that all the flavours are well combined.
  10. Remove the pan from the heat.
  11. Remove the bones and slice the chicken.
  12. Serve with those deliciously tasty vegetables and olives.
