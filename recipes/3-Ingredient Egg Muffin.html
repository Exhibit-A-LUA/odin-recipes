3-Ingredient Egg Muffin

Description

This keto breakfast is a simple single-portion breakfast that you can make in minutes. Simply beat eggs, cheese, and ham and microwave in a buttered ramekin.

Ingredients

  • 1 tsp butter
  • 2 large eggs
  • 35 (g) smoked deli ham or deli turkey, chopped
  • 60 ml (30 g) cheddar cheese, grated
  • salt and ground black pepper, to taste

Instructions

  1. If not using a microwave, preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Grease one mug or ramekin per serving with butter.
  3. Beat the eggs with a fork in the mug.
  4. Season with salt and pepper.
  5. Add the chopped ham and grated cheese and mix well.
  6. Microwave on high for 90 seconds to 2 minutes, or cook in the oven for 15 minutes or until the cheese has melted and the eggs are firm.

Tips

  • Feel free to substitute the cheddar cheese or ham for any other cheese or deli meat you have in your fridge.
