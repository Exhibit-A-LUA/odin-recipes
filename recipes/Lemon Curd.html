Lemon Curd

Description

Lemon Curd

Ingredients

  • 3 egg yolks
  • 3 tbsp powdered erythritol
  • 1 tbsp lemon zest
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 28 (g) butter

Instructions

  1. Add the egg yolks to a saucepan with powdered erythritol, lemon zest, lemon juice, and vanilla extract.
  2. Combine with a rubber spatula and place the saucepan over low heat.
  3. Stir constantly.
  4. When the mixture starts thickening (after 4-5 minutes), turn off the heat and add the butter.
  5. Stir until the butter has melted and the lemon curd is smooth.
  6. Allow the curd to cool a little, then cover and chill in the fridge for at least 15 minutes.
  7. This will help thicken the curd.
