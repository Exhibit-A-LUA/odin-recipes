Keto Chicken Salad With Creamy Chimichurri Dressing

Description

This keto meal is simple to make, healthy, and bursting with colour and flavour Originating in South America, chimichurri is made of finely chopped parsley, minced garlic, olive oil, oregano, and red wine vinegar. A real treat if you've never tried it! Either sauté or grill the chicken thighs, chef's choice!

Ingredients

Chimichurri Dressing

  • see Chimichurri Dressing Recipe

Chicken Thighs

  • 1 tbsp olive oil or coconut oil
  • 450 (g) boneless chicken thighs
  • salt and ground black pepper

Serving

  • 475 ml (110 g) leafy greens
  • 1 (200 g) avocado, sliced
  • ¼ (28 g) red onion, sliced
  • 1 lemon, cut into wedges (optional)
  • ½ red chilli pepper, sliced (optional)

Instructions

Chimichurri Dressing

  • see Chimichurri Dressing Recipe

Chicken Thighs

  1. Heat a large skillet with olive oil.
  2. Season the chicken thighs with salt and pepper.
  3. Add them to the hot pan and fry on both sides until completely cooked.
  4. It will take about 10-15 minutes depending on the thickness of the meat.

Serving

  1. While the chicken is frying, prepare the vegetables and arrange them on individual plates or a big serving platter.
  2. Place the chicken on top and serve with the dressing, a couple of lemon wedges, and chilli if you like to add a bit more heat to the dish.
