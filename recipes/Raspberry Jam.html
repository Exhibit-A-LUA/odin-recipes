Raspberry Jam

Description

Raspberry Jam

Ingredients

  • ½ tsp unflavoured powdered gelatin
  • 2 tsp cold water
  • 120 ml (70 g) frozen raspberries
  • ½ tbsp powdered erythritol
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • ½ tbsp chia seeds

Instructions

  1. Prepare the raspberry jam by combining the gelatin and water in a small bowl.
  2. Allow gelatin to bloom.
  3. Add the remaining raspberry-jam ingredients to a small saucepan.
  4. Heat over medium-low heat.
  5. Stir continuously, mashing the raspberries as your stir.
  6. Cook for 3-4 minutes, then add the gelatin.
  7. Stir the mixture to dissolve gelatin.
  8. Remove from heat and allow to come to room temperature.
