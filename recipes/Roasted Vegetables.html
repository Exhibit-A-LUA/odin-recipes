Description

Roasted Vegetables

Ingredients

  • 220 (g) Brussels sprouts
  • 85 (g) cherry tomatoes
  • 85 (g) (290 ml) mushrooms
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste
  • 1 tsp dried rosemary or dried thyme
  • ½ tbsp olive oil

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Rinse and trim the vegetables and peel the outer layer of the Brussels sprouts if needed.
  3. Chop the vegetables, so they're roughly the same size.
  4. Place them in a baking dish.
  5. Season with salt, pepper, rosemary, and/or thyme.
  6. Drizzle with olive oil and mix.
  7. Bake for 20 minutes or until the vegetables have softened and turned a nice colour
