Mustard Mayonnaise

Description

Mustard Mayonnaise

Ingredients

  • 180 ml mayonnaise
  • 1 tbsp Dijon mustard
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste

Mayonnaise

  • see Mayonnaise Recipe

Instructions

  1. Mix the mayo and mustard in a small bowl.
  2. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
