Description

Garlic Thyme Butter

Ingredients

  • 140 (g) butter, softened
  • 1 garlic clove, minced
  • 1 tbsp fresh thyme, finely chopped

Instructions

  1. Stir the butter, garlic, and thyme together in a bowl.
  2. Taste and add salt if needed.
