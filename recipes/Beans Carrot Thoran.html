Beans Carrot Thoran

Description

Beans Carrot Thoran - Serves 8

Ingredients

Vegetables

  • 1 Cup Beans
  • 1 Cup Carrot
  • 1 Medium Onion chopped small
  • 1 green chilli
  • 3 cloves Garlic
  • ½ tsp Jeera
  • 3 tbsp Coconut Oil
  • Urad daal
  • ½ tsp Red chilli powder
  • Salt
  • Mustard Seeds
  • 2 Whole Red Chillies
  • Curry leaves

Paste

  • ½ Cup coconut grated
  • 1 green chilli
  • ¼ tsp turmeric

Prep

  • Slice green chilli into small pieces.
  • Soak tamarind in water
  • Mixie- Coarsely Grind with NO water, Coconut, Green Chillies, Turmeric

Instructions

  1. In 2 tbsp coconut oil add mustard seed on medium flame.
  2. Add urad daal green chillies and whole Red Chillies, fry, add onions and curry leaves.
  3. When onions are soft add the salt and add the beans and carrots.
  4. Mix through, add a little water and cover and cook.
  5. Check and add some salt.
  6. Keep opening a couple of times and stir, until well cooked.
  7. Add the coconut mixture, mix through add curry leaves and cover and cook for a couple of minutes.
  8. Add 1 tbsp coconut oil and stir though for flavour, cover the lid and turn off the heat.
