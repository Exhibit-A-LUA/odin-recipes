Ranch Seasoning

Description

Our sugar-free Ranch spice mix is full of flavour! This recipe makes a large batch that you can always have ready to use in dressings, dips, or marinades. Also, a jar of spice mix is a wonderful gift for family and friends.

Ingredients

  • 180 ml (19 g) dried parsley
  • 80 ml (50 g) garlic powder
  • 80 ml (38 g) onion powder
  • 1 tbsp dried dill
  • 1 tbsp dried chives
  • Â½ tbsp ground black pepper

Instructions

  1. Mix all the ingredients well and pour into a jar with a tight-fitting lid.

Tips

  • Making your own spice mixes has many benefits. There are no unwanted additives, you can adjust the flavour to your preferences, and save money!
  • If you prefer, blend all ingredients in a blender or spice grinder for a more powdery mix and a creamier end result when added to recipes.

Using the dry mix in recipes

  • Be sure to mix the dry ingredients well before measuring them into recipes as the ingredients will settle when stored.
  • If you love the taste of Ranch, you might enjoy trying the dry seasoning mix not only in traditional dips and dressings but also in marinades, soups, casseroles.
  • It's perfect for seasoning veggies and sprinkling over eggs or into omelets.
  • Let your imagination be your guide!
  • We have left out the salt in this spice mix to make it more versatile so you can adjust the amount of salt depending on what you pair it with.
