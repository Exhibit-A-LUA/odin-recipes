No-tato Salad

Description

No-tato Salad - Like potato salad, but without potatoes.

Ingredients

  • 60 ml sour cream
  • 2 tbsp mayonnaise
  • 2 tsp Dijon mustard
  • 1 tbsp fresh chives or dried chives (save some for garnish)
  • 280 g zucchini, diced
  • 85 g (200 ml) celery stalks, diced
  • salt and ground black pepper

Mayonnaise

  • see Mayonnaise Recipe

Instructions

  1. Combine all the ingredients for the no-tato salad in a bowl.
  2. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  3. Sprinkle with chives to garnish.
