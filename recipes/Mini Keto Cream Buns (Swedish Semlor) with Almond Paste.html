Mini Keto Cream Buns (Swedish Semlor) with Almond Paste

Description

In Sweden it's tradition every year on "Fat Tuesday," a.k.a Mardis Gras, to eat this irresistible pastry! A soft cardamom-flavoured bun is filled with delicious rich almond paste and heavy whipped cream. Enjoy this keto version of semlor for a Swedish "fika" all year round!

Ingredients

Cardamom Buns

  • see Cardamom Buns Recipe

Almond Paste

  • see Almond Paste Recipe

Serving

  • 240 ml heavy whipping cream, whipped
  • 1 tbsp powdered erythritol

Instructions

Cardamom Buns

  • see Cardamom Buns Recipe

Almond Paste

  • see Almond Paste Recipe

Serving

  1. Take a sharp knife and cut out the top on each bun.
  2. Make a small pit in the middle and fill with the almond paste mixture.
  3. Spread out or pipe heavy whipped cream on top and put the lid on.
  4. Dust with powdered erythritol using a small sieve or a tea strainer for the final touch.
