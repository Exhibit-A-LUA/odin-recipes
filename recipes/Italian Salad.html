Description

Italian Salad

Ingredients

  • 240 ml (55 g) leafy greens, chopped or Romaine lettuce
  • 28 g (85 ml) shredded Parmesan cheese

Instructions

  1. Mix all ingredients together.
  2. Sprinkle cheese amongst.
