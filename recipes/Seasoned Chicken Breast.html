Description

Seasoned Chicken Breast

Ingredients

  • 2 (450 g) chicken breasts 8 oz (230 g) each
  • salt and ground black pepper
  • 2 tsp Italian seasoning or taco seasoning or garam masala seasoning (optional)
  • 1 tbsp butter or olive oil

Instructions

  1. To speed up the cooking process, cut the chicken breasts lengthwise into thin cutlets.
  2. Season both sides of the chicken with salt, ground black pepper, and seasoning of choice.
  3. Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat.
  4. Add the butter or oil to the skillet when hot.
  5. Carefully place the chicken breasts in the skillet and cook for about 4 minutes on each side.
  6. The chicken is ready to serve when it's no longer pink.

Tips

  • When the chicken is almost cooked, add some heavy cream and more seasoning to the skillet to make a quick, delicious sauce.
  • Spice it up! Try oregano, garlic, and rosemary for a Greek twist; Tex-Mex spice for some heat; or tarragon and herbes de Provence for a French flair.
