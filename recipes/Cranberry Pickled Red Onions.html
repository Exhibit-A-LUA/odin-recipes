Cranberry Pickled Red Onions

Ingredients

  • 1 (110 g) red onion, thinly sliced
  • 120 ml fresh or frozen cranberries or loganberries
  • 120 ml apple cider vinegar
  • 120 ml water
  • Â½ tsp salt

Instructions

  1. In a medium-sized bowl, mix together red onions, cranberries, vinegar, water, and salt.
  2. Let marinate in the refrigerator for at least one day before serving.
