Tuna Burgers

Description

Tuna Burgers

Ingredients

  • 280 g canned tuna in water, drained
  • 2 tbsp mayonnaise
  • 85 g (180 ml) shredded cheese
  • 1 large egg
  • ½ tbsp garlic powder
  • ½ tbsp onion powder
  • ¼ tsp salt
  • ¼ tsp ground black pepper
  • 1 tbsp olive oil, for frying

Instructions

  1. Drain and squeeze out any excess liquid from the canned tuna.
  2. Mix it with all the other ingredients (except for the olive oil) in a medium-sized bowl.
  3. Shape the mixture into small patties, about 2-inches (5 cm) wide and 2/5-inch (1 cm) thick.
  4. In a large frying pan over medium heat, add enough oil to coat the base of the pan.
  5. Once the oil is hot add the tuna patties to the pan.
  6. To avoid overcrowding, you may need to fry them in batches.
  7. Flatten them by lightly pressing them down with a spatula.
  8. Cook for 3-4 minutes per side, until golden brown and crispy.
