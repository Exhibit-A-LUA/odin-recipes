Audrey's Chocolate Cake

Description

Audrey's Chocolate Cake

Ingredients

  • 110 (g) Dark Chocolate
  • 110 (g) Unsalted Butter
  • 110 (g) Castor Sugar
  • 110 (g) Ground Almonds (Audrey uses more ... 140g)
  • 4 Large Egg Yolks
  • 4 Large Egg Whites

Instructions

  1. Melt butter and chocolate in basin over hot water until blended
  2. Cool over cold water whilst preparing other ingredients
  3. Put sugar and ground almonds into mixing bowl and blend
  4. Beat in egg yolks
  5. Place egg whites in a separate bowl and whisk until stiff ("A firm snow")
  6. When chocolate mixture is cool, beat into sugar mixture
  7. Carefully fold in the whisked egg whites
  8. Pour into a greased and lined tin (20cm)
  9. Bake in a cool oven (300 F / 150 C) for 1 hour or until firm to touch
  10. Let cool in tin before turning out
