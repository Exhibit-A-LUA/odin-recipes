Mutton Pasanda Kebab

Ingredients

  • 550 gm mutton
  • 150 gm butter
  • 4 bread slices
  • 1 Â½ teaspoon ginger paste
  • 550 ml fresh cream
  • 3 teaspoon white pepper powder
  • 200 gm green papaya
  • 2 teaspoon garlic paste
  • salt as required

Instructions

  1. Rinse the mutton under cold running water and then pat dry.
  2. Meanwhile, take the papaya, peel and cut it into pieces.
  3. Put papaya pieces in a mixer grinder with little water and grind it to form a smooth paste.
  4. Now, take a large bowl and cut mutton into small pieces.
  5. Flatten it with a meat mallet and set aside.
  6. Take a small bowl and mix half of the ginger-garlic paste, papaya paste and salt.
  7. Apply this mixture on the mutton pieces so that the mutton is covered with it properly.
  8. Now, preserve it for an hour in the refrigerator.
  9. Now, take the butter and melt it in a frying pan over medium flame.
  10. Add remaining ginger-garlic paste and garam masala powder in it and fry it till it is brownish or golden in colour.
  11. Take the preserved mutton pieces and put them in the pan and let it fry.
  12. Turn the pieces to cook on both the sides, until brown or golden in colour.
  13. Take out the cooked mutton on a clean paper tissue to drain the access butter and set it aside.
  14. Put cream and white pepper powder in the above mixture, and cook till it is almost dry.
  15. Now cut the bread slices into strips and toast it till it is prepared.
  16. You can garnish the kebabs with these toasted bread fingers.
  17. Pasanda Kebab is ready to eat.
  18. Serve with green or mint chutney and ketchup.
