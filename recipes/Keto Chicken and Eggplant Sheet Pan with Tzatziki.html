Keto Chicken and Eggplant Sheet Pan with Tzatziki

Description

One pan, no fuss, no mess. This keto sheet pan meal combines juicy chicken, fresh veggies, and classic Greek flavours of feta cheese, olive oil, tomatoes and eggplant. Serve it with a generous dollop of tzatziki and it's sure to become a family week night favourite

Ingredients

Chicken and Vegetables

  • 900 (g) chicken thighs (bone-in with skin)
  • 400 (g) eggplant, cubed
  • 8 (140 g) cherry tomatoes

Mayonnaise Tzatziki Sauce

  • see Mayonnaise Tzatziki Sauce Recipe

Marinade for Chicken and Vegetables

  • see Marinade for Chicken and Vegetables Recipe

Serving

  • 120 ml (65 g) olives, pitted (preferably Kalamata)
  • 55 (g) (85 ml) feta cheese, crumbled
  • 60 ml (23 g) roughly chopped, fresh mint or fresh parsley (optional)

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 375째F (200째C).
  2. Line a large sheet pan with parchment paper.
  3. Place the chicken and eggplant into the marinade and toss them together to completely coat.
  4. Marinate for at least 10 minutes at room temperature, or in the refrigerator, for a couple of hours.
  5. Evenly spread the chicken, eggplant, and tomatoes on the lined sheet pan.
  6. Bake on the middle rack for 35 minutes, or until the skin is crispy and the internal temperature reads 165째F (74째C).
  7. Remove the baking tray from the oven.
  8. Scatter the olives around the chicken, sprinkle the feta cheese on top, and garnish with fresh mint or parsley.
  9. Serve with the tzatziki.

Tips

  • If you want the tomatoes to be a little firmer, you can put them in the oven for the last 10 minutes or add them fresh before serving.
  • To get the moisture out of the shredded cucumber, you can either squeeze it out using your clean hands or use a cheesecloth or a kitchen cloth.
