Asian Chicken and Veggie Salad

Description

In a salad rut? This Asian low Carb salad will fix that! This dish only requires chopping (the veggies) and shaking (the dressing), and cooked chicken! It's a great salad to make if you're meal prepping for the week, and it features bright Asian flavours to get you out of that afternoon slump.

Ingredients

Asian Chicken Salad

  • 110 (g) (550 ml) Romaine lettuce, shredded
  • 110 (g) Napa cabbage, shredded
  • 110 (g) (375 ml) red cabbage, shredded
  • 1 (120 g) red bell pepper, sliced
  • 55 (g) bean sprouts
  • 110 (g) cucumber, sliced
  • 450 (g) cooked chicken breast, cooked and shredded

Asian Salad Dressing

  • see Asian Salad Dressing Recipe

Asian Salad Toppings

  • 1 red chilli pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 tbsp fresh cilantro, leaves only (optional)
  • 1 tbsp sesame seeds

Instructions

  1. Combine the Romaine lettuce, Napa cabbage, and red cabbage, bell pepper, bean sprouts, cucumber, and shredded chicken in a large bowl.
  2. In a jar with a tight-fitting lid, combine the scallions, garlic, ginger, olive oil, tamari soy sauce, rice vinegar, and sesame oil.
  3. Close the lid tightly and shake vigorously to make the dressing.
  4. You can also use a small bowl and a whisk.
  5. Drizzle half the dressing over the salad and toss to combine.
  6. Garnish with red chilli pepper slices, cilantro leaves (if using), and sesame seeds.
  7. Serve with extra dressing on the side.

Tips

  • To shorten the prep time, you can use a food processor or a mandolin to shred the cabbage.
  • If you're making this in advance or prepping meals, don't add the dressing until ready to serve.
