Keto Meatloaf Muffins

Description

This fun keto recipe will have sceptics asking for more of these delicious meatloaf muffins. The versatility of this recipe allows you to customize the muffins to your unique taste. Don't forget the cheese!

Ingredients

  • 1 (110 g) yellow onion diced finely
  • 650 (g) ground beef or ground turkey
  • 2 lightly beaten large eggs
  • 2 tbsp Italian seasoning
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 120 ml (55 g) shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 60 ml grated Parmesan cheese (optional)

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350째F (180째C).
  2. Place the onion, meat, eggs, Italian seasoning, salt, and pepper in a large bowl.
  3. Mix all the ingredients with your hands and place a small handful of the meatloaf mixture into muffin trays (2 per serving).
  4. Press gently; otherwise, they will turn into meatballs.
  5. Cover with the grated cheese, and sprinkle with grated parmesan if desired.
  6. Cook in the oven for 20-30 minutes or until meat reaches 165째F (75째C).

Variations

  • These meatloaf muffins can be easily adapted to your taste preferences.
  • Feel free to add garlic, oregano, rosemary, or basil to your meatloaf mixture for a more Italian taste.
  • You can also make a Mexican version by adding coriander, cumin, chilli powder, capsicums, and/or peppers to the mixture.

Toppings

  • This recipe used mozzarella and parmesan cheese
  • Give your favourite cheese or blend a try
  • Bacon strips or slices work well too.

Stuffed muffins

  • Take this recipe to the next level by adding grated carrot, finely diced mushrooms, celery, grated zucchini, or chopped spinach inside your muffins!
