Description

Dijon Vinaigrette

Ingredients

  • ½ tbsp olive oil
  • ½ tbsp red wine vinegar
  • ½ tsp Dijon mustard
  • 1 tsp Italian seasoning

Instructions

  1. Whisk all the ingredients for the vinaigrette in a small bowl.
