Cardamom Buns

Description

Cardamom Buns

Ingredients

  • 2 large eggs
  • 110 (g) (110 ml) cream cheese, room tempered
  • 55 (g) butter, room tempered
  • 120 ml (65 g) powdered erythritol
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 1½ tsp ground cardamom (green)
  • 350 ml (170 g) almond flour
  • 3 tbsp whey protein isolate (unflavoured) or coconut flour
  • 1½ tbsp ground psyllium husk powder

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Combine eggs, cream cheese, butter, erythritol, baking powder, and cardamom in a large bowl and mix well.
  3. Add almond flour, whey protein powder, and psyllium husk powder to the same bowl.
  4. Stir until smooth.
  5. Let the dough rest for about 5-10 minutes, or until the psyllium husk has absorbed the liquid and the dough has a solid consistency.
  6. Shape into separate buns and place on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  7. Bake for about 15 minutes or until the buns are a nice golden colour.
  8. Use a toothpick or thin skewer to test that they're cooked in the middle - when a toothpick comes out with no dough on it.
  9. Remove from oven and let the buns rest until they have cooled down.

Tips

  • Do you want rounder buns? Then add double amount of fibre husk.
  • If you don't like the taste of psyllium, bake according to the original recipe.
  • The buns will be slightly flatter but still moist and delicious!
  • Brush the buns with beaten egg before baking for glossy buns.
