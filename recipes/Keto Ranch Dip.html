Keto Ranch Dip

Description

LOVE Ranch? Yes! This tangy version goes perfectly with salads, snacks, grilled meat, chicken, or veggies. Get your dip right with our quick, creamy keto ranch dip recipe, and you'll never mess with that bottled stuff again.

Ingredients

  • 240 ml mayonnaise or vegan mayonnaise
  • 120 ml sour cream
  • 2 tbsp ranch seasoning
  • salt to taste

Mayonnaise

  • see Mayonnaise Recipe

Instructions

  1. In a medium-sized bowl, combine the mayonnaise, sour cream, and ranch seasoning.
  2. Cover and refrigerate for at least 15 minutes to let the flavours develop.
  3. Serve as a dip or a salad dressing.

Tips

  • Want a thinner consistency for a ranch dressing? Swap out half of the sour cream with buttermilk, or use water to dilute it.
  • Plan ahead and mix up a big batch of our ranch seasoning.
  • That'll make it super easy to throw this dip together whenever you feel like it.
  • Why not give away a few jars?
  • It makes a great hostess gift!
