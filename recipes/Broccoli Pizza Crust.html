Broccoli Pizza Crust

Description

Have you heard? Green is the new colour in pizza these days! If you're already a fan of the better-known cauliflower crust, this cheesy broccoli version will bring some competition to the (kitchen) table. The method is similar, and this particular version is topped with all things green — asparagus, arugula, pesto, and scallions. Heaps of flavour, low carb, and great for sharing, too!

Ingredients

  • 450 g broccoli
  • 2 large eggs
  • 240 ml (110 g) mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • 120 ml freshly grated Parmesan cheese
  • ½ tsp garlic powder
  • ½ tsp salt
  • it¼ tsp ground black pepperem

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  3. Break the broccoli into florets, peel and roughly chop the stalk.
  4. Place the broccoli in a food processor and pulse until riced.
  5. Place the riced broccoli in a large bowl.
  6. Add the eggs, shredded mozzarella cheese, Parmesan cheese, garlic powder, salt, and pepper.
  7. Stir until well combined.
  8. Transfer the mixture to the prepared baking sheet and use a spatula to shape it into a 1/2-inch (approximately 1.25 cm) thick disc.
  9. Bake for 15-20 minutes until the crust is set and the edges start to brown.

Tips

  • It is easier to use mini mozzarella balls over tearing up a large one because the mini mozzarella balls release less moisture.

Special equipment

  • A food processor makes it much easier to rice the broccoli.
