Swedish Keto Gingerbread Cookies

Description

The smell of baking gingerbread will fill your kitchen with a Christmas spirit like few other recipes. Our version of keto gingerbread cookies is based on a traditional Swedish recipe that we ketofied for you. They are equally as fun to bake as they are delicious to eat. God Jul!

Ingredients

  • 120 ml water
  • 2 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 2 tsp ground ginger
  • 2 tsp ground cloves
  • 110 (g) butter
  • 300 ml (140 g) almond flour
  • 180 ml (160 g) erythritol
  • 120 ml (50 g) coconut flour
  • 1 tbsp ground psyllium husk powder
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 egg white

Instructions

  1. Mix the water and spices in a small saucepan.
  2. Bring to a boil.
  3. Remove from heat and add the butter.
  4. Stir until melted.
  5. The cinnamon will make the consistency a bit slimy.
  6. Mix all of the dry ingredients in a separate bowl.
  7. Add the spice mixture and egg white.
  8. Mix thoroughly and shape the dough into a ball.
  9. Cover with plastic wrap and refrigerate overnight to let the flavours mature.
  10. Preheat the oven to 300째F (150째C).
  11. Cut the dough in half and use a rolling pin to flatten the dough.
  12. Placing the dough between two pieces of parchment paper is the easiest.
  13. When flattened, the dough should be about 1/8 inch (3 mm) thick.
  14. Using cookie cutters, cut the gingerbread cookies into shapes.
  15. Use a spatula to carefully move them to a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  16. Bake the cookies for 7-9 minutes or until lightly browned.
  17. Lower the heat to 200째F (100째C) and continue to bake them for about 30 minutes or until crisp depending on the size and thickness of the cookies.
  18. Let the cookies cool completely on a cooling rack before serving or decorating them.

Tips

  • For a more traditional Swedish experience, we suggest serving these gingerbread cookies with a schmear of butter and some crumbled blue cheese.
  • It may sound like an odd combination, but it is a delicious treat and a sophisticated flavour combination for holiday hors d'oeuvre.
