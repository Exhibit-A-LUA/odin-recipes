Chipotle Mayonnaise

Description

Need a little extra spice in your life? We thought so. Liven up your fave dishes with this smokey, spicy chipotle mayo. Oh yeah!

Ingredients

  • 80 ml mayonnaise
  • 2 tsp tomato paste
  • ½ tsp chipotle powder
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste

Mayonnaise

  • see Mayonnaise Recipe

Instructions

  1. Mix the mayonnaise, tomato paste, and chipotle powder in a small bowl.
  2. Add additional seasoning to taste — more means a spicier sauce.
  3. Serve right away or let sit in the fridge for 30 minutes or more to allow the flavours to develop.

Tips

  • This is one of those condiments that can work with almost anything.
  • Try it on burgers or sandwiches, or as a dip with veggies.
  • It even makes a perfect but unexpected match as a dipping sauce with sushi rolls!
  • The tomato paste is primarily included for colour and can be excluded if you’re using chipotle paste instead of powder.
  • Keeps in the fridge for at least 5 days.
