Indian Paneer Tikka

Description

Indian Paneer Tikka

Ingredients

  • 200 grams paneer, cubed
  • 2 bell peppers, chopped into 1-inch cubes
  • ½ cup greek yogurt
  • 3 clove garlic, minced
  • A small piece ginger, minced
  • ½ tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp red chilli powder
  • ½ tsp cumin
  • ½ tsp garam masala
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • Salt, to taste
  • 1 tbsp oil

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 450 F.
  2. Line a baking sheet with parchment paper or a silicone baking sheet (I use this one).
  3. In a medium bowl, combine yogurt, garlic, ginger, turmeric, chili powder, cumin, garam masala, lemon juice, salt and oil.
  4. Adjust seasonings to taste, if needed.
  5. Add in bell peppers and mix until combined.
  6. Then add in the paneer cubes and toss to combine.
  7. Cover the bowl with a plastic wrap or towel and let the mixture marinate for at least 2 hours.
  8. Arrange the paneer and bell peppers alternating in a skewer.
  9. Place the skewers in a single layer on the lined baking sheet.
  10. Bake for 8 to 12 minutes, or until paneer tikka is golden and crisp on the edges.
