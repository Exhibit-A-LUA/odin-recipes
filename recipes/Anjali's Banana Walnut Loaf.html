Description

Anjali's Banana Walnut Loaf

Ingredients

  • 300 grams (three to four) over ripe bananas
  • 125 grams salted butter at room temperature
  • 150 grams demerara or brown sugar
  • 250 grams all purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 2 medium eggs
  • 1/4 cup thick curd
  • One eighth cup ( I just took half the 1/4 cup ) warm water
  • 100 grams chopped walnuts

Instructions

  1. Sift the flour and the baking powder and baking soda.
  2. In the food processor or blender jar or with an egg beater whip the butter, sugar and eggs.
  3. Add the flour, peeled bananas ,the curd and warm water all in one go and mix till well blended.
  4. Fold in the walnuts.
  5. Pour the batter into a nine inch loaf tin and bake in a preheated oven at 180Â° for 60 minutes.
