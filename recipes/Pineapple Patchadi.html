Pineapple Patchadi

Description

Pineapple Patchadi - Serves 8

Ingredients

  • ½ pineapple cubed
  • 1 med-large ethaka banana cubed
  • ½ small coconut grated
  • 2 green chillis
  • ½ tsp red chilli powder
  • ½ tsp turmeric
  • Jeera
  • Mustard seeds
  • Black Grapes (optionally add at end)

Prep

  • Cube the pineapple
  • Cube ethaka banana
  • Grate coconut

Tempering

  • see Tempering Recipe

Instructions

  1. Cook pineapple, banana, green chilli, red chilli powder, turmeric,
  2. Add a little salt with a little water
  3. Cook on medium flame until sloppy, (optionally add 2 tbsp sugar)
  4. Add salt to taste
  5. Lower flame
  6. Mixi coarsely coconut, 2 pinches jeera, little water
  7. Add pinch mustard seeds and quick mixi (2-3 seconds)
  8. Add to pineapple mixture, keep cooking
  9. Add 10-12 curry leaves, keep cooking
  10. Optionally bung in grapes
  11. Temper
  12. Bung temper on top
