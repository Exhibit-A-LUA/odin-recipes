Description

Strawberry Pie Filling

Ingredients

  • 550 (g) fresh strawberries, hulled (see tip)
  • 180 ml heavy whipping cream
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 tsp erythritol

Instructions

  1. While the tart cools, add a bit more than 1/4 of the strawberries to a food processor bowl, and purée until smooth.
  2. Use a knife to quarter the remaining berries.
  3. Add the purée and quartered berries to a medium-sized bowl, mix together, and set aside.
  4. Add the heavy cream, vanilla, and erythritol to a mixing bowl.
  5. Use a whisk, or an electric mixer (on medium/medium-high speed) to whip together, until soft peaks form.
  6. Set aside in the fridge.

Strawberries

  • The hull or calyx, is the green leafy top of the berry.
  • Just underneath is the white, flavourless core of the berry.

Hulling strawberries

  • Paring knife method:
  • Lift up the stem leaves, and insert the tip of the paring knife at an angle, under the leaves.
  • Move the knife around the stem, cutting out a cone shape from the top to remove the stem, along with the white core of the berry.
  • Drinking straw method:
  • Insert a drinking straw into the bottom of the berry, and push it all the way through the stem.
  • The stem leaves and the white core will be removed.
