Chicken Curry and Roti

Description

Chicken Curry and Roti

Ingredients

  • 400 (g) boneless chicken
  • Salt to taste
  • 1½ tsp oil
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 large onion chopped
  • 1 cup tomato puree
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1 tsp pav bhaji masala
  • ¼ tsp turmeric powder
  • ½ tsp cumin powder
  • 2 tbsp fresh coriander leaves
  • 1 tsp red chilli powder

Instructions

  1. Heat oil on a pan.
  2. Once hot, add cumin seeds and wait for a change of colour.
  3. Now add chopped onions and saute until it turns golden brown.
  4. Add ginger-garlic paste and continue to saute until you get an aroma.
  5. Next, add salt along with tomato puree and cook it until it becomes nice and mushy, integrating with the onions.
  6. After you have got a nice mushy paste consistency, add all the powders to get the masala flavours.
  7. Add chicken, coriander leaves, salt and mix well and increase the heat for 2 minutes.
  8. Add 1 ½ cups of water and cook until you get a nice curry consistency.
  9. Serve with some coriander leaves as garnish.
