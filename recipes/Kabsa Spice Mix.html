Description

Kabsa Spice Mix

Ingredients

  • 1/4 tsp ground cardamom (cardamom powder)
  • 1/4 tsp ground white pepper (white pepper powder)
  • 1/4 tsp saffron
  • 1/2 tsp ground cinnamon (cinnamon powder)
  • 1/2 tsp ground all spice
  • 1/2 tsp dried whole lime powder

Instructions

  1. Combine together cardamom, white pepper, saffron, cinnamon, all spice and lime powder in a bowl.
