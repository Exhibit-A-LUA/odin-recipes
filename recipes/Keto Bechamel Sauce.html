Keto Bechamel Sauce

Description

Ever wondered how to make that lovely creamy white sauce without using any flour? It's way simpler than you'd think. Not only gluten-free but also keto and so much easier to make than the original version, without compromising on its authentic taste. What a great addition to your repertoire!

Ingredients

  • 425 ml heavy whipping cream
  • 200 (g) (210 ml) cream cheese
  • ½ tsp salt
  • ¼ tsp ground black pepper
  • ¼ tsp ground nutmeg

Instructions

  1. Add the heavy whipping cream, cream cheese, salt, pepper, and nutmeg to a medium-sized, non-stick saucepan.
  2. Bring to a boil over high heat, while stirring continuously.
  3. Reduce temperature to medium-low, and simmer uncovered for a few minutes, until a desired consistency is reached.
  4. Prior to serving, season with salt, pepper, and nutmeg, to taste.
