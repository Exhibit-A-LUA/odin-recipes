Sponge Cake

Description

Sponge Cake

Ingredients

  • 4 eggs
  • 80 ml (30 g) cocoa powder
  • 180 ml (85 g) almond flour
  • 2 tbsp powdered erythritol
  • 3 tbsp unsweetened almond milk
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. You will need a Swiss roll tin or a shallow baking sheet about 13 "x 9" (33 x 23 cm).
  3. Line it with parchment paper.
  4. Separate the eggs and put the yolks in one mixing bowl and the whites in another.
  5. Sift the cocoa powder into the bowl with the yolks.
  6. Add the ground almonds, sweetener, milk, baking powder, and vanilla.
  7. Mix together thoroughly with a metal spoon.
  8. Beat the egg whites with an electric beater until they form stiff peaks.
  9. Add 2 tablespoons of the beaten whites to the almond mixture, mixing it in with the electric beater.
  10. Then, switch to a metal spoon and gently fold in the remaining egg whites.
  11. Spoon out the batter onto the prepared baking sheet and spread evenly with a palette knife until is it 1/4" (0.5 mm) thick.
  12. Bake for 8 minutes or until the sponge is just firm to the touch. Do not overcook.
  13. Remove from the oven and invert the sponge onto a clean tea towel, with the long edge of the sponge lining up along the long edge of the towel.
  14. Roll the sponge up into a tight spiral using the cloth (but don't roll the cloth inside the sponge, just have it wrapped around it.)
  15. Leave it to cool with the edge side down so that it sets into this shape.
