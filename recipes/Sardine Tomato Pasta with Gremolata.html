Sardine Tomato Pasta with Gremolata

Description

Sardine Tomato Pasta with Gremolata

Ingredients

Gremolata Sauce (13.50g)

  • see Gremolata Sauce Recipe

Sardine Tomato Pasta

  • 75g wholemeal spaghetti
  • 1⁄2 x 120g can sardines in oil
  • ½ tbsp capers, drained
  • 1 garlic clove, crushed
  • 2 tomatoes, roughly chopped
  • 30g rocket

Instructions

Gremolata Sauce (13.50g)

  • see Gremolata Sauce Recipe

Sardine Tomato Pasta

  1. Cook the pasta following pack instructions in a large pan of boiling salted water.
  2. Heat 1 tbsp oil from the can of sardines in a non-stick frying pan over a medium heat and sizzle the capers and 1 garlic for 1-2 mins until fragrant.
  3. Tip in the tomatoes and fry for 4-5 mins more until softened and bursting.
  4. Stir in the sardines and rocket, tossing a few times to break up the fish and wilt the leaves.
  5. Season
