Eggplant Dip

Description

Eggplant at its yummiest. Slice, roast, and blend with olive oil, spices, and a touch of lemon. That's it! Try it as a low-Carb dip for veggies or a topping for burgers and sandwiches. Terrific both ways!

Ingredients

  • 600 (g) eggplant
  • ½ tsp ground cumin
  • 60 ml olive oil
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 2 tbsp sesame seeds
  • salt and pepper

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Cut the eggplant lengthwise.
  3. Salt the cut surface and put the salty side upwards on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  4. Bake for 30 minutes or until the eggplant is soft.
  5. Take it out and let it cool for a while.
  6. Peel off the skin and cut the eggplant into cubes.
  7. Place in a bowl with oil, lemon juice, pepper and cumin.
  8. Blend to a smooth consistency and season to taste. Set aside.
  9. Add a couple of tablespoons of sesame seeds to a hot and dry frying pan and toast for a minute or two, stirring often.
  10. Serve the dip in a bowl with the toasted seeds, sea salt and olive oil on top.

Tips

  • Try it with our vegetarian halloumi cheese burger without bread.
  • If you don't like cumin, try paprika!
