Fishcakes

Description

Fishcakes

Ingredients

  • 600 g white fish fillets fillets
  • 1 (110 g) medium white onion, roughly chopped
  • 7 g (18 ml) fresh mint, roughly chopped
  • 7 g (100 ml) fresh cilantro, or fresh parsley, stalks and leaves, roughly chopped
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 tbsp olive oil or ghee

Instructions

  1. Cut the fish fillets into small pieces, and place into a food processor bowl.
  2. Add the onion, herbs, and salt.
  3. Pulse to form a rough paste.
  4. Measure 1/3 cup (80 ml) of the mixture for each fishcake (two fish cakes per serving).
  5. Using your hands, form even-sized fishcakes.
  6. Heat the oil in a large, non-stick frying pan over medium-high heat.
  7. Fry the fishcakes for about 4 minutes per side, or until lightly browned and cooked through.
  8. Serve the warm fishcakes with a salad on the side.
  9. item

Tips

  • Any white fish is suitable, such as haddock, cod or coley.
  • If you're catering for a crowd, buy frozen white fish and defrost them overnight.
  • If using previously frozen fish fillets, squeeze out any liquid before adding to the food processor bowl.
  • Cooking time will vary, depending on the thickness of the fish cakes; thinner fishcakes will require less time than thicker ones.
