Prosciutto-Wrapped Mozzarella Sticks with Tomato Basil Salad

Description

Our Prosciutto-wrapped mozzarella sticks make a complete, high protein lunch in minutes. Served with a cherry tomato and fresh basil salad, this Italian-inspired meal will fuel you up without slowing you down.

Ingredients

  • 45 (g) cherry tomatoes chopped
  • 1 tbsp chopped fresh basil
  • ½ tsp balsamic vinegar
  • ½ tsp olive oil
  • salt to taste
  • pepper to taste
  • 110 (g) sliced prosciutto
  • 55 (g) (120 ml) mozzarella cheese string cheese

Instructions

  1. Chop the tomatoes and basil and place in a small bowl.
  2. Stir in the vinegar, olive oil, salt, and pepper.
  3. Set aside.
  4. Place the slices of Prosciutto on a plate and remove the wrapping from each string cheese.
  5. Wrap 1-2 slices of Prosciutto around each mozzarella stick.
  6. Serve immediately or refrigerate until ready to eat.
  7. May be made a day before serving.

Substitutions

  • We used Prosciutto, but you could use any ham or turkey that you prefer.
  • For a creamy tomato salad, add 1/2 teaspoon of mayonnaise instead of olive oil.

About balsamic vinegar

  • This recipe calls for just a splash of balsamic vinegar.
  • When selecting balsamic vinegar, check for added sugars.
  • Some brands have more carbs than others.
  • You can also substitute red wine vinegar instead.

Tip

  • Each wrapped stick offers about 10 grams of protein.
