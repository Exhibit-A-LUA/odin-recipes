Keto Thai Fish Curry and Bok Choy

Description

White fish is a wonderful protein source, full of vitamins and essential minerals. Red curry and coconut cream give this dish a spicy and silky Thai flavour, and bok choy adds a healthy, mild crunch. Choose any firm white fish that you wish.

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp coconut oil or avocado oil
  • 2 tbsp red curry paste
  • 350 ml coconut cream
  • 240 ml water
  • 1 tbsp fish sauce
  • 900 g white fish fillets, cubed
  • 450 g bok choy, thinly sliced
  • salt, to taste

Instructions

  1. Heat the oil in a large saucepan.
  2. Add the curry paste, and fry on moderate heat for 2-3 minutes to activate the spices.
  3. Add the coconut cream, water, and fish sauce and bring to a boil.
  4. Carefully add the fish and reduce the heat.
  5. Simmer for 10 minutes.
  6. Add the bok choy and cook for another 3-4 minutes.
  7. Serve in large bowls.

Tips

  • If you can't find bok choy, spinach is a great substitute.
