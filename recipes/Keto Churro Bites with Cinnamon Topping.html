Keto Churro Bites with Cinnamon Topping

Description

The keto dough to make these cinnamon doughnut bites takes only minutes to prepare. Then you shape, fry, and roll your way to a plateful of churros. You won't believe that cheese is one of the main ingredients in these gluten-free treats — we had to try more than one to believe it! They taste like the real thing, but they're delightfully keto!

Tips

  • There is one churro bite per serving.
  • These are best served on the same day they're made.
  • You can bake them instead. Grease a mini muffin tin and fill each muffin hole with a churro bite. Bake in the oven at 400°F (200°C) until golden, for about 20 minutes. Roll the bites in the cinnamon-erythritol mixture while warm.
