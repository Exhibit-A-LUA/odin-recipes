Almond Filling

Ingredients

  • 60 ml (65 g) almond butter
  • 1 tbsp powdered erythritol

Instructions

  1. Add almond butter and powdered erythritol to a medium mixing bowl. Mix well.
  2. If your almond butter is more firm, you may want to heat the mixture in the microwave for a few seconds.
  3. Remove chocolate cups from the fridge.
  4. Cover the base of each chocolate cup with a teaspoon of almond filling and top with a teaspoon of raspberry jam.
  5. Cover filled chocolate cups with remaining chocolate.
  6. Refrigerate for 1 hour.
