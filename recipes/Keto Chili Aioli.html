Keto Chilli Aioli

Description

Creamy and light. A French emulsion of avocado oil goodness, with just the right blend of garlic, lemon and chilli. Wonderful with grilled seafood, veggies and salads… or use it as a sandwich spread. Bon appétit!

Ingredients

  • 1 egg yolk
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 180 ml avocado oil, or other flavourless oil of your choice or light olive oil
  • ½ tsp chilli flakes
  • ½ tsp salt
  • ¼ tsp ground black pepper
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 3 tbsp Greek yoghurt (4% fat)

Instructions

  1. Press the garlic into a bowl.
  2. Add the egg yolk and mix well.
  3. Add the oil in a thin stream while continuously whisking vigorously by hand or with a hand blender.
  4. Whisk the yoghurt and spices. Taste
  5. If you feel the need, add some more salt or lemon juice and garlic—its up to you and your taste buds!

Tips

  • The secret to this amazing aioli is to use a high-quality avocado oil or light olive oil and a creamy yoghurt that will delicately balance the flavours.
  • This blend will keep for 4 to 5 days in the refrigerator.
