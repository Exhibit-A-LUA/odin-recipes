Description

Garlic Lime Sauce

Ingredients

  • 240 ml (180 g) Greek yoghurt (0% fat)
  • 3 tbsp mayonnaise
  • 1 lime, juice and zest
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • salt and ground black pepper

Mayonnaise

  • see Mayonnaise Recipe

Instructions

  1. In a small bowl, combine the Greek yoghurt, mayonnaise, lime juice and zest, garlic, salt, and black pepper.
