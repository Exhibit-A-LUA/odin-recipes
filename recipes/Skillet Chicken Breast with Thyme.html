Skillet Chicken Breast with Thyme

Ingredients

  • 2 (450 g) chicken breasts 8 oz (230 g) each
  • salt and ground black pepper
  • 2 tsp dried thyme
  • Â½ tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tbsp butter or olive oil

Instructions

  1. To speed up the cooking process, cut the chicken breasts in half, lengthwise, into thin cutlets.
  2. Season both sides of the chicken with salt, ground black pepper, thyme, and garlic powder.
  3. Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat.
  4. Add the butter or oil to the skillet when hot.
  5. Carefully place the chicken breasts in the skillet and cook for about 4 minutes on each side.
  6. The chicken is ready when it's no longer pink.
