Cheese Pancake Filling

Ingredients

  • 1 large egg
  • 160 ml (160 g) full-fat cream cheese, room temperature
  • 1 tsp lemon zest
  • Â½ tsp sugar-free vanilla extract
  • 2 tbsp powdered erythritol

Instructions

  1. Separate the egg white from the egg yolk and put them in two different bowls.
  2. Add the cream cheese to the bowl with the egg yolk.
  3. Add the lemon zest, vanilla, and erythritol.
  4. Mix until smooth.
