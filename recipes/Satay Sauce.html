Satay Sauce

Description

Here's a wonderful, low Carb, peanut butter sauce recipe also known as satay sauce. It's very popular with kids because of the creamy sweetness of coconut milk, and it's delicious with Thai food.

Ingredients

  • 425 ml coconut milk
  • 2 tsp red curry paste
  • 1 garlic clove, minced
  • 1 tbsp tamari soy sauce or fish sauce
  • 80 ml peanut butter, without added sugar

Instructions

  1. Combine all the ingredients in a small saucepan until smooth.
  2. Bring to a boil.
  3. Let simmer on low heat for 5â€“10 minutes until the sauce is of desired consistency.
  4. Taste and add more soy sauce if you think it needs more salt.

Tips

  • Is your peanut butter sugar-free?
  • Most branded peanut butters contain around 2 grams of sugar (half a teaspoon) per serving, so make sure to check the label.
  • Ideally, your peanut butter should contain nothing but peanuts and sometimes salt.
  • Don't be put off by peanut butter with a little layer of oil at the top of the jar, That means it's natural.
  • Just stir it into the peanut butter.
