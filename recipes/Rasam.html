Description

Rasam

Ingredients

The Rasam

  • 1 tomato (chopped)
  • 2 inch ginger (finely chopped)
  • 3 chilli (finely chopped)
  • few curry leaves
  • 2 tbsp coriander (finely chopped)
  • ½ tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 5 cup water
  • 1½ cup toor dal
  • 2 tbsp coconut (grated)
  • 2 tbsp coriander (finely chopped)
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice

Tempering

  • see Tempering Recipe

Instructions

  1. In a large kadai
  2. Add the tomato, ginger, chilli, curry leaves and coriander.
  3. Add the turmeric, salt and water.
  4. Mix well, cover and boil for 15 minutes.
  5. After boiling, the tomato has softened and all the flavours are in the water.
  6. Add the toor dal and bring to a boil.
  7. Prepare the tempering
  8. Pour the tempering over the rasam.
  9. Add the coconut, coriander and lemon juice. mix well.
