Description

Capers Butter

Ingredients

  • 85 (g) butter at room temperature
  • ½ tsp sea salt
  • ¼ tsp ground black pepper
  • 1 tbsp small capers
  • 1 tbsp shallots, finely chopped
  • ½ tbsp Dijon mustard

Instructions

  1. Stir together butter, salt and pepper in a bowl with a fork, which is easiest to do when the butter is soft.
  2. Add flavouring and mix thoroughly.
  3. Let the butter sit in the refrigerator for at least 30 minutes to let the flavours settle.
  4. Three ounces of butter is enough for 2 to 3 people, depending on what the butter will served with.
