Egg and Cheese Pie Crust

Description

Egg and Cheese Pie Crust

Ingredients

  • 1 egg
  • 350 ml (170 g) almond flour
  • 350 ml (170 g) mozzarella cheese
  • 45 g (45 ml) cream cheese
  • 2 pinches salt
  • 1 egg yolk to give the crust a shiny golden colour

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, mix the egg, almond flour, and salt. Stir together.
  2. In another bowl, add the shredded mozzarella and cream cheese.
  3. Microwave for two minutes, watching closely to make sure it does not burn.
  4. Mix well with the help of a spatula.
  5. Then add to the egg and flour mixture.
  6. Knead well with moistened hands until a homogeneous dough ball is formed.
