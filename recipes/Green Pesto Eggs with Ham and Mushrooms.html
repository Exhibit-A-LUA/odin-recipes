Green Pesto Eggs with Ham and Mushrooms

Description

Green Pesto Eggs with Ham and Mushrooms

Ingredients

Ham and mushrooms

  • ½ tbsp butter
  • 85 (g) ham, cured or Canadian bacon
  • 100 (g) (350 ml) mushrooms
  • salt and pepper, to taste

Green pesto eggs

  • ½ tbsp butter
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 tbsp heavy whipping cream
  • 1 tbsp green pesto

Instructions

Ham and mushrooms

  1. Heat the butter in a non-stick pan.
  2. Add the ham and cook until crisp, flipping occasionally.
  3. When done, remove the ham from the pan.
  4. In the same pan, add the mushrooms and cook until soft.
  5. Season with salt and pepper.
  6. Remove from pan and keep warm.

Green pesto eggs

  1. Beat the eggs with the whipping cream and pesto.
  2. Heat the butter in the same pan you used before.
  3. Lower the heat and pour in the egg mixture.
  4. Keep moving the eggs around in the pan, letting them cook low and slow.
  5. Remove from the pan once your preferred done-ness is reached.
  6. Serve the green eggs with the ham and mushrooms.

Tips

  • The original recipe combines paprika, oregano, and chilli flakes when frying mushrooms.
