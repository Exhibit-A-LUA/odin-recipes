Low Carb Cheesecake with Sour Cream Topping

Description

A simple, baked cheesecake never goes out of style. We've topped ours with a classic sour cream topping, but you can also use fresh berries, our sugar-free milk chocolate sauce, a dollop of freshly whipped cream, or nothing at all! See our tips for a perfect cheesecake every single time.

Putting it all Together

  1. Pour the filling into the par-baked crust.
  2. Bake the cheesecake on the middle rack, for 60 to 70 minutes, until just set.
  3. It may still jiggle just a bit in the centre.
  4. Turn off the oven and let the cake cool in the oven with the door ajar for at least 30 minutes.
  5. Remove the cheesecake from the oven, run a knife around the edge of the pan, and let the cake cool to room temperature, about 1 hour.
  6. When completely cool, cover and refrigerate the cheesecake for at least 8 hours before slicing and serving.

Serving

  1. Remove the outer ring from the spring-form pan and remove the parchment paper.
  2. Top the cake with sour cream topping.
  3. For a more decorative cheesecake: Whip the cream and pipe it around the sides of the cake and top with strawberries.

Tips

  • First, to avoid cracks in the top of the cheesecake, let the cheesecake cool gradually by leaving it in the oven with the oven turned off, and the door slightly ajar.
  • Running a knife around the edge of the pan as soon as the cheesecake comes out of the oven will help prevent cracks as the cake cools and contracts.

  • Second, for the best texture, avoid over-mixing the eggs.
  • Over-mixing the eggs results in a more fluffy texture while gently mixing the eggs yields a more dense, traditional cheesecake.
  • Lastly, be sure to let the cheesecake cure in the refrigerator for at least 8 hours (overnight is better) for the best flavour and texture.

Storing the cheesecake

  • Store in an airtight container, in the refrigerator, for up to five days.
  • You may also freeze the cheesecake whole, or in individual slices for up to three months.
  • Thaw in the refrigerator before serving.
