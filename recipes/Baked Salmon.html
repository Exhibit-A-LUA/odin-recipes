Baked Salmon

Description

Baked Salmon

Ingredients

  • 900 g salmon, boneless fillets, cut into portions
  • 2 tsp olive oil
  • sea salt or ground black pepper, to taste

Instructions

  1. Line a baking tray with parchment paper.
  2. Place the salmon fillets on the parchment paper, skin side down.
  3. Drizzle with olive oil, and season with salt and freshly ground black pepper.
