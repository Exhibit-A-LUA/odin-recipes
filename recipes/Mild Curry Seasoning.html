Mild Curry Seasoning

Description

Want an exotic blend of spices without too much kick? Curry doesn't have to be hot and spicy. This flavourful blend adds flair and complexity to veggies, meat, poultry and fish. Mmmm, without the hot-hot-hot.

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp ground cumin
  • 2 tbsp coriander seed, ground
  • 2 tbsp turmeric
  • ½ tbsp chilli flakes
  • ½ tbsp yellow mustard seeds
  • ½ tbsp ground ginger
  • 1 tbsp sea salt (optional)

Instructions

  1. Add all ingredients to a bowl and stir until thoroughly blended.
  2. Pour into a jar with a tight-fitting lid.
  3. If you use whole seeds, grind in a grinder either in advance or when cooking.

Tips

  • Keep the spices in a dark, dry and cool/room temperature place.
  • Small tin cans are great.
  • Make a big batch to last for 4–6 months; after this the spices will lose some flavour and colour.
  • They won't go bad, but will just be less powerful.
  • If you want to take up the heat, just add more chilli.
