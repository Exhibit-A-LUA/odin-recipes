Keto Tomato Smash Burger

Description

Feel like a kid again with this messy, juicy, fresh burger with a tomato bun. This quick and easy, high protein, keto burger will become your new favourite go-to meal for busy week-nights

Ingredients

  • 450 (g) ground beef
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • salt and pepper, to taste
  • 450 (g) beefsteak tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp mayonnaise
  • 2 tsp Dijon mustard
  • 2 tbsp pickles, sliced

Instructions

  1. Roll the ground meat into two balls per portion.
  2. Heat a large frying pan with butter over medium-high heat.
  3. When hot, add the meatballs to the frying pan and press them down to into patties using a big spatula or a small plate.
  4. Preferably, try to make them as wide as your "tomato buns".
  5. Generously season the top of the burger with salt and pepper.
  6. Fry for a few minutes before flipping over.
  7. Add salt and pepper to the other side and continue frying until preferred done-ness is reached.
  8. Slice the beefsteak tomatoes into 0.5" (1 cm) thick slices, 4 per serving.
  9. Pat them dry with a paper towel.
  10. Put mayonnaise on half of the slices.
  11. Top with a burger, mustard, pickles, and another tomato slice.

Tips

  • You can replace the ground beef with any other meat, such as ground turkey, pork or lamb.
  • Just make sure to adjust the cooking time accordingly.
