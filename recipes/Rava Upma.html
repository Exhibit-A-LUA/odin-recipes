Rava Upma

Description

Rava Upma

Ingredients

  • ¾ cup rava or sooji
  • ¼ tsp mustard seeds
  • ¼ tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tsp chana daal and urad daal
  • 1 pinch asafoetida or hing
  • 1 – 2 sprigs of curry leaves
  • 1 medium onion, finely chopped
  • ½ tsp ginger grated
  • 1 – 2 green chillies, chopped
  • ½ cup frozen peas and other veggies like beans or carrots
  • 1 large tomato, finely chopped
  • 2 tsp of coriander leaves
  • 1 tsp ghee/oil
  • One large glass buttermilk

Instructions

  1. Add rava or semolina to a pan and dry roast it on low medium flame until it turns crunchy.
  2. Remember to keep stirring to prevent rava from burning.
  3. In another pan, pour oil and add mustard seeds followed by cumin seeds.
  4. When it starts spluttering, add the lentils and cook.
  5. Add a pinch of hing.
  6. Next, add the chillies and finely chopped ginger to the mix.
  7. Add the curry leaves too and cook until it becomes fragrant.
  8. Now add the chopped onions and the veggies you are using.
  9. Cook under it turns tender.
  10. Pour thick buttermilk and boil.
  11. Add salt and stir.
  12. Add the roasted rava slowly and mix it well.
  13. Make sure there are no lumps.
  14. Cover and cook till the water is absorbed.
