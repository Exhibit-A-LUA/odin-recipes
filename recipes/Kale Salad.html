Kale Salad

Description

Kale Salad with nutrient-packed seeds.

Ingredients

  • 85 g kale, chopped or baby spinach
  • 2 tsp cider vinegar
  • 1 tbsp chia seeds
  • 1 tbsp sunflower seeds
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • salt

Instructions

  1. Mix the greens, seeds, vinegar, olive oil, and salt in a bowl.
  2. If you use a tougher type of kale, such as curly kale, you can tenderize the leaves by massaging the kale salad firmly with your hands.

Tips

  • You can use whatever seeds you'd like in this salad.
  • You can also drizzle the greens with a squeeze of lemon if you wish.
