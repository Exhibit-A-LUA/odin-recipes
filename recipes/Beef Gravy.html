Beef Gravy

Ingredients

  • 3 tablespoons butter (optional, see notes above)
  • 4 cups beef broth (or beef drippings, or combination of both)
  • 3 teaspoons onion powder
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 4 tablespoons cornstarch
  • 1/3 cup water
  • beef bouillon to taste
  • ground black pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. In a medium-sized sauce pan, combine butter, beef broth, onion powder, and garlic powder.
  2. Bring to a boil, stirring occasionally.
  3. Reduce heat and bring to a simmer.
  4. In a small bowl or liquid measuring cup, combine cornstarch and water, stirring until well combined.
  5. While whisking the broth CONSTANTLY, slowly add cornstarch mixture to the saucepan.
  6. I like to pour in about a tablespoon at a time to gauge consistency.
  7. You may not need all of the cornstarch mixture, add only until desired consistency is reached.
  8. Add beef bouillon (or Kosher saltâ€“ I just like the depth of flavor the beef bouillon adds as compared to straight up salt) and pepper to taste.
  9. Allow to simmer 2-3 minutes.
  10. Serve over potatoes, rice, poutine, or your favorite beef dishes.
