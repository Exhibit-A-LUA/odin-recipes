Luscious Lime High Protein Smoothie

Description

Whip up our simple, high protein lime smoothie in mere minutes to start your day with the protein boost you need without protein powders.

Ingredients

  • 140 (g) (160 ml) cottage cheese
  • 350 ml (270 g) Greek yogurt (4% fat)
  • 80 ml sour cream
  • 80 ml (45 g) powdered erythritol or a few stevia drops (optional)
  • 2 tbsp lime juice
  • 2 tsp lime zest

Instructions

  1. Place the cottage cheese in a blender or food processor and blend until smooth.
  2. Add the yoghurt, sour cream, sweetener, lime juice, and lime zest.
  3. Blend until creamy.
  4. Pour the mixture into two serving glasses.
  5. Serve immediately.
  6. Garnish with additional lime zest, a lime wedge, or a dollop of freshly whipped cream.

Tips

  • Using the zest of the lime is a fantastic way to add a lot of flavour and no carbs.
  • The zest (the very thin outer green part) has oils that carry a lot of zip.
  • Be sure to avoid the white part of the peel since it is very bitter.
