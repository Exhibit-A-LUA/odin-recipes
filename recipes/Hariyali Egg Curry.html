Hariyali Egg Curry

Description

Hariyali Egg Curry

Ingredients

Egg Curry

  • 4 whole eggs, boiled
  • 1 cinnamon stick (dalchini), small
  • 1 bay leaf (tej patta)
  • 2 cloves (laung)
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 2 tsp coriander powder (dhania)
  • 1 tsp garam masala powder
  • 2 tbsp fresh cream

Hariyali Masala

  • 2 cups coriander (dhania) leaves, tightly packed, chopped
  • 1 cup mint leaves (pudina), chopped
  • 2 green chillies, chopped

Onion Masala

  • 1 onion, roughly chopped
  • 1-inch ginger, chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic

Instructions

  1. Into the Mixer Grinder (medium jar), add the coriander leaves, mint leaves, green chillies and 1/2 cup water.
  2. Blend all of them together to make a smooth paste and keep it aside.
  3. Add onions, ginger and garlic into the small jar of the mixer grinder and grind to make a paste.
  4. Heat 1 tablespoon oil in a kadai over medium heat, add the bay leaf (tej patta), cloves, cinnamon stick and roast for a few seconds.
  5. Add the onion ginger garlic paste.
  6. Saut√© for 4 to 5 minutes until the raw onion smell goes away.
  7. Once the onions are roasted, add garam masala powder, coriander powder and saute for a few seconds.
  8. Add the hariyali coriander mint mixture, the fresh cream and the 4 boiled eggs.
  9. Add water if required to adjust the consistency of the hariyali egg curry.
  10. Cover & simmer the hariyali egg curry for 5 minutes.
  11. Check the salt and spices and adjust according to your taste and transfer the hariyali egg curry into a serving bowl.
  12. Serve with ragi roti for that healthy combo.
