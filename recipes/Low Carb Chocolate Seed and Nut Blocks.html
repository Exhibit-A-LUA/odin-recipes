Low Carb Chocolate Seed and Nut Blocks

Description

A healthy, low Carb, nutrient-dense and vegan-friendly with a variety of seeds and plant-based coconut butter and cocoa butter.

Ingredients

  • 60 ml (35 g) sesame seeds, chopped
  • 60 ml (35 g) sunflower seeds
  • 60 ml (33 g) pumpkin seeds
  • 60 ml (35 g) slivered almonds
  • 1 tbsp chia seeds
  • 1 tbsp erythritol
  • Â¼ tsp salt (optional)
  • 85 (g) (150 ml) sugar-free dark chocolate
  • 55 (g) (60 ml) coconut butter
  • 55 (g) (60 ml) cocoa butter
  • unsweetened finely shredded coconut or shaved (optional)

Instructions

  1. Mix seeds, nuts, erythritol, and salt (if using) in a medium mixing bowl. Set aside.
  2. Place chocolate, coconut butter, and cocoa butter in a microwave-safe container.
  3. Heat in 30-second bursts, stirring after each burst of heat, until completely melted.
  4. Add melted chocolate to seed and nut mixture.
  5. Stir until well combined.
  6. Evenly divide the mixture between silicone ice cube trays.
  7. Refrigerate for a minimum of 1 hour.
  8. Remove seed and nut blocks from trays. Enjoy!

Tips

  • Roast the seeds and almonds in a dry frying pan until fragrant to maximize the flavour.

Storing

  • Store in an airtight container in the fridge for up to 4 days or in the freezer for up to 4 weeks.

Decoration

  • Decoration Top it off with a drizzle of melted chocolate and shredded or shaved coconut.
