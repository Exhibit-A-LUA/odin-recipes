Description

Marinara Sauce

Ingredients

  • 2 (411g) cans stewed tomatoes
  • 1 (170g) can tomato paste
  • 4 tbsp chopped fresh parsley
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp salt
  • ¼ tsp ground black pepper
  • 6 tbsp olive oil
  • ⅓ cup finely diced onion
  • ½ cup white wine

Instructions

  1. Place tomatoes, tomato paste, parsley, garlic, oregano, salt, and pepper in a food processor; blend until smooth.
  2. Heat oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
  3. Add onion and cook until slightly softened, about 2 minutes.
  4. Stir in blended tomato sauce and white wine.
  5. Simmer, stirring occasionally, until thickened, about 30 minutes.
