High Protein Turkey Breakfast Burger

Description

Whether you enjoy this protein-packed keto burger for breakfast, lunch or dinner, it will keep you satisfied for hours. The best part is that you only need one pan, which means fewer dishes. Be prepared to get your hands dirty if you forgo the knife and fork, we won't tell.

Ingredients

  • 55 (g) uncooked bacon, cut in half
  • 450 (g) ground turkey or ground beef
  • salt and ground black pepper
  • 2 large eggs
  • 4 butterhead lettuce leaves
  • 2 tbsp mayonnaise
  • Â½ (55 g) tomato, sliced

Instructions

  1. Fry the bacon in a large non-stick frying pan over medium-high heat until crispy.
  2. Move bacon to a paper towel-lined plate to absorb excess fat. Keep warm.
  3. Shape the meat into two balls per serving. Set aside.
  4. Place the meatballs in the frying pan over medium heat.
  5. Using a spatula or small plate, press down on the meatballs to form burgers.
  6. Season generously with salt and pepper.
  7. Fry on both sides until browned.
  8. Remove from the pan and keep warm.
  9. Over medium heat, fry the eggs until cooked to desired done-ness.
  10. Season with salt and pepper.
  11. To serve, place the lettuce on a plate, and layer the burgers, mayonnaise, tomato, egg, and bacon on top.
  12. Make one big burger or two smaller (easier to eat!) ones.

Tips

  • Feel free to add extra seasoning to the ground meat, such as onion, garlic powder, and/or chipotle chilli.
  • If you don't eat pork you can replace the bacon with turkey bacon.
  • In this case, you might need to add some extra fat to the pan when frying since turkey bacon has much less fat.
