Biscuit Pudding

Description

Biscuit Pudding

Ingredients

  • 1 cup butter
  • 1 cup powdered sugar
  • 4 eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 tbsp vanilla essence or extract
  • 1 1/2 tsp rum
  • 1 cup milk
  • about 9 packets of glucose biscuits-u can use marie too.

Instructions

  1. Cream butter and sugar until light and fluffy.
  2. Beat the eggs in the creamed mixture.
  3. Add the essence and 1/4 cup milk.
  4. Take a long rectangular or square glass dish.
  5. Dip the biscuit in a cup of milk in which rum is added and arrange layer of biscuits and pouring the creamed mixture.
  6. Keep alternating till the biscuits finish.
  7. Lastly whip cream and decorate the top and refrigerate.
  8. It should be refrigerated for at least 24 hrs before serving.

Tips

  • I like a mix of chocolate and plain. so if you want that then divide the mixture into 2 bowls, to one add 3 tbsp of chocolate powder and beat well and then alternate. if you like coffee then add some instant coffee to the half mixture and then alternate.
