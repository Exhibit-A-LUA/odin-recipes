Description

Cinnamon Roll Filling

Ingredients

  • 110 (g) butter, softened
  • 1 tbsp ground cinnamon
  • 60 ml (32 g) powdered erythritol

Instructions

  1. Mix together cinnamon, erythritol, and butter in a bowl.
  2. Spread the butter mixture carefully on top of the dough with a spatula.
