Lemon Butter Salmon

Description

Lemon Butter Salmon

Ingredients

  • ½ tbsp butter
  • 140 g salmon, boneless fillets
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste
  • ½ lemon

Instructions

  1. Heat a skillet over medium-high heat.
  2. Add the butter and wait for it to melt.
  3. Season the salmon with salt and pepper and add it to the pan.
  4. Squeeze the lemon over the top.
  5. Fry for a couple of minutes on each side until your preferred level of done-ness is reached.
