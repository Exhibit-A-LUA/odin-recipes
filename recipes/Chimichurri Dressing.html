Chimichurri Dressing

Description

Chimichurri Dressing

Ingredients

  • 4 tbsp mayonnaise
  • ¼ tbsp red wine vinegar
  • 120 ml (8 g) fresh parsley or fresh cilantro
  • ½ tbsp red chili peppers, chopped
  • 1 tbsp dried oregano
  • 1 garlic clove

Mayonnaise

  • see Mayonnaise Recipe

Instructions

  1. Add all the ingredients for the chimichurri dressing to a food processor or a blender.
  2. Mix until smooth.
  3. Add some water if you prefer a runnier consistency.
