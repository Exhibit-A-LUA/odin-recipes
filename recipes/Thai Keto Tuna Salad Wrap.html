Thai Keto Tuna Salad Wrap

Description

This is the perfect high protein lunch or dinner for busy weekdays. Fresh, colourful, and fuss-free with all the Thai flavours that you love. No cooking required!

Ingredients

  • 140 g tuna in water, drained
  • 2 tbsp mayonnaise
  • 14 g (33 ml) scallions, finely chopped
  • 60 ml (4 g) chopped fresh cilantro
  • ½ tbsp finely chopped red chilli peppers (save some for garnish)
  • ½ lime, the juice
  • salt to taste
  • 4 small lettuce leaves

Instructions

  1. Add the tuna, scallions, cilantro, mayonnaise, chilli, and lime juice to a bowl and give it a good mix.
  2. Taste and season with salt if needed.
  3. Place the lettuce leaves on a plate and top with the tuna mix.
  4. Fold together like a wrap and enjoy your first bite.

Tips

  • Instead of the chilli you could also use any other chilli paste, such as sriracha, sambal oelek, or sugar free chilli and garlic sauce.
  • For an even more filling meal, add half of an avocado and a soft boiled egg.
  • You could replace the tuna with cooked chicken breast if you prefer.
