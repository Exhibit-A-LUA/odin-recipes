Grilled Chicken Caesar Salad

Description

Calling all Caesar salad lovers! Our grilled Caesar salad is a smoky spin on the classic recipe. Leafy wedges of romaine are seared on the barbecue to create a lightly charred flavour, then drizzled with creamy home-made Caesar dressing and Parmesan cheese.

Ingredients

Caesar Salad Dressing

  • see Caesar Salad Dressing Recipe

Grilled Chicken and Lettuce

  • 2 (55 g) Romaine lettuce
  • 900 (g) boneless chicken thighs
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • salt and ground black pepper

Instructions

Caesar Salad Dressing

  • see Caesar Salad Dressing Recipe

Grilled Chicken and Lettuce

  1. Take the entire head of romaine and cut it into quarters lengthwise.
  2. Brush the chicken thighs and romaine lettuce with olive oil.
  3. Season with salt and pepper.
  4. Grill the chicken until it's no longer pink inside.
  5. If you have room for the lettuce on the grill or grill pan, add it halfway through.
  6. Otherwise, remove the chicken when done and keep warm while you grill the romaine lettuce until charred and slightly soft.
  7. Serve the grilled chicken and romaine salad with the dressing.
  8. Top with the remaining Parmesan cheese and freshly ground black pepper.

Tips

  • If you are missing crunchy croutons, try pork rinds instead.
