Fish Salad

Description

Fish Salad

Ingredients

  • 55 g (280 ml) Romaine lettuce
  • 28 g (325 ml) arugula lettuce or watercress
  • 28 g (40 ml) red onions, sliced
  • 140 g cucumber, sliced
  • 110 g cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 280 g tuna in water, drained and flaked
  • 28 g (50 ml) olives, pitted

Instructions

  1. Place the lettuce leaves into serving bowls.
  2. Top each with arugula, onion, cucumber, tomatoes, tuna, and olives.
  3. Drizzle with dressing and serve.

Tips

  • Swap the tuna for any canned fish such as sardines, mackerel, salmon, or herring.
  • To store, refrigerate for up to 1 day.
  • Optionally, you can add avocado or a hard-boiled egg.
