Aviyal

Description

Aviyal - Serves 8

Ingredients

Vegetables

  • 1 Kerala Cucumber (Dosakaya)
  • 3 green chilli
  • 1 Snake Gourd
  • ½ tsp Red chilli powder
  • 3-4 Carrots
  • 2 Drumstick
  • ½ tsp turmeric
  • 1 Raw Banana
  • 20 curry leaves
  • Yam
  • Salt
  • ½ Green Mango
  • 20 beans
  • 3-4 tbsp coconut oil

Paste

  • 2 sambar onions
  • Curd (optional)
  • ½ cup coconut grated
  • ½ tsp jeera
  • 2 green chilli

Prep

  • Cut all vegetables into finger length pieces
  • Grate coconut

Instructions

Vegetables

  1. Bung all veges except drumstick in a large pan.
  2. Add as much mango as needed (depending on how sour)
  3. Add green chillis, turmeric, Red chilli powder, salt, coconut oil, a little water, and let it steam on medium flame covered for 5 minutes.
  4. Then add drumstick, put on lid and cook, keep mixing.
  5. Check if tangy enough and add more mango if needed.
  6. Add salt, keep mixing adding little water at a time. All on low flame.

Paste

  1. Mixi coarsely, 6 heaped tbsp coconut, jeera, 2 green chillis, onions
  2. Mixi more coarsely, rest of coconut,
  3. Bung together
  4. Once veges cooked, add coconut, with curry leaves, cover for 5 minutes.
  5. Mix. Add salt to taste.
  6. Add curd (optionally), and mix.
  7. Add 1 ½ tbsp coconut oil. Keep covered.
