Boiled Egg Sandwich

Description

Boiled Egg Sandwich

Ingredients

  • 4 Eggs
  • 6 Slices of bread
  • ½ cup Hung Curd
  • ¼ tsp Coarsely ground fresh black pepper
  • ½ tsp Mustard paste

Instructions

  1. Put the eggs in a cooking bowl and add water till they are submerged.
  2. Put the eggs in first to be sure they don’t crack.
  3. Bring the water to a rolling boil and reduce the heat to low.
  4. Let the eggs cook for 8 – 15 minutes depending on how well cooked you like it.
  5. After that, drain the water and cool the eggs off in water or ice.
  6. Once the eggs cool, peel off the shell and dice the eggs up into small bits.
  7. Mix well in a bowl with black pepper, mustard paste, salt and half a cup of curd.
  8. Divide the mix into equal portions for each sandwich.
  9. Once the eggs cool, peel off the shell and dice the eggs up into small bits.
  10. Mix well in a bowl with black pepper, mustard paste, salt and half a cup of curd.
  11. Divide the mix into equal portions for each sandwich.
