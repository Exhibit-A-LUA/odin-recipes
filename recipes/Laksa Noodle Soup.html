Laksa Noodle Soup

Description

Laksa Noodle Soup

Ingredients

  • 260 g green cabbage, shredded
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 lime, cut into wedges
  • 1 tbsp fresh cilantro
  • tamari soy sauce (optional)

Instructions

  1. Carefully add the eggs to boiling water and boil for 7 minutes (if you like the yolks slightly runny).
  2. Remove the eggs from the water and cool them off in ice cold water.
  3. When cool enough to handle, peel, and halve, the eggs.
  4. Divide the cabbage between four deep bowls and ladle the broth into the bowls.
  5. Top each bowl with two egg halves, cilantro, a lime wedge, and a few shakes of tamari before serving.
