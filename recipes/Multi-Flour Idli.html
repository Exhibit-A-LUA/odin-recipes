Multi-Flour Idli

Description

Multi-Flour Idli

Ingredients

  • ½ cup urad dal
  • 1 tsp fenugreek seeds
  • ½ cup bajra
  • ½ cup jowar
  • ½ cup ragi
  • ½ cup whole wheat flour
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Combine the split black lentils and fenugreek seeds in a bowl and soak them for a couple of hours.
  2. Drain well and make a smooth blend with 3/4 cup of water.
  3. Transfer the smooth mixture into a big bowl, add the different flours to the mixture, add salt and 1 3/4 cups of water.
  4. Mix it well.
  5. Cover and keep it overnight to ferment.
  6. Give the batter a good mix in the morning.
  7. Pour spoonfuls of batter into well-greased idli stands.
  8. Steam the idlis for 10 minutes until they are cooked.
  9. Serve idlis with sambar or chutney!
