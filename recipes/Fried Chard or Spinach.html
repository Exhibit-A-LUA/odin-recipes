Fried Chard or Spinach

Description

Fried Chard or Spinach

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 garlic clove, sliced
  • 300 g (2 litres) chard, roughly chopped or fresh spinach
  • salt or ground black pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Heat a large skillet with olive oil over medium heat.
  2. Once hot, add the garlic and cook for a minute.
  3. Add the chard and cook until wilted.
  4. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
