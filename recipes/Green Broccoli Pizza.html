Green Broccoli Pizza

Description

Have you heard? Green is the new colour in pizza these days! If you're already a fan of the better-known cauliflower crust, this cheesy broccoli version will bring some competition to the (kitchen) table. The method is similar, and this particular version is topped with all things green — asparagus, arugula, pesto, and scallions. Heaps of flavour, low carb, and great for sharing, too!

Ingredients

Broccoli Pizza Crust

  • see Broccoli Pizza Crust Recipe

Toppings

  • 3 tbsp green pesto
  • 55 g green asparagus, trimmed and sliced diagonally
  • 2 (30 g) scallions, sliced diagonally
  • 85 g mozzarella, mini cheese balls, halved
  • 14 g (170 ml) arugula lettuce
  • ¼ tsp flaky sea salt
  • ¼ tsp or more to taste, chilli flakes

Instructions

Broccoli Pizza Crust

  • see Broccoli Pizza Crust Recipe

Toppings

  1. Remove the pizza from the oven.
  2. Spread the pesto over the base and top with asparagus and scallions.
  3. Halve the mini mozzarella balls and dot them over the pizza.
  4. Bake for another 10-12 minutes.
  5. Remove from the oven and let it cool for 5 minutes.
  6. Scatter the arugula over the pizza, sprinkle with flaky salt and chilli flakes, if desired.

Tips

  • More green things to add to the pizza: bell peppers, spinach, thin zucchini slices, and if you like spice, try sliced jalapeño!
  • For an even more satiating meal, serve with a fried egg on top.
  • It is easier to use mini mozzarella balls over tearing up a large one because the mini mozzarella balls release less moisture.
