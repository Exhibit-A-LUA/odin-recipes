Keto Salmon Poke Bowl with Zoodles

Description

Hawaiian poke bowls (pronounced poh-kay) are now the rage and no wonder. They're simple, fresh and so tasty with a spicy umami sauce. This low-carb version features marinated raw salmon with cucumber, red cabbage, bean sprouts, avocado, and zoodles, drizzled with a sriracha mayonnaise sauce.

Putting it together

  1. Place the salmon in a bowl with the marinade and toss together.
  2. Cover or seal, and refrigerate for at least 10 minutes.
  3. Bring a large pot of water to a boil.
  4. Place the zoodles in the boiling water for just one minute, and then drain in a colander.
  5. Divide zoodles, cucumber, bean sprouts, red cabbage, and avocado in serving bowls.
  6. Top with the salmon and drizzle with the mayo.
  7. Garnish with some sesame seeds.
