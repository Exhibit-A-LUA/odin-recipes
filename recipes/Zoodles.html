Zoodles

Description

Zoodles

Ingredients

  • 650 (g) zucchini, spiralized
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • salt and ground black pepper

Instructions

  1. Bring a large pot of water to a boil.
  2. Place the zoodles in the boiling water for just one minute, and then drain in a colander.
  3. Transfer the zoodles to a serving platter, drizzle with olive oil, and season with salt and pepper to taste.
