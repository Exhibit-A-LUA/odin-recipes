Keto Cauliflower Mash with Pancetta and Sour Cream

Description

Not your average cauliflower mash! This keto side dish is perfect for your elaborate holiday dinner or can accompany your week-night grilled chicken. It's topped with sour cream, parmesan, pancetta, and chives for an extra fancy touch.

Ingredients

  • 110 g pancetta, chopped
  • 400 g cauliflower
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 2 tbsp sour cream
  • 60 ml (20 g) shredded Parmesan cheese
  • salt or ground white pepper, to taste
  • 1 tbsp fresh chives, chopped

Instructions

  1. Cook the pancetta until crispy in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
  2. Transfer to a small bowl.
  3. While the pancetta is cooking, bring a pot of lightly salted water to a boil, over high heat.
  4. Add the cauliflower and boil for 6-8 minutes until the florets are tender, yet retain a semi-firm texture.
  5. You can test this with a fork.
  6. Strain the cauliflower in a colander and discard the water.
  7. Mash the cauliflower, butter, and sour cream together until smooth.
  8. You can also use a food processor or an immersion blender.
  9. Season with white pepper and salt to taste.
  10. Fold through the parmesan, chives, and reserved crispy pancetta.

Tips

  • You can use thick-sliced smoked ham in place of pancetta if you wish.
