Description

Escalivada Roasted Vegetables

Ingredients

  • 1 (120 g) red bell pepper
  • 1 (500 g) eggplant
  • 1 (110 g) yellow onion
  • 4 garlic cloves (optional)
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • coarse salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Wash and dry the red pepper and eggplant.
  3. Peel the onion and garlic cloves.
  4. Prick the eggplant with a fork so it can release steam as it cooks.
  5. This prevents the eggplant from splitting open.
  6. Place the vegetables on a baking sheet and drizzle with extra-virgin olive oil.
  7. Place in the oven and roast for 30 minutes.
  8. Turn the vegetables over and roast for another 30 minutes.
  9. Remove the baking sheet from the oven (but do not turn off the oven).
  10. Let the vegetables cool for about 10 minutes so you can handle them without getting burned.
  11. Remove the skin from the pepper and eggplant.
  12. Remove the outer layers of the onion.
  13. Cut the vegetables into strips.
  14. Place all the ingredients on a plate and add a pinch of coarse sea salt.
