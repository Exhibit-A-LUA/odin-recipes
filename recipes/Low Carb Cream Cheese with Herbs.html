Low Carb Cream Cheese with Herbs

Description

This low Carb cream cheese with herbs is so easy to make! Mix a non-flavoured, full-fat cream cheese with fresh herbs and spices. Voila! Bring on yumminess!

Ingredients

  • 230 (g) (240 ml) cream cheese
  • 2 tsp olive oil
  • 120 ml (8 g) fresh parsley or fresh basil, chopped
  • 1 garlic clove, minced
  • 1 tsp lemon zest
  • salt and pepper, to taste
  • 4 (160 g) celery stalks, or other fresh vegetables of your liking

Instructions

  1. Stir all ingredients into the cream cheese.
  2. Let sit in the refrigerator for at least 10 minutes to let all the flavours develop.
  3. Add salt if needed.
  4. Rinse celery stalks, cut into 2-3 inch lengths, and serve together with the soft cheese.

Tips

  • We used fresh herbs, garlic and lemon in this recipe, but feel free to experiment with your favourite spices!
  • Flavoured cream cheese works well with cucumber, bell-pepper or carrot sticks, and with low-Carb bread and sesame crispbread.
  • And why not add some to gratins or sauces?
