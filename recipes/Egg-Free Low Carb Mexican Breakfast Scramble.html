Egg-Free Low Carb Mexican Breakfast Scramble

Description

This savoury breakfast is loaded with ground turkey and plant protein, Mexican flavours, and fresh veggies. It's an egg-free and dairy-free way to start your action-packed day.

Ingredients

  • 375 g firm tofu, drained and cut into small cubes. 5" (1.5 cm)
  • 450 g ground turkey
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 3 tbsp taco seasoning
  • 1 (120 g) red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 (110 g) yellow onion, diced
  • salt and pepper
  • 1 (200 g) avocado mashed
  • 1 tbsp fresh cilantro, finely chopped
  • 4 tbsp sour cream, dairy-free or vegan
  • 1 tbsp lime juice

Instructions

  1. In a large frying pan, use half of the oil to brown the turkey meat.
  2. Season with salt and pepper.
  3. Once the meat is almost cooked add â…” of the taco seasoning.
  4. Stir well and finish cooking the meat.
  5. While the meat is cooking, heat the other half of the oil in a separate skillet and cook peppers and onions for 2 min until they begin to soften.
  6. Add the tofu and the rest of the taco seasoning.
  7. Gently cook for a few more minutes until the tofu begins to brown.
  8. Scoop the meat and the veggie tofu mixture onto a plate or bowl.
  9. Top with mashed avocado, sour cream, cilantro, and a drizzle of lime juice.

Tips

  • You can substitute the tofu for 2 eggs per serving.
