Lemon and Dill Butter

Description

In this spread, the heavenly flavours of lemon and dill work their magic on creamy butter. Enjoy with tons of dishes... from fish and poultry to meat and veggies.

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp cream cheese
  • 140 (g) butter, at room temperature
  • 3 tbsp fresh dill, finely chopped
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 1 pinch ground black pepper

Instructions

  1. Put all the ingredients in a bowl and beat with a hand mixer for 1–2 minutes until the butter cream becomes fluffy.

Tips

  • Leave the cream cheese out for a dairy-free version.
