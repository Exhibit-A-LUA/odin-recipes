Description

Marinade for Chicken and Vegetables

Ingredients

  • 80 ml olive oil
  • 1 lemon, zested and juiced
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tbsp dried oregano
  • 2 tsp chilli flakes
  • 1½ tsp salt
  • ½ tsp ground black pepper
  • ½ tsp ground cinnamon

Instructions

  1. Combine the ingredients for the marinade in a large bowl or resealable bag.
