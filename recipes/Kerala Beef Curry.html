Kerala Beef Curry

Ingredients

  • 900 (g) beef, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 2 tablespoon coconut oil
  • 3 onion, chopped
  • 3 tomatoes, diced
  • 1 green chilli, sliced
  • 8 clove garlic, minced
  • 1-inch piece ginger, minced
  • A small handful of curry leaves
  • 1 tablespoon chilli powder
  • 2 tablespoon coriander powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala (I recommend this one)
  • 1 cup water
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions

  1. Heat coconut oil in a pressure cooker (or Instant Pot) over medium heat.
  2. Add in onion and salt.
  3. Cook until soft, about 5 minutes.
  4. Add tomatoes and green chilli.
  5. Saut√© until the tomatoes become soft and juicy.
  6. Then add in garlic, ginger, and curry leaves and cook until fragrant, about 2 minutes.
  7. Add in the beef, followed by chilli powder, coriander powder, black pepper, and garam masala.
  8. Mix well to combine.
  9. Add in water.
  10. Close the lid of the pressure cooker and cook for 20 to 30 minutes (4 to 6 whistles) or until beef is thoroughly cooked through.
  11. Allow the pressure to release completely before opening the pressure cooker.
  12. Serve the Kerala beef curry with warm Basmati rice or home-made roti.
