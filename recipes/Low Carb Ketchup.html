Low Carb Ketchup

Description

Anticipation... anticipa-ay-tion. Wait no longer for a delicious home-made version of America's favourite condiment. This tangy, well-spiced version offers bright but complex flavours. So cook it thick and spread it on thick, too!

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • ½ (55 g) grated or finely chopped yellow onion
  • 1 pressed garlic clove
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 80 ml red wine vinegar
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1 tsp paprika powder
  • 1 tsp coriander seed
  • ½ tsp cloves
  • ½ tsp ground black pepper
  • 1 pinch cayenne pepper
  • 400 (g) (400 ml) canned whole tomatoes

Instructions

  1. Place everything in a small sauce pan and simmer over low heat for 5-10 minutes
  2. Add a dash of water if it's too thick.
  3. Place everything in a blender or use an immersion blender and pulse until smooth.
