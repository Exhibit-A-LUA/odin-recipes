Description

Mint Tzatziki Sauce

Ingredients

  • 85 (g) cucumber, grated
  • 1 tbsp fresh mint, finely chopped or ground black pepper, to taste
  • 2 garlic cloves, finely chopped
  • 240 ml (180 g) Greek yoghurt (4% fat)
  • salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Put the grated cucumber in a strainer and sprinkle salt on top.
  2. Mix well and let the liquid drain for 5 minutes.
  3. Wrap the cucumber in a tea towel and squeeze out excess liquid.
  4. Place the drained cucumber in a bowl with the yoghurt, garlic, and fresh mint.
  5. Stir and add black pepper and salt to taste.
  6. Let the sauce sit in the refrigerator for at least 10 minutes for the flavours to develop.

Tips

  • It will keep for about 3 days in the fridge.
