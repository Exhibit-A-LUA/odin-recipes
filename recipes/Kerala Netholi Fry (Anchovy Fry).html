Kerala Netholi Fry (Anchovy Fry)

Description

Crispy and spicy Anchovy or Kozhuva or Netholi fry. Anchovies marinated with spices and then shallow fried. Anchovy is also called nethili or chooda or podimeen in different parts of Kerala. A quite easy and tasty appetizer.

Ingredients

The Fish

  • 500g Netholi / Kozhuva/ Anchovy
  • 1 tablespoon rice flour
  • coconut oil / vegetable oil
  • curry leaves

Marinade Paste

  • 5 small shallot
  • 1/2 tsp ginger
  • 1 tsp garlic
  • 2 tbsp chilli powder
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper powder
  • 1/4 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp lime juice
  • 1/2 tsp salt

Instructions

  1. Clean the fish (netholi / kozhuva / anchovy) and pat dry using a paper kitchen towel.
  2. Grind the Marinade Paste ingredients and make it a fine paste.
  3. As needed, add just enough water to the marinade.
  4. Adjust the salt as per your taste.
  5. Marinate cleaned fish with the prepared marinade and curry leaves.
  6. Keep it aside for 30 minutes.
  7. Before frying, add rice flour. Rice flour gives a crispy touch
  8. Heat oil in a flat pan.
  9. Add the marinated fish to the pan.
  10. Deep Fry the fish over medium high flame until done and crispy. It should turn into a golden colour
  11. Serve it with onion rings and lemon. Enjoy!
