Easy Lemon Herb Aioli

Ingredients

  • 180 ml mayonnaise
  • 1 lemon, zest
  • 1 tbsp fresh parsley, chopped finely
  • 1 tbsp fresh chives, chopped finely
  • 1 garlic clove, minced
  • Â½ tsp Dijon mustard
  • salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions

  1. Combine the mayonnaise, lemon zest, parsley, chives, garlic clove, Dijon mustard, salt, and pepper in a small bowl.
  2. Cover and let it sit in the fridge for at least 15 minutes to allow the flavours to develop.
