Ginger Ribs With Creamy Red Cabbage

Description

These tender, fall-apart ribs taste of citrus and ginger, making a perfect low-Carb meal for the holidays, especially when paired with colourful creamy cabbage. In fact, we think you'll enjoy them all year-'round.

Ingredients

Ginger Orange Ribs

  • see Ginger Orange Ribs Recipe

Oven Baked Veggies

  • 2 (220 g) red onions, in wedges
  • 5 bay leaves
  • 3 tsp salt, to the ribs

Creamy Red Cabbage Salad

  • see Creamy Red Cabbage Salad Recipe

Instructions

Ginger Orange Ribs

  • see Ginger Orange Ribs Recipe

Oven Baking

  1. Pre-heat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Spread the onion wedges and bay leaves evenly in a baking dish.
  3. Remove the ribs from the marinade and season with salt on both sides.
  4. Pour the marinade into the baking dish.
  5. Place the seasoned ribs on top of the onions.
  6. Bake for 1.5-2 hours depending on the size of the meat.
  7. Let the ribs cool on a cutting board.
  8. Slice along with the rib bones.
  9. Reserve the pan drippings to use when making the cabbage salad.
