Andy's Iced Coffee

Description

Andy's Iced Coffee

Ingredients

  • 4g (2 tsp) Coffee Powder
  • 450g Milk
  • 16g (4 tsp) Brown Sugar
  • 200g Ice

Instructions

  1. In a large glass
  2. Put Coffee and Sugar into glass
  3. Add a small amount of hot water
  4. Mix thoroughly
  5. Slowly add ice cubes into coffee mix while stirring
  6. Fill to about half the glass
  7. Add the milk while stirring
