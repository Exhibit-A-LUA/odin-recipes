Tzatziki with Lemon

Ingredients

  • 110 (g) cucumber, grated
  • 240 ml (180 g) Greek yogurt (4% fat)
  • 1 garlic clove, crushed
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tsp lemon zest
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp fresh dill, chopped
  • salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions

  1. Put the cucumber into a bowl.
  2. Add the yogurt, garlic, lemon juice, lemon zest, olive oil, and dill.
  3. Add salt and pepper to taste.
  4. itMix until combined, then set to the side.em

Tips

  • Refrigerate the tzatziki in a separate airtight container for up to four days.
  • If you want thicker tzatziki, you can squeeze out the liquid in the grated cucumber before mixing it with the other ingredients.
