Barbecue Seasoning

Ingredients

  • 3 tbsp ground black pepper
  • 3 tbsp paprika powder
  • 1 tbsp garlic powder
  • 1 tbsp dried thyme
  • 1 tbsp ground cumin
  • ½ tbsp cayenne pepper
  • ½ tbsp ground nutmeg

Instructions

  1. Mix the spices carefully and pour into a small jar.
  2. Spices are sensitive to both light and heat, so store the jar in a cool and dark place.

Tips

  • You get more aroma if you use whole, dried spices—seeds and leaves—and grind right before use.
  • Try this with the pepper, cumin, nutmeg, and thyme.
  • Organic spices and herbs usually offer the best flavour... unless you grow your own!
