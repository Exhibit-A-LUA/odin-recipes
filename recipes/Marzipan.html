Marzipan

Description

Audrey's Marzipan

Ingredients

  • 227g Caster Sugar
  • 454g Ground Almonds
  • 2 Egg Yolk
  • 1 Egg White
  • 1 desert spoon of Tia Maria

Instructions

  1. Mix ingredients to a firm paste.
  2. Roll out into sheets to become layers ('little finger' thickness)
