Greek Tuna Salad

Description

This beautiful Greek tuna salad has it all! Filling protein, colourful veggies, and healthy fats for flavour. It's a 10-minute meal that's perfect for a quick lunch or a week-night dinner.

Ingredients

  • 280 g tuna in water, drained
  • 140 g (220 ml) feta cheese
  • ¼ (28 g) red onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tsp dried oregano
  • salt and ground black pepper
  • 2 (230 g) ripe tomatoes, quartered and sliced
  • 140 g cucumber, diced
  • ½ (70 g) green bell pepper, sliced
  • 10 olives (preferably Kalamata)
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp red wine vinegar
  • ¼ lemon, cut into wedges

Instructions

  1. Crumble the feta cheese into a bowl and mix with tuna, onion, and oregano.
  2. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  3. Cut the tomatoes, cucumber, and bell pepper.
  4. Add to a big bowl.
  5. Add the olive oil, vinegar, and olives and give it a mix.
  6. Arrange the salad on a serving platter or individual plates.
  7. Top with the tuna/feta mix and garnish with more oregano, freshly ground black pepper, and lemon wedges.
