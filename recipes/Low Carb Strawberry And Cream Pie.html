Putting it Together

  1. As soon as the pastry has almost cooled to room temperature, transfer it to a serving plate.
  2. You can use the base of a loose-bottomed cake pan to transfer it from tray to plate.
  3. Pour the strawberry mixture over the pastry and top with the whipped cream.
  4. Serve straight away or chill for up to a day in the fridge.
