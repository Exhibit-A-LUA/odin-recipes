Pumpkin Filling

Description

Pumpkin Filling for pie.

Ingredients

  • 425 g pumpkin puree, canned
  • 120 ml (110 g) erythritol
  • 28 g (30 ml) unsalted butter melted
  • 1 tsp ground cinnamon
  • ½ tsp pumpkin pie spice
  • ¼ tsp salt
  • 2 large eggs
  • 180 ml heavy whipping cream

Instructions

  1. In a medium-sized bowl, use a hand mixer to combine the pumpkin purée, sweetener, butter, cinnamon, pumpkin pie spice, and salt.
  2. Pour in the egg and the heavy whipping cream, and mix until just combined.
  3. Pour the filling into the pie crust.
  4. Place aluminium foil or a pie shield along the crust edges, to prevent browning too quickly.
  5. Bake on the middle oven rack for 35-40 minutes, or until the filling is just set.
  6. Cool on a wire rack for 2 hours, or until at room temperature.
  7. Serve at room temperature or refrigerate for several hours before serving.
