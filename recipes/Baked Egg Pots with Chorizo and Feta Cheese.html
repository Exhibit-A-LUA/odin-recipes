Baked Egg Pots with Chorizo and Feta Cheese

Description

These breakfast egg pots with spinach, chorizo, and feta, are perfect for a filling and nutritious breakfast. Or enjoy one as a light meal.

Ingredients

  • 55 (g) (425 ml) fresh spinach
  • 1 tsp olive oil
  • 2 large eggs
  • 55 (g) chorizo (see tip)
  • 60 ml (38 g) feta cheese, crumbled
  • 55 (g) cherry tomatoes, halved
  • salt and ground black pepper

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 425°F (220°C).
  2. Place spinach in oven-proof, 12 oz (360 ml) ramekins, or mugs (two per serving), and drizzle with olive oil.
  3. Crack eggs into the ramekins.
  4. Top with the chorizo, feta cheese, and tomatoes.
  5. Place the ramekins on the middle oven rack, and bake uncovered for 10 to 12 minutes, until the egg whites are set, and the yolks are still runny.
  6. Season with salt and pepper, to taste.

Tips

  • If using "Spanish" chorizo (hard and cured), dice, or cut it into small cubes.
  • If using fully cooked chorizo sausage links, simply crumble the sausage after removing it from the casing.
  • No time to cook? Use a microwave and cook on high for 1 to 2 minutes until the whites are set, and the yolks are runny.
  • Season with salt and pepper, to taste.
