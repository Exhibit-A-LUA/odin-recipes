Keto Avocado Hummus

Description

Whip and dip! Blend together this tasty keto take on hummus and you will find your new to-go dip for veggies. Creamy, with a bright citrus edge, and ready in minutes!

Ingredients

  • 3 (600 g) ripe avocados
  • 120 ml (8 g) fresh cilantro
  • 120 ml olive oil
  • 60 ml (35 g) sunflower seeds
  • 60 ml tahini (sesame paste)
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 garlic clove, pressed
  • ½ tsp ground cumin
  • ½ tsp salt
  • ¼ tsp ground black pepper

Instructions

  1. Cut the avocado lengthwise, remove the pit and spoon out the flesh.
  2. Put all ingredients in a blender or food processor and mix until thoroughly smooth.
  3. Add more oil, lemon juice or water if you want to have a looser texture.
  4. Adjust seasoning as needed.

Tips

  • This sauce is boss, so feel free to use it as a spread, too!
  • It's a perfect addition to your low-Carb sandwich or wrap.
