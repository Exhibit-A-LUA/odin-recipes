Spice Paste

Ingredients

  • 28 (g) (70 ml) fresh ginger, peeled
  • 2 garlic cloves, peeled
  • 2 red chilli peppers
  • 1 lemongrass stalk, outer layer removed and roughly chopped
  • 1 tsp turmeric, dried
  • 2 tsp salt
  • 80 ml water

Instructions

  1. In a small food processor, blend the ginger, garlic, chillies, lemongrass, turmeric, salt, and water into a smooth paste.
  2. Set aside.
