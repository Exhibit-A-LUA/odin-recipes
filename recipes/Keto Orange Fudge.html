Keto Orange Fudge

Description

Keto orange fudge is just like your favourite fudge, with all the flavour, texture, and richness — without the sugar. The fresh orange zest gives the rich dark chocolate a burst of brightness. Make a batch for yourself, or wrap them up as a healthy gift for a special occasion.

Ingredients

  • 85 (g) (150 ml) sugar-free dark chocolate, chopped into small pieces
  • ½ orange, the zest
  • 240 ml heavy whipping cream
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 55 (g) butter, room temperature

Instructions

  1. Line a mini-muffin tray with mini-muffin liners. Set aside.
  2. Add chocolate and orange zest into a medium mixing bowl. Set aside.
  3. Pour cream and vanilla extract into a heavy-based saucepan, bring to boil
  4. Reduce heat and simmer until the cream reduces by half and starts to thicken, about 10 minutes.
  5. Add butter; mix until completely melted and smooth. Remove from heat.
  6. Pour the hot cream mixture over the chocolate pieces and orange zest.
  7. Stir well until all chocolate pieces are melted, and the mixture is smooth.
  8. Fill each muffin liner with the mixture.
  9. Refrigerate for at least 2 hours.

Tips

  • Add some more orange zest and sprinkle some sea salt on top for a more elegant look and a boost of flavour.
  • Store fudge in an airtight container in the fridge for up to 4 days or in the freezer for up to 4 weeks.
