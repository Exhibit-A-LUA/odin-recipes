Cottage Cheese and Strawberry Jam

Ingredients

  • 70 (g) frozen strawberries
  • 2 tsp erythritol (optional)
  • 80 ml (70 g) cottage cheese
  • Â½ tsp vanilla extract or sugar-free vanilla powder (optional)

Instructions

  1. Add the strawberries to a bowl and microwave if frozen.
  2. Mash with a fork and add erythritol (if using).
  3. Mix the cottage cheese with vanilla extract (if using)

Tips

  • If you can't get perfectly ripe strawberries from the market, frozen berries work too.
  • You can replace the strawberries with your favourite berries such as raspberries, blackberries, or blueberries.
  • If using fresh berries you don't need to microwave them before mashing.
