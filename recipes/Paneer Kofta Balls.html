Paneer Kofta Balls

Description

Paneer Kofta Balls

Ingredients

  • 100 gms paneer/cottage cheese, grated
  • 2 medium size potatoes, boiled, peeled and grated
  • 1/4 tsp red chilli powder
  • 1 tbsp cornflour
  • 1/4 tsp garam masala or a pinch
  • 3/4 tbsp almond flour or 1/2 tbsp milk powder or 3/4 tbsp khoya/evaporated milk
  • salt as required

Instructions

  1. Mix all the ingredients in a bowl.
  2. Make medium sized balls and fry the koftas in medium hot oil till golden.
