Blue Cheese Dressing

Description

Blue Cheese Dressing

Ingredients

  • 80 ml mayonnaise
  • 60 ml sour cream
  • 2 tbsp heavy whipping cream
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • ¼ tsp garlic powder
  • ¼ tsp salt
  • 55 (g) blue cheese, crumbled

Instructions

  1. Whisk together the ingredients for the dressing, except for the cheese.
  2. Once it is well blended, add in blue cheese crumbles and mix well.
  3. Thin with a little bit of water if you prefer.
  4. Chill until serving.
