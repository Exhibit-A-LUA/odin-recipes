Malai Kofta

Description

Potato and paneer koftas in a creamy, rich, mild and lightly sweet gravy.

Ingredients

Paneer Kofta Balls

  • see Paneer Kofta Balls Recipe

Garam Masala

  • see Garam Masala Recipe

Kofta Gravy (Curry)

  • see Kofta Gravy (Curry) Recipe

Garnish

  • 1 tbsp grated paneer
  • 1 or 2 tbsp cream
  • a few chopped coriander leaves

Instructions

  1. Add the koftas in the curry if serving immediately or you can also add the curry first in the serving plate or bowl and then place the koftas.
  2. Garnish the malai kofta with some cream, grated paneer and coriander leaves.
  3. Serve hot with rotis, naan, steamed basmati rice or jeera rice.
