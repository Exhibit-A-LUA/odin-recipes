Description

Creamed Spinach

Ingredients

  • ½ tbsp butter
  • ½ (55 g) yellow onion, finely chopped
  • 110 (g) (850 ml) fresh spinach, rinsed, trimmed, and dried
  • 28 (g) (30 ml) cream cheese
  • 2 tbsp heavy whipping cream
  • ¼ tsp garlic powder
  • 2 tbsp shredded Parmesan cheese

Instructions

  1. Melt the butter in a large skillet over medium heat.
  2. Add the onion and sauté until tender, about 3 minutes.
  3. Add the spinach to the skillet.
  4. As it cooks down, add the cream cheese, heavy cream, and garlic powder.
  5. Stir until the cream cheese has melted and the mixture is smooth and creamy.
  6. Remove from heat and sprinkle with freshly grated parmesan just before serving.
