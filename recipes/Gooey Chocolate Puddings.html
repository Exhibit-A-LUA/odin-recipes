Gooey Chocolate Puddings

Description

Gooey Chocolate Puddings for 6

Ingredients

  • 200 (g) dark chocolate (or part and part with milk chocolate)
  • 150 (g) butter, plus some for lining the dishes
  • Pinch sea salt
  • 3 whole eggs and 3 yolks
  • 50 (g) caster sugar
  • 20 (g) plain flour
  • Cocoa powder for dusting

Instructions

  1. Line the ramekins with butter and sprinkle cocoa into each so it all sticks to the butter.
  2. Melt 150g of the chocolate with the butter (in a glass bowl over a pan of bubbling water) and add a pinch of sea salt.
  3. While that’s melting whisk the eggs, yolks and sugar until they are pale and creamy.
  4. When the chocolate is done take it off the heat for a minute.
  5. In that minute grate a few chunks of chocolate (leaving six squares behind) into the egg/sugar mix then add the melted chocolate and butter and stir through gently.
  6. Next add the flour and stir into a pudding mix.
  7. Pour into the ramekins and pop a square of chocolate into the middle of each one.
  8. Chill until you need it.
  9. To cook the puddings the oven must be preheated to 220C – exactly 220C.
  10. Add the ramekins and watch the clock very closely.
  11. As soon as ten minutes are up take them out, run a knife around the edge and turn them out onto a plate.
