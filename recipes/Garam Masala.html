Garam Masala

Ingredients

  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1 inch cinnamon
  • 1 or 2 black cardamom
  • 2 to 3 green cardamom
  • a pinch of mace
  • 2 to 3 cloves

Instructions

  1. Mix all the ingredients well
