Low Carb Christmas Pâté With Cranberry Pickled Red Onions

Description

Pass the pâté please! Liver, ground beef, and bacon are seasoned with rosemary and juniper berries to make this rustic country pâté. Pickled cranberries and red onions provide a pop of colour and flavour to this festive dish.
