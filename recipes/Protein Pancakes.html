Protein Pancakes

Description

These gluten-free breakfast pancakes are high in protein, thanks to cottage cheese.

Ingredients

  • 4 large eggs
  • 230 g (260 ml) cottage cheese
  • Â½ tbsp ground psyllium husk powder
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 2 tbsp butter, for frying

Instructions

  1. Add eggs, cottage cheese, psyllium husk, and salt to a medium-sized bowl and whisk until combined.
  2. Let the batter sit for a few minutes until it has thickened up a bit.
  3. Heat the butter in a non-stick frying pan over medium-high heat.
  4. When the pan is hot, use a 1/4 cup (0.5 dl) measuring cup, or ladle, to pour batter into the frying pan.
  5. Fry the pancakes for about 3-4 minutes or until the tops start to set.
  6. Flip and fry for another minute or two.
  7. The smaller you make them, the easier they will be to flip.
