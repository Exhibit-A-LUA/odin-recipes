Turkey Burger

Description

Turkey Burger

Ingredients

  • 450 (g) ground turkey or ground chicken
  • 1 tbsp mayonnaise or vegan mayonnaise
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • ¾ tsp salt
  • ½ tsp ground black pepper
  • 1 tbsp butter or olive oil

Instructions

  1. Combine the ground turkey with mayonnaise, garlic powder, salt, and black pepper.
  2. Divide the meat mixture into balls — roughly 4 oz (110 g) each.
  3. Flatten and shape them into burgers with wet hands.
  4. The thinner they are, the faster they will cook.
  5. Heat a large frying pan over medium-high heat.
  6. Add the butter.
  7. When the pan is hot, place the burgers in the pan.
  8. Fry for about 5 minutes on each side until cooked through, and have a nice brown crust.
  9. There are two burgers per serving.

Tips

  • When the chicken is almost cooked, add some heavy cream and more seasoning to the skillet to make a quick and delicious sauce.
  • You can use fresh garlic instead of garlic powder for more of a kick. Use as much as you like.
