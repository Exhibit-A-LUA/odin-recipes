Tandoori Seasoning

Description

Tandoori Seasoning - Makes 2 tablespoons plus 1 teaspoon

Ingredients

  • 1 tsp ground ginger
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 1 tsp ground coriander
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp cayenne pepper

Instructions

  1. Whisk all ingredients into small bowl to blend.

Tips

  • Spice blend can be made 2 weeks ahead.
  • Store airtight at room temperature.
