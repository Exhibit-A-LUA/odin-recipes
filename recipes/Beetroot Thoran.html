Beetroot Thoran

Description

Beetroot Thoran

Ingredients

  • 500 g Beetroot, very finely chopped or grated
  • 1 cup Fresh coconut, grated
  • 1 tsp Mustard seeds (Rai/ Kadugu)
  • 2 Dry Red Chillies, broken
  • 2 Green Chillies, slit
  • 1 tbsp White Urad Dal (Split)
  • 1 sprig Curry leaves
  • Salt as required
  • 1 tsp Red Chilli powder
  • 1 tsp Turmeric powder (Haldi)
  • 1-1/2 tsp Coriander Powder (Dhania)
  • Coconut Oil as required
  • 1 cup Water

Instructions

  1. In a non stick wok, add 2 tablespoons coconut oil.
  2. Saute the finely chopped beetroot for a while.
  3. Add 1 cup water or more and cook the beetroot till soft with lid closed.
  4. Turn off the heat and drain the water.
  5. You can collect the water and later use it in making some curry or stew.
  6. In the same wok heat 2 tbsp coconut oil and add mustard seeds, urad dal, curry leaves and dry red chilli.
  7. Saut√© them till mustard crackles.
  8. Now add the cooked beetroot and fry for some time till the moisture starts absorbing.
  9. Add salt, red chilli powder, turmeric, coriander powder and stir nicely.
  10. Adjust oil if required and close the lid to cook for another 5 to 6 minutes.
  11. Meanwhile add coconut and green chillies to a mixer and grind coarsely.
  12. Once the beetroot thoran is done, turn off the gas and garnish with the freshly ground coconut and chilli.
