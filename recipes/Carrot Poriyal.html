Carrot Poriyal

Description

Carrot Poriyal

Ingredients

  • 1 tsp moong daal
  • 1 cup freshly chopped carrot
  • ½ tsp mustard seeds
  • ½ tsp urad daal
  • 1 pinch asafoetida or hing
  • 1 dry red chilli
  • 5 – 6 sprigs of curry leaves
  • 1 pinch turmeric powder
  • 3 tsp freshly grated coconut
  • 2 – 3 green chillies
  • ½ tsp cumin seeds
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Soak moong dal in water for 10 minutes, drain and keep it aside.
  2. Put grated coconut, green chillies, cumin seeds and 1 tsp water in a grinder.
  3. Heat oil in a pan and put mustard seeds and hear them sizzle.
  4. Add the urad daal next.
  5. Add curry leaves, dry red chilly and curry leaves next.
  6. Next, pour the rinsed moong daal and add turmeric and finely chopped carrots and stir.
  7. Add 1/3 cup of water and salt to taste and let it cook.
  8. Add the ground coconut paste next, mix well and cook on a low flame for a minute.
  9. Serve hot with a side of ragi dosa.
