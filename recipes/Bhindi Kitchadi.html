Bhindi Kitchadi

Description

Bhindi Kitchadi - Serves 8

Ingredients

  • Bhindi around 12
  • 2 green chillis
  • 1 cup curd (beaten)
  • ¼ to ½ cup grated coconut
  • 10 curry leaves
  • 2 big red chillis
  • Jeera
  • Mustard Seeds

Prep

  • Wash Bhindi, then towel dry, put under fan for 1 hour
  • Cut the Bhindis into medium cube sized slices
  • Grate the coconut

Tempering

  • see Tempering Recipe

Instructions

  1. Deep fry the bhindi
  2. Mixi the coconut, green chillis, some jeera, and a little water
  3. Add mustard seed and quick mixi a little
  4. Stir fry the coconut mixture a little until not raw
  5. Let it cool down
  6. Add curd and a little salt, mix
  7. Add bhindi
  8. Make the Tempering
  9. Pour onto the mixture
