Sirloin Steak With Butter-Fried Green Beans And Almonds

Description

Five ingredients plus ten minutes equals one delicious steak dinner. With 44 grams of protein and only 4 net carbs, your body and your taste buds will thank you for treating them right.

Ingredients

  • Â½ tbsp butter or ghee
  • 200 (g) sirloin steaks, room temperature
  • 55 (g) fresh green beans
  • 2 tbsp sliced almonds
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Heat a frying pan with butter or ghee over high heat.
  2. Season the steak generously with salt and pepper on both sides.
  3. Place it in the hot pan.
  4. Fry the steak for a couple of minutes per side until preferred done-ness is reached.
  5. Remove the steak from the pan and cover it with a plate or aluminium foil to rest.
  6. While the steak is resting, add the beans and almonds to the frying pan and fry until the almonds get some colour Season with salt and pepper.
  7. Serve the steak together with the green beans and almonds.
